Vulcan Software Development Team News

NEWS


Deimos Design, the development team responsible for the Amiga PPC CDRom title 'Maim & Mangle' have come to the painstaking decision to cease all development on the Amiga platform and move to the PC-CDRom platform.

Since early 97, Deimos Design has existed with one goal in mind, to support the Amiga with high specification PPC products. Throughout development of 'Maim & Mangle' many things have affected the fluidity, but the overwhelming factor that prompted the change of platform was the economic realities of the Amigas constantly eroding user base.

Vulcan Software are also sad to see the disappearance of what promised to be an awesome Amiga title but fully understand and support their decision.
