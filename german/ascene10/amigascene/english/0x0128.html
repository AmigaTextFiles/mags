YAM 2.0 FINAL Version

 
STOP PRESS! While our last internal meeting was held, the final version of this widespread and beloved mailreader called YAM (Y-et A-nother M-ailer) from Marcel Beck was ready. This was great news - many of us went into raptures, as we've been waiting seemingly far too long for the update of this masterpiece of Amiga software...

 
A detailed review is underway and will be available soon from http://www.amigascene.com!

Curious? Here's a half-sized screenshot, what I especially appreciate among the features of the new YAM is the iconset - no more b/w/grey or blue/apricot MagicWB styled icons, unfortunately I forgot the name of the iconset...
