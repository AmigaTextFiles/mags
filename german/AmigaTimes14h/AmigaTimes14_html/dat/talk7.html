2. TWF Opinion Polls

The current poll we've been running asked the question:

With quite a number of ports of popular PC games currently being ported to the Amiga platform, what would be your prefered option for TWF to do ?

Just two choices this time, Game Ports or Originals.

The current scores are:

- Game Ports 44
- Original Games 136

So a resounding vote in favour of original games development. We have no plans at present to port other games anyway, we were just curious to know what people think on the subject. :)

Another poll will be put on as soon as we can think of a new one.
