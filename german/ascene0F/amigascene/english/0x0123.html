Yeah, at last we reached our aim - the AmigaScene has gone multilingual. Sorry for the delay, but we only had two native speakers available for translating our reviews. Some of them have been translated by ourselves, so please forgive us one or another error (semantics, spelling, grammar, etc.). We'll try to translate the whole mag by native speakers next month and hope you'll nevertheless enjoy this issue!

Have fun, and spread this URL to everyone:

                       http://www.amigascene.com

Finally, let me tell you one thing: We always appreciate getting articles in English, so we do not only translate from German into English, but also the other way round. Feel free to send in some reviews written in English, we're goin' to include it ASAP.

Especially we lack these sub-categories:

=> Games (also previews)
=> Demoscene section (events or demo reviews, stories)
=> Hardware (also hacks)

We accept:

=> text: ASCII preferred over HTML
=> additional goodies/screenshots: less than 500K (packed)

Send this to one of these nice guys:

=> Oliver Gerler <Oliver Gerler>
=> Bernhard Lukas <Bernhard Lukas>
=> Robert Niessner <Robert Niessner>

And if it's a rather big archive (>200K), ask us whether this hasn't been tested before, because we're trying to avoid duplicates. If the addresses above don't work, check out our staff members page.
