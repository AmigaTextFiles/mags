Amiga Times 03/98 -Fun & Talk

ANNEX Interview

Nearly every Amiga user has already heard of the Amiga hymn CD, and it has been accepted with enthusiasm. We have prepared an interview with the group ANNEX for this issue. Have fun with it!

AT: First, I want to congratulate you to your great CD!
Daniel: Thanks a lot. And we thank the Amiga people for their support. Every day we get a dozen of eMails from all over the world, that gives us a lot of courage.
AT: Since when does ANNEX exist?
Daniel: Georgette, Sandra and Neal have already been on stage four years ago with a self produced dance performance. Two years ago the contact to Ricardo Pérez was established. When the cooperation between AMIGA and PÉREZ MUSIC appeared in outlines, Neal got the two befriended female dancers Ornella and Mimi for the team. With that, ANNEX was born.
AT: How did you get this idea ?
Daniel: I am an enthusiastic AMIGA-Freak since many years, who had the idea to compose a song for the international fan community. First of all, I wanted to create an international musical idenification sign for the fans, on the other hand, I also wanted to call people's attention to the AMIGA, for so announcing the big AMIGA comeback in public. Also the text of the song is orientated to that.
AT: How long did the production take ?
Daniel: I already began with the song in mid 1997. All together we have filed nearly eight months on the hymn.
AT: Which systemconfiguration was used ?
Daniel: I used an A1200/060/50 MHz. You don't have to own a highly extended Amiga to make music with it. If anyone is interested in my exact configuration, he can email me.
AT: Which program did you use ?
Daniel: I used OctaMED Soundstudio Pro. It is simply brilliant.
AT: Why did you produce this CD with the Amiga ?
Daniel: The answer ist simple: I just do not own a peecee or Atari, and I also will never buy myself such thing.
AT: What do you think of the Amiga in general respectively what is your attitude ?
Daniel: One should think, that there ist no better computer, but unfortunately software companies cross our plans again and again. Alone these applications that run with real good performance on Fusion or Shapeshifter should be truly a convincing argument for further software development. Besides that, unfortunately quite nobody does professional public advertisement for the Amiga, and all these companies that do develop Amiga software, underestimate their software by lengh. Maybe the theme CD and the song "Back for the future" will arise corresponding attention in the media and so cause free Amiga adversising. It is now time for massive advertisement ... however, the Amiga is for me an unreplaceable "friend", that I never would exchange for anything in the whole world.
AT: How has your idea been accepted by the Amiga users ?
Daniel: Fantastically, before the acception by AI-president Petro Tyschtschenko, some international user clubs agreed to the next official song. So is the today's mostly positive resonance of the Amigians, that ist demonstrates to us every day by a lot, lot of eMail from all over the world.
AT: Will you produce something different for the Amiga in future ?
Daniel: We'll see, what future brings; but we will try everything to get more and more atention for the Amiga and to push the computer system. If we'll have success with "Back for the future", we would like to give to other AMIGA musicians the chance to release their songs. Maybe on some kind of AMIGA compilation. The first demo tapes from users have already been sent to us.
AT: How will it be commercialized - also in cd shops or just in the amiga market ?
Daniel: Besides the distribution of the fan release cd via AMIGA International, "Back for the future" will be available in shops soon. We are now in dialogue with different big marketing organizations. But in spite of that we urgent need air plays to give more weight to the song (and therefore to Amiga) in public. We hope to get the support of the amigians.
AT: Will your song also be hearable/viewable in radio or television ?
Daniel: Yes, that's what it looks like now. A lot of amigians asked us to coordinate an eMail campaign to the most important radio stations. They are convinced to be able to express their power as user by this way (, also without the support of AI). So it is not just our aim to sell the CD possibly more often, but to make it largely and internationally known.
The realisation of this idea may will be made by the FunTime magazin, that wants to organize the eMail-campaign for germany honorary. May other magazines will join with this magazine to cause an AMIGA advertisement campaign of the superlative, made by fans. News can always be found on our ANNEX Homepage at:
http://home.t-online.de/home/ripera/nx.htm

There is in case of appropritate receipts from the CD selling also a video planed. But a clip can easily cost DM 30.000, so we ja need those appropriate receipts. But if someone already should have good contacts for live-performances of ANNEX (i.e. concerts, television), please write to us.We are thankful for any support.

AT: What do you think should happen to the amiga ?
Daniel: A lot of more professional advertising will be te most important in future to inspire new users for the Amiga. And then user groups and freaks should continue to work on good software (i.e. conversions of games). They are the true engine and the real power center of our "Amiga movement".
AT: What would you want to say to the Amiga users ?
Daniel: Help us to spread the song around the whole world. It is a trojanian "media" horse to make the AMIGA again be talked about. At this time Amiga Inc. itself has evidently not enough means to start huge ad campaigns. So now it lies in the hands of the fans to make the Amiga heard in public, in the full meaning of the words.
For questions please directly ask us by mail: perez@gmx.de
AT: We want to thank you for this interview and wish you all the best for the future !
Daniel: Thank you very much. We wish also all the best for your magazine. SUPPORT AMIGA!
