 The StarMag is a German DiscMag consisting of articles  of  its  nice
 readers.  It only contains AMIGA-topics. In English: [= Auf Deutsch:]
 News, "tips", tricks, tests, help with problems, free adverts as well
 as  PD  and scanning for lowest possible costs and much more (but not
 MuchMore ;-))

 The StarMag (this is the 7th issue) appears  every  second  month  on
 disc or on the Aminet. It`s a mag for all Amigas (and Amigans).

 You can unpack the mag on your harddisk (into an extra directory)  or
 on a formatted disc - it MUST fit! If it doesn`t work, try to use the
 installer and put it in a new directory or on a new  formatted  disc.
 If it still doesn`t work, worry ;-( Or contact me:
