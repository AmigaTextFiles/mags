» 02.10.1998   PerSuaSiVe SoftWorX Announces Introductory Freebie Offer


   Starting with 5 October 1998, PerSuaSiVe SoftWorX offers a special
   introductory freebie offer for their new tool "JPEG-Box" - limited
   until 31 December 1998.

   JPEG-Box completes the current set of "WWW datatypes" (akJFIF and akPNG)
   and WWW image creation programs (PNG-Box) by adding the missing
   complementary tool for akJFIF datatype.

   With JPEG-Box you can easily convert any source graphics as supported
   by datatypes or SuperView-Library into progressive or non-progressive
   JPEG (JFIF) graphic files for use on the WWW or otherwise. Of course
   with PPC support for faster image saving (in the registered version).

   Anyone who registers

      - anything from us via RegNet (see "RegNet-Offers" listing)
      - any of the available "discount" Shareware bundles from us
        (see "Discount" offers listing) for more than 25 DM

   until 31 December 1998, additionally will receive a free keyfile
   for JPEG-Box as well.

   Of course JPEG-Box can be registered solely as well, using the
   included registration form.
