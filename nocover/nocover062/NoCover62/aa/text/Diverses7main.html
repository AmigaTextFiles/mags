Pixel64 ZorroIII

Atéo Concepts is designing a new version of the Pixel64 named Pixel64 ZIII.
This new version of the Pixel64 will plug on a ZIII slot of the A3000 or
A4000 (Sorry, no ZII support...)


3D features for the Pixel64!!!

Atéo Concepts is working on a 3D expansion for the Pixel64. This expansion
will be based on the Voodoo chip (version 2 or 3). The 3D expansion will
be available for all the Pixel64 version. The release date is not yet
available.«
