The return of the great adventure !

PHOENIX is a new space combat adventure which brings You back the original joy of dogfights and wingmen interaction in the cold darkness of space.

PHOENIX is based on a strong storyline full of intrigues, terror and...friendship. You are 2nd Lieutenant Alan Best, a young graduate from the TNA (Terran Navy Academy), who has just earned his wings and callsign Phoenix and is experiencing his first tour of duty on the old outdated carrier Montego Bay, where You serve as a fighter pilot in the Dancing Devils squadron.

The Bay is rather an educational installation instead of a war ship, and that is why she is assigned to a a far away station for exercising purposes.

There, far away from the next military outpost, the young crew and captain of the Montego Bay will have to fight for our fate against an enemy who seems to be invincible....can You turn a sure loss into a victory ?

*Rich, immersive storyline with over 25 missions and branches
*action-packed dogfights with Your wingmen against multiple enemies *state-of-the-art 3-d-engine with breathtaking realtime effects such as lensflares,fogs,shockwaves,engine glows, particles, explosions, transparencies, motion blur..
*virtual cockpit
*a variety of opponents: slow flying drones and giant motherships
*enemy AI, the computer acts like a human: unpredictable and dangerous
*in-cockpit-animations and digitized speech of Your wingmen talking
*on-board data engine: any new enemy You encounter will be registered, You can check the facts, talk to Your crewmates and draw Your own conclusions
*on-line-guide and help
*powerful, atmospheric soundtrack
*fully animated CG-sequences tell the story further
