NoCover

NoCover, the Club-Diskmagazine edition for the Amiga. Includes postage within Germany (2 Disks!!).

Hot tests, workshops, discussions and much more are presented in this disk mag . This is not one of the usual "Scene-Mags". No testing of pirated software takes place here, only originals. And PD-Soft is not neglected either. On the contrary ! You won't find as much information about PD-Soft in any other magazine. Even commercial magazines can't match our up-to-date reports. And how could they ? After all we are one of the first to get our hands on PD software. NoCover also deals regularly with DFÜ items.

Here anyone can air their frustrations, which they might have accumalated in dealing with some shop or dealer. Everyone can write in ! Simply send your text on disk to NoCover or upload into our mailbox ! And it does not matter, what topic you choose, as long as it is presentable. So, what do you say ? Have you sent anything off yet ? Get your fingers cracking !

NoCover is published regularly on every 1st of the month. The NoCover mag requires at least 1 MB ChipRam und Amiga-OS 2.x. Recommended are Amiga-OS 3.x, 2 MB RAM as well as a hard disk.
