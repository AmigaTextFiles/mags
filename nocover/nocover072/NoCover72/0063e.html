Amiga Times CD-ROM

Amiga Times - the entertainment magazine

After lenghty deliberations we have decided to publish a CD-Rom. With this, our first CD, we are starting a trial, which, if successful, may lead to further projects of this kind. To make this CD not only interesting for our confirmed Amiga Times readers we spared no effort to present a first rate product to you all. Thanks to the cooperation of many firms and individual programmers this was deemed a success and we can present you with a full version with numerous Modules, graphics, tools, text documents, demos, shareware, programs and games, as well as all published Amiga Times in html and guide format (inclusive 2 director's cut versions) - certainly an interesting proposition for fans of magazines. This will complete your collection of our publicized mags for the time being.
The Amiga Times CD totals a file size of more than 620 MB.
