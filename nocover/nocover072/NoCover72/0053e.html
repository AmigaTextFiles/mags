Forest Dumb Forever


It has been a lot of years ago, since I`ve been in a forest full of interesting secrets. So it`s time for me to make a journey into the wonderful land. Join my way through the forests and take care, so we can get home safe. The game features great, colorful graphics and 6 big levels with many different opponents.

Requires A500, A500+, A600, A1200 with at least 1 MB
