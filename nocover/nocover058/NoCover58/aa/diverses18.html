November 5th, 1998 (Hamilton, ON)- Asimware Innovations Inc. is proud to 
announce today's release of MasterISO v2.2. This is a maintenance update 
for the MasterISO v2 package.

In this release, we have added support for the following drives:

 * Caravelle CD-R820EX CD-R drive
 * Hi-Val 4x8 IDE CD-R drive
 * HP 8100 CD-RW drive
 * HP 8110 CD-RW drive
 * Mitsumi 4801TE CD-R drive
 * Smart and Friendly CD-R8020 CD-R drive
 * Sony CRX-100E CD-RW drive
 * That's Write FastX 820S CD-R drive
 * Yamaha 2216S CD-RW drive
 * Yamaha 2216E CD-RW drive
 * Yamaha 4416S CD-RW drive
 * Yamaha 4416E CD-RW drive
 * Yamaha 4261 CD-RW drive

We have also added support for Disc-At-Once writing for the following 
drives:

 * HP 7100 series of CD-RW drives
 * Memorex CDRW-1622 CD-RW drive
 * Philips CDD3610 CD-RW drive

Registered users may contact us directly or may obtain this release via our 
web site.

We believe that MasterISO represents the future of CD-R/RW mastering 
software for the Amiga platform. Don't take our word for it - download our 
demo version and give it a try today.
