 FPU Move Commands 

fmr   Fd,Fa
fneg  Fd,Fa
fabs  Fd,Fa
fnabs Fd,Fa

fmr copies Fa to Fd, fneg copies -Fa to Fd, fabs copies abs(Fa) to Fd, and
fnabs copies -abs(Fa) to Fd.

All these commands support the . Notation, like described in
Important things, but not the o Notation.
