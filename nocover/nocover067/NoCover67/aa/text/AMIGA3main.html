»Engineering and Technology Jobs at Amiga

Please choose: San Jose, San Diego


General

Amiga is a highly entrepreneurial start-up company. We are headquartered in
San Diego, and have development and technology groups in both San Diego (La
Jolla) and San Jose (near the airport). The following positions are based
in one of these two locations.

At Amiga we are designing next generation Internet-ready, consumer-oriented
digital appliances. The Amiga name is associated the world over with
user-friendly, low-cost, powerful computing. We are taking this philosophy
into the next generation, enabling products from hand-held Internet
appliances to high-end graphics computers that help the user rather than
frustrate them. See www.amiga.com for more details on Amiga, our history
and the community.

We are looking for senior engineering candidates who have a proven industry
track record of developing and delivering robust, top-quality software and
hardware on time. At Amiga we believe strongly in both teamwork and
individual contribution, and all staff members are expected to drive
his/her commitments to completion. We expect all candidates to be
entrepreneurial, focused and fast moving. We are hiring only the best.

With our new revolutionary architecture we are looking for candidates with
proven industry experience in object-oriented methodologies, technology and
programming. In particular, we are looking for experience in Java, Java
classes, C++/C, Corba, EJB, Internet protocols, encryption and
authentication algorithms, transactional eCommerce systems, Linux/UNIX or
QNX operating systems, real-time multimedia programming (audio, video and
graphics), advanced user interface design, and hardware/systems
architecture for digital appliances.


Key Positions in San Jose

Multimedia R&D:

The multimedia R&D team is responsible for the design and implementation of
the Amiga multimedia architecture. Successful candidates will have a deep
understanding of the state-of-the-art in multimedia algorithms and
implementation.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience using
C++ and/or Java, industry experience with object oriented methodologies and
a solid understanding of real-time multimedia operating systems and
services.

We have job openings in the following areas:

   o 2D and 3D graphics engines
   o OpenGL application programming
   o Implementation and application of Digital video algorithms (MPEGII,
     codec's)
   o Implementation and application of Digital audio algorithms (AC-3,
     positional audio, Environmental Audio, mp3)
   o Interactive gaming technologies


Systems Software R&D:

The Systems Software group is responsible for the design and implementation
of system level software for the new Amiga computers. Candidates are
expected to have a deep understanding of system level programming and the
general design of operating systems.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience with
system-level programming for real-time multimedia operating systems.

We have job openings in the following areas:

   o Linux kernel, device drivers and system internals
   o Linux multimedia support, including audio, vide and graphics
   o Embedded Linux
   o X windowing systems and their implementation
   o Java system technologies
   o AmigaObject implementation

User Experience R&D:

The User Experience group is responsible for the design and implementation
of the Amiga's advanced human interaction paradigms. Candidates are
expected to have a deep understanding of user interfaces, their
implementation and their integration into an operating environment.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience using
in the user interface field and an appreciation for today's real-time
multimedia operating systems and services.

We have job openings in the following areas:

   o Human Factors/Human Interface with an emphasis on design, prototyping
     and test of advanced human interaction paradigms
   o Java based development of advanced consumer and desktop user
     interfaces
   o Java based application development of digital information appliances
   o Configuration and setup
   o Graphic Artists to provide design support for the Amiga software
     development team. creating artwork for user interfaces and
     prototyping UI paradigms.

Networking and Distributed Applications R&D:

The networking group is responsible for design and implementation of the
Amiga networking and distributed processing architecture. Candidate are
expected to have a deep understanding of Java based networking technologies
(Sockets, RMI, Jini, Security) and/or standard network technologies
(TCP/IP, sockets, Security).

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience writing
network-based applications or network-based services.

We have job openings in the following areas:

   o Java-based distributed applications using Sockets, RMI, Jini and
     TCP/IP.
   o Object distribution using Sockets, RMI, Jini and TCP/IP
   o Security, authentication, and encryption using Java and C++
   o eCommerce protocols

Developer Support and Quality Assurance:

The group focuses on supporting developers and the in-house engineering
team. This group works closely with the entire development team and the
Amiga developer community.

We have job openings in the following areas:

   o Manager of developer support and relations
   o Developer support engineers
   o Quality assurance engineers
   o In-house build and release management
   o Development tools and release of tools
   o Technical writer for software documentation
   o Web master for developer support site.

The hardware group is responsible for hardware architecture and design of

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven hands-on industry
experience in hardware design, systems architecture, and ability to work
with external technology partners.

We have openings in the following areas:

Transaction Services Engineering:

The group is responsible for architecture and implementation of Amiga
back-end services. The group works closely with the Networking and
Distributed applications group.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience in
electronic commerce, server systems and transaction processing.

We have openings in the following areas:

   o EJB and Corba
   o Java based services development

Advanced Technology:

This group of "Technologists" is focused on our tracking and evangelizing
emerging technologies.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience in
his/her respective field.

We have openings in the following areas:

   o Advanced Human Interaction Technologies, with a particular emphasis on
     digital information appliances.
   o Advanced Networking/Communications, including evolving Internet
     standards and wired and wireless home networking technologies.
   o Convergence/Broadband Technologies, including digital video and
     interactive television.
