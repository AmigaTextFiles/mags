Merry Cryptmas everyone and welcome to this page of festive humour. We were all thinking a while back of what Special items we could include on this page when Zola our Webmaster came up with this idea.

Well as you all know it's The Crypt's tradition to poke fun at something or someone each edition and so keeping with it we can't think of a better person than good old Santa Claus.

Santa, that jolly, fat gutted old fart that pays us a visit every Christmas, bringing toys for the children and yet more socks and handkerchiefs for the adults. Yes Santa, it's YOUR turn to be a victim of The Crypt.

Readers, enjoy the humorous cartoons we have prepared for you, but above all have a very lovely Cryptmas.
