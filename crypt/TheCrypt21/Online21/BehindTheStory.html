The Story behind the Story

by The Dungeon Master

The following story was not really intended for publication, it is in fact an EMAIL! Let me explain.

Way back on Halloween Sunday, Ferret and I were bored, the last edition of The Crypt magazine had just been released and the mailing list was very quite. Ferret decided to post the start of a silly story to The Crypt mailing list and I responded by adding a bit more to it. The story went back and forth with each of us adding to it until it was complete.

Just a bit of fun was all it was mean't to be,but others on The Crypt list had different ideas. "Put it in The Crypt, put it in The Crypt" was response that we received. So apart from some grammar and spell checking....You can now ALL read what daft things your Editors get up to on their day off!
