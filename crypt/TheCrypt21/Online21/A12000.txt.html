The A1.2000 - Background

The A 1.2000 is the result of more than 4 years of continuous work, first as a dream, second as a personal project and at last as a company product.

It was born to response the user's demands of a reliable, full up and easy management computer.

Based on the original format of the Amiga A1200, but with a brand new design and surprising features like the "EASY OPEN" case, automatic power-up, 24x CD-ROM with double audio output, fast SCSI II controller, 3D accelerated graphics for applications, games and digital video, RISC processor at 300mHz, Powerlan ethernet PCMCIA card for local network or internet through ADSL, 3D glasses output and a modular system, upgradeable to its next generation.

Forget gigantic and monolithic towers with obscene innards that needs half a room, and forget to reinstall your operating system every few weeks. We guarantee you that AmigaOS WORKBENCH NEVER has to be reinstalled.

Forget computers with fixed and limited screens and expensive peripherals dominated by a few companies, with the A 1.2000 feel the power of a 17" monitor or whatever you wish to buy and use standard components with free or public domain drivers.

Forget software at prohibitive prices, You can be legal and original because the Amiga software cost a fraction of the prices of other platform software with the same or better features.

In Vision we think this is the definitive computer, take a closer look and you will agree.
