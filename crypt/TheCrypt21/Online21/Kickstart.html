Kickstart

Kickstart is an Amiga user group, formed in December 1997. We had our initial meeting in March 1998 and we haven't looked back since. We currently number over 70 full members, meeting every last Monday of the month in Ottershaw, which is about 1 mile from junction 11 of the M25.

Our members have a wide variety of interests, which cover almost every use you could possibly put the Amiga to. This was why Kickstart was formed, to help others to learn more about and get more from their Amigas. We welcome anyone from the serious user to the game player. So come along, Ottershaw is real easy to get to.
