Hoisin Sauce

 




Hoisin Sauce ... one of the Life Blood of Chinese cooking .. However buying these Small jars from the Supermarkets can work out very expensive indeed. Try this very simple recipe to create your own. The Ingredients can be purchased from most supermarkets, Or if you prefer try a Chinese Warehouse/Supermarket which is normally a lot cheaper.


Ingredients


4 Tbsp. soy sauce 
2 Tbsp. peanut butter (Smooth)
1 Tbsp. honey
2 tsp. white vinegar 

1/8 tsp. garlic powder 
2 tsp. sesame seed oil 
20 drops Chinese-style hot sauce 
1/8 tsp. black pepper 



Method


Simply mix together by hand. At first it does not appear like it will mix but keep at it just a bit longer and you have Hoisin.
