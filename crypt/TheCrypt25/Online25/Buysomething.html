Purchasing by Mail Order

By Riyan Productions

THE CRYPT features many adverts from smaller companies that no longer advertise in the newsagents paper magazines. Just because they choose not to do so does NOT make them or their products inferior in any way what so ever. You can order in confidence just the same as you would from the large mail order companies.

Please remember especially when ordering software that the item you require is available and also suitable for YOUR particular computer set up. A quick telephone call or E-Mail is all that is required of you to avoid disappointment BEFORE sending off your money.

Remember that THE CRYPT compiles its adverts some time before the magazine is finally completed and distributed to its readers, so a quick telephone makes sense just in case the item is temporary out of stock.

Please also remember when ordering items to TELL the company WHERE you saw their advertisement. It will greatly assist them as to their success in magazine advertising.

Go on, don't just sit there, order something NOW , you and your computer deserve it.
