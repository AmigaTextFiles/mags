Sausage Stovies




Serves 4



INGREDIENTS


2 lb Potatoes
1 lb Onions
1 lb Sausages
1 Tsp Sage
1 Tsp Thyme
2 Beef Stock Cubes
Salt and Pepper



METHOD

Peel and slice the potatoes and peel and chop the onions. Dice the sausages. In a baking dish put a layer of potatoes, a layer of onions and a layer of sausage. Season to taste and sprinkle over half the herbs.

Continue the layers until you have used up all the ingredients, ending with a layer of potatoes. Sprinkle on the rest of the herbs.

Dissolve the stock cubes in 1 pint of hot water and pour into the baking dish. Bake in a medium oven, 350F, for 40 minutes.

Serve with crisp green cabbage.
