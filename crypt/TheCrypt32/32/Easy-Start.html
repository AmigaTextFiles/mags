Easy-Start


        


It's time to throw away your jump leads.  The Easy Start is the quickest and most effective way to start a car with a flat battery.

A flat battery on a cold winter's morning can be a nightmare.  You're desperate to get to work, and what are your options?  Almost electrocuting yourself trying to do a jump start, or doing battle with the public `transport' system?  Well now there's a new safe, clean and easy option that doesn't involve getting covered in engine oil or being talked to death by grannies on the bus.

The Easy Start is one of those simple but revolutionary concepts.  All you have to do is connect it to your car's cigarette lighter, then connect the other end to the cigarette lighter of a car with a good battery.   As little as five minutes charging time can provide enough of a boost to get you moving!

The Easy Start is incredibly, well, easy, and effective too. There's no messing around under the bonnet or waiting around for hours.   Even if your headlights aren't lighting up, a 25 minute charge is enough to get you going.  It's not only a quick and easy method of starting your car battery, but a much safer one than jump starting.

A 4.5 metre cable means your partner/friend/ kind neighbour probably won't even have to move their car to connect their end of the Easy Start to yours.   The up to the minute electronic technology utilised by the Easy Start makes it completely safe for both cars.

The Easy Start is an essential accessory for any driver, easily stored in a glove compartment or toolbox, and ideal for car trouble on the move.  Whether it's a brass monkey winter or a temperamental old banger that's worrying you, it gives you peace of mind for less than 15 quid.

Suitable for all 12V car batteries.

Warning:  Make sure to disconnect the Easy-Start from both cars before starting the car with the flat battery to avoid blowing the fuses of the cigarette lighters.


Average Charging Times:

If engine only turns over slowly - 5 minutes
Clicking sound is heard - 10 minutes
No clicking sound is heard - 15 minutes
Headlights do not light - 20 to 25 minutes
