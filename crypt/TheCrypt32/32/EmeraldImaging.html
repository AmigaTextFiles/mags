Our friends at Emerald Imaging  (The makers of the popular adventure game "AQUA")  Have announced new of their latest Project ... HYPERCANNON.  Sadly for Classic Amiga users, HYPERCANNON will initally be released for the Pegasos range of computers running MorphOS.


For a review of the Pegasos running Morphos see: http://browse.to/emerald/review/review.html




Announcing a new game from Emerald Imaging!



HyperCannon is the next project that we will be working towards. It's a unique "arcade style" action shooter with 3D rendered graphics and addictive gameplay.   HyperCannon has been in development for several months and is currently our most active project.   Our Webpage will be updated as HyperCannon develops. Enjoy some screenshots from current beta version at. http://browse.to/emerald


HyperCannon features:

o 24bit 3D rendered graphics
o alpha channel transparency
o fully animated in-game objects
o 16bit digital sound (AHI)
o responsive controls
o many onscreen objects
o various weapon and powerup types/levels
o various "wave types" and bonus rounds
o several different game modes
o branching paths
o end of level bosses
o highscore table
o Gamepad and keyboard input
o focuses on classic form of gameplay
o animated backgrounds
o nice intro sequence


In discussions with the Author I asked Kelly Samel of the posibility of HYPERCANNON being ported to the AMIGA platform, His reply was thus:

"It will depend somewhat on what arrangements we make for distributing HyperCannon. I am fairly open to porting to other OSes but at the same time the Pegasos is our focus right now. HyperCannon runs well on a G3 600 based PPC system currently and uses around 16MB of ram, although this will increase in the future. The release date is not certain but the game is progressing well and some "beta testing" is being done already."

Aqua is coming to a Pegasos near you!



Our original adventure game Aqua will soon be released for inclusion in the SuperBundle for the Pegasos community to enjoy. The SuperBundle is a growing software repository provided by Genesi for all Pegasos customers. We hope you enjoy Aqua!


Aqua is still available to purchase for the Amiga range of computers, Or try before you buy, Download the demo here: http://www.telusplanet.net/public/samel/aqua/aquaindex.html



Aqua features:

- Hi-resolution gfx
- 3D Raytraced visuals
- animated cut-scenes
- Sampled sound
- Easy point'n'click control
- Saving and loading at any point in the game
- Lots of interactions
- First person perspective
- ScreenMode requestor
- And much more...


Requirements:


68020 or higher processor
OS 3.x or higher
8MB of ram
AGA or RTG display (Works with CGFX4 and Picasso96)
CD-ROM drive
