Thalion Software


Remember the good old days of Gaming on your Amiga?   Can you remember such wonderful titles as Amberstar - Ambermoon - Chamber of Shaolin or perhaps A Prehistoric tale.

All these excellent titles (And loads more) were produced by a company called Thalion Software.

Thalion Software was formed back in 1988 by two friends, Erik "ES" Simon and Holger Flöttmann.   Over the next six years Thalion went on to create some of the most technically brilliant games ever.

Although Thalion Software is long gone, Their games and memories live on, in the excellent Thalion Webshrine website, maintained by enthusiast Alex Holland



http://thalion.amiga.tm


A quick click on the above link will take you back into a wounderous world of Retro gaming delights . Read all about these excellent games, Listen to the original music, AND better still, freely download some of these classic games, and again enjoy the experience of the Amiga at its best.
