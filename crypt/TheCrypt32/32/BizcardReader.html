BizCard Reader




The most compact and mobile card reader available.  If you're being handed business cards right left and centre, it's difficult to keep track.  The details of a potentially vital client could be screwed up and forgotten in your pocket.   But now you can keep track of all your contacts with one incredible tiny device - The BizCard Reader.

The BizCard Reader is an amazing miniaturised scanner.  It scans business cards in seconds, then accurately organises them into the correct fields, such as contact's address, telephone number and e-mail address.  You can then export data directly to your PC or Palm, and synchronise it with Outlook, Excel or other programs for easy management.

With the BizCard you can categorise, edit, sort and search through your contact list quickly and easily.   Sort cards alphabetically, by contact or company name, or contact job title.  You can view records as a list, or as if looking through an album.   There's also a simple editing mode for altering contact details or deleting them from your records.

Because the BizCard is so incredibly small, it's ideal for taking on conferences or business trips.  It's ideal for use with a palm device or laptop, so you can scan card details in almost as soon as you get them.

You can also send data directly to your printer with the BizCard.  Save hours that were once wasted typing up address labels! And don't worry about cards that use fancy or unusual fonts.  The BizCard recognises virtually all fonts, and also features Background Removal technology, which extracts pertinent text from a colour-filled background.

The Biz Card is easy to use, convenient and portable, and will save you huge amounts of time and effort.  Why just stuff business cards in your wallet and end up losing them when you can use this?
