Credit Card Cam


    


Introducing the world's thinnest digital camera.  The Credit Card Camera really does fit in your wallet!

As its name suggests, the Credit Card Camera is the same size as an everyday credit or debit card.  Measuring just 8 x 5cm, less than half a centimetre thick, and weighing just 35 grams, you'll never find a more portable camera.   But this isn't some novelty item that's all about the size.  It's actually a genuinely high quality digital camera.

Despite its tiny size, the sleek silver Credit Card Camera manages to cram in a 64MB SDRAM memory.   Store up to 26 images in high res mode, and 104 in low res mode.  It also features its own built-in viewfinder! And a major feature of the Credit Card Camera is its revolutionary battery which re-charges while you download pictures to your PC - a brilliant time-saver.

The Credit Card Camera is easy to use, and also boasts an Auto Exposure and white balance adjuster, to give crisper detail in bright and dim areas of a picture.   You'll be pleasantly surprised by the picture quality.

You can take the Credit Card Camera anywhere.   Why carry a heavy, bulky camera around with you on holiday, on excursions or day trips?  This slips unnoticed you're your pocket, bag, or of course your wallet.  It's the ultimate convenient camera.
