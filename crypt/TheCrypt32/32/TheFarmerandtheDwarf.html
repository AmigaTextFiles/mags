The Farmer and the Dwarf
Submitted by - Dennis Robb


A dwarf with a lisp goes into a stud farm.

"I'd like to buy a horth" he says to the owner of the farm.

"What sort of horse?" said the owner.

"A female horth" the dwarf replies.  So the owner shows him a mare.



"Nithe horth." says the dwarf, "Can I thee her eyeth?"

So the owner picks up the dwarf to show him the horses eyes.

"Nithe eyeth.", says the dwarf, "Can I thee her teeth?"

Again the owner picks up the dwarf to show him the horses teeth.

"Nithe Teeth" says the dwarf "Can I thee her eerth"

By now the owner is getting a little fed up but again, picks up the dwarf to show him the horses ears.

"Nithe eerth.' he says 'Now...can I see her twot?"

With this the owner picks the dwarf up by the scruff of his neck and shoves his head deep inside the horses vagina.  He holds him There for a couple of seconds before pulling him out and putting him down.  The dwarf shakes his head and says:

"Perhaps I should weefwaze that...



Can I see her wun awound?"
