Kelly Samel of Emerald Imaging  ( Authors of the Popular Aqua Adventure game)  has announced work on a "New" Amiga game called eVe,  (an Action RPG)  has begun.

Announcing the next game from Emerald Imaging!


eVe is the next project that we will be working towards.  It's a unique   "console style"  action RPG with hand drawn artwork beautiful music and addictive gameplay.  Work has just began on this project and the game is still in Alpha state but I am putting it on the site so that I can keep people updated on the development status.


Here are few preliminary screenshots .


    


These are still early and much of the gfx etc.  will be changed/improved in the full game.  (particularly the "in-game" shot).   It should give a small idea to what is planned.  I have finished much of the NPC and "talk window" code.   More work on the actual gameplay and programming will be the focus for the next bit and then the gfx will be touched up and finished.  Most of the main characters have been designed now and work has begun on the story and events in the game.  I will reavel some of these aspects when they are finalized...
