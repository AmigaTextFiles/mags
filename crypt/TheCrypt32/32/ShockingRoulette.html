Shocking Roulette




Russian Roulette has to be the ultimate game of chance.   Now you can experience the fear and anticipation in your own home with this `shocking' new game.

Now don't worry that we're advocating playing with guns.  Shocking Roulette doesn't feature bullets, and you won't have to scrape anybody's brains off the floor afterwards in a Deer Hunter style.   But you will have to face your fears and leave your fate to chance, or end up looking like a prize wuss.

Shocking Roulette is an electronic game for 2 - 4 players.   Each player places their finger in one of the holes on the board.  Press the start button, and the agonising wait begins.  A flashing light makes its way around the board, accompanied by the sound of a roulette wheel.   As it slows, the tension reaches fever pitch - and then it stops.  And one unlucky player receives a jolt of electricity through his or her finger!

Shocking Roulette is great for parties, the pub or lads' nights in, and is a great way to make cheapskates get their round in; you'll be amazed at how quickly they disappear to the bar.  Until you actually play it's hard to understand just what the anticipation feels like.   But whatever you do, keep control of your nerves - if you sweat, the shock gets worse.

Are you man, or woman, enough to face the challenge of Shocking Roulette?  Can you avoid screaming like a big sissy if you get shocked?  The choice is yours:  risk a mild electric shock, or risk making a complete turkey of yourself by wimping out.

Please note:  The mild electric shock you get from Shocking Roulette is completely harmless.   However, it can interfere with pacemakers, so if you use one, we don't recommend this game.  Also, Shocking Roulette should not be used by sufferers of epilepsy or related illnesses.
