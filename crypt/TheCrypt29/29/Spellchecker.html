Spellchecker/Thesaurus/Databank





The spelling of 160,000 words can be checked on this new powerful pocket Spellchecker/Thesaurus/Databank. It even searches for words with similar spelling or pronunciation i.e. enter ELIFANT and it becomes ELEPHANT.

The Databank will store up to 200 files (names, addresses, e-mail and web sites etc.) has a programmable currency exchange feature and also a clock with daily alarm setting.

Measuring 7cm x 12cm x 1.2cm (2¾ins x 4¾ins x ½ins), it has an incorporated calculator making this the ideal pocket Spellchecker/Thesaurus/Databank. Requiring 2 x lithium batteries (supplied).
