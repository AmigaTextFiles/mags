CardCam - Ultra thin digital camera


Get clicking with CardCam - the world's slimmest digital camera.

Only 6mm thick and small enough to slip into any wallet or purse alongside your bank and credit cards, this advanced compact digital camera is the business. Carry it with you everywhere and capture the moments other cameras miss.

This couldn't be easier to use. Just switch the power on, frame your shot through the optical viewfinder and click - it's as simple as that. Unlike many other digital cameras, this is one model you don't need a degree in astrophysics to use!

It's 8MB internal memory will hold up to 101 320x240 photographs or 26 larger shots of 640x480. Manufacturer Oregon Scientific's unique automatic brightness adaptive technology Autobrite, meanwhile, ensures that the bright regions of an image never saturate and that the darker details are always crisp and clear.

What that adds up to is sharp images perfect for dropping into business documents, files or presentations. Just connect the camera to your Windows or Macintosh computer's USB port, download the photos via the included software and they're ready to email to friends or publish on the web immediately.

The CardCam comes complete with protective wallet, strap, USB cable and all the software you'll need. Fitted with a rechargeable battery, it takes just two hours to power the unit up by connecting it to your computer.





CardCam Specifications:


Actual pixels - 0.31 MP

PC system requirements - Win 98, ME, 2000 & XP 200Mhz Pentium or better with 32MB RAM, 200 MB free disk space, CD-ROM drive and USB port.

Mac system requirements - Power Mac G3 200Mhz or better running OS 8.6 - 9.2 with 32MB RAM, 200 MB free disk space, CD-ROM drive and USB support.

Measures 85.6mm x 54mm x 6.0mm (35g)
