STEVE'S EDITORIAL



 

We know the Amiga market is very small, but to hear AIO (Amiga Information Online) is closing is a great loss to all of us. It`s bad enough that we have no commercial magazine, and we have to rely on volunteers to make the high quality printed magazines that are available. But to hear that another disk mag is closing, and one that set the standard for all the rest of us makes me very sad indeed.

Thanks to the many readers who send in stuff for inclusion, we are over whelmed with all you contributions, but we always have plenty of space for more


If an article catches your eye, or there`s something that you disagree with in the Crypt then write to us and let us know, we like to hear from you and will reply to all of them.

steve@thecryptmag.com

Steve Evans
Webmaster
