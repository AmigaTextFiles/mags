AIO - Amiga Information Online to Close



It has been announced that the popular, and long running Amiga Magazine AIO is to close down later this year. AIO has been a faithful servant to the Amiga users for some 6 years, and will be sadly missed by many.

RIYAN Productions would like to offer their thanks to all members of the AIO team, for not only their obvious devotion to the Platform, but also all their hard work over the years.

We wish you all the best for the future.





AIO Announcement



Amiga Information Online


AMIGA INFORMATION ONLINE will sadly be closing with the release of issue 71, May 2003. After 6 years in production we feel we can no longer offer the Amiga market the kind of support it deserves. With the Amiga market getting smaller and smaller by the day we can no longer bring you as many reviews, as much news or as many articles as we would like. Instead of turning into a poorly run magazine we have decided to close from the May 2003 issue.

We will be printing your goodbye comments in our last issue so please feel free to send your goodbye comments to : goodbye@aioworld.com
