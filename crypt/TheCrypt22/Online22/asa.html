ASA is based on the south coast of Hampshire.
Our members come from all over the world, including Europe and the US.
We are a fairly large group with over 50
members, most of whom are active and dedicated users.

As a group we don't have fees or a membership system, all we ask
is you contact us and if possible join our mailing list.

We offer: Help, support and advice
Our large knowledge base means we can usually help sort out
any problems users are having. We offer to fix machines for free and
give advice on all aspects of the Amiga.

Email mailing list
Our active mailing list is guaranteed to be 100% Amiga related.
Feel free to subscribe and ask any questions you like, from the most basic
to the most complex.

Regular pub meetings
The true reason for our group's existence! :)
