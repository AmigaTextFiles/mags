Valhalla I (The Lord of infinity)

Level 1

Pick up the door key, operate the floor tile in the bottom left hand corner of the room, pick up the map. Come out of the room and take the first left, pick up the gold coin. Go back to the main corridor and walk down the wide but narrow room, pick up the silver key from a stool, and the Talisman of Light from the altar. Walk up and right again, the first door you come to on your left can be opened with the first key you found. Inside take another gold door key. Come out of the room and back the way you came, find the door that is opened with the key you have just found. In this room operate the floor tile in the bottom right, pick up the map that was beneath it. Leave the room and turn right, pull the lever which opens the door next to it. The room beyond this door is trapped so watch your step, you now have the map but the floor is marked so you may not need it. Pick up the small chest key, (this fits a chest in another room where a withered rose is hiding) the grey crystal and at the bottom right right hand corner of the room is a lever you should pull. Leave this room, walk up and turn right and go in the room you have opened by pulling the lever. Put the gold coin in the wishing well and take the red gem. Take the stick and operate the floor tile that it was lying on, this reveals a ring belonging to the Princes father. Take it and travel back to the room where you began the level. Put the ring on the identity stone, it is exchanged for your birthright which should be placed on the altar of justice. This is in the other trapped room. You should now have the scales of justice in your possesion. Give these to the skull that asks "What is your Quest?" He will allow you passage into another room. In here there are two crowns, leave the jewelled crown where it is or you will come a cropper. Take the other crown instead. Pick up the strength potion and the chest key, then pull the lever. In the next room, drop the red gem on the portcullis that came up behind you to remove it. Leave the poison but pick up the plank. Put the withered rose in the water of life and take the fresh rose. Go back to the trapped room where you found the grey crystal and open the chest. Take cold protection potion and another chest key. Find the door that is opened with the silver key, put the plank across the chasm to get to the chest, inside find a chalice and a gem. Travel to the room where the skull is that says "Do You Love Me?" Give him the rose and he opens a door. Ignore the first portcullis that appears behind you and continue through the corridor, pull the lever to open the door. Inside is the gaurdian with blue eyes, pull the lever, take the gold symbol, then leave the room and turn left. Another portcullis appears in front of you, drop the gem onto it to get past. Travel through the door you opened by pulling the lever and head back into the room where the skull is that asks "Do You Love Me? Put the crown on Meredith's throne and take the warriors shield that appears in it's place. Fill the chalice with water from the tap in the same room, then go and find the miracle stone. Put the chalice full of water on the miracle stone to turn it to wine, present this to the relevant skull who rewards you with a rubber soles potion. Travel to the boulder that has so far blocked your passage and drink the strength potion, pick up the boulder and put it down were the hole it creates doesn't trap you for eternity. When the strength potion has run out, drink the rubber sole potion. The corridor beyond is filled with the warriors electrocution devices, the rubber sole potion will protect you but time is short. When you reach the end of the corridor, insert the stick in the hole in the wall to make a lever which you have to pull to open the door below. Take the warriors sword then put the warriors shield on the warriors tomb to disarm the passageway. Pull the lever which opens the door opposite. Before entering the room make sure you have found the gem in the U bend of the passageway so you can disarm the portcullis. In this room place the gold symbol on the engraved altar then travel back the way you came and put the warriors sword on the bloodstone, this action covers the sword in blood and is the perfect gift for the skull who asks you for blood, he opens the door to the next room. Inside here find the gaurdian with the red eyes. Take the chest key. Open the relevant chest in which you find the a door key and an unlit candle, the key opens the door at the end of the now disarmed passageway. Inside four skulls all ask you for the talisman of light, give it to the skull in the bottom left of the room and he allows you passage into the next room, all the others will send you screaming to your death. Next door is the gaurdian with the green eyes, put the grey crystal on the stone of two colors which turns it into a yellow crystal. Take the credit card off the floor and hurry back to the room where the warriors tomb is located. Insert the credit card in the electrical machine to open yet another door. In here, take the chest key and place the cold protection potion onto the altar decorated with a black and white circle take the fire protection potion that appears in it's place and take the rock off the stool. Drink the potion and take the eye of the beholder from the hot coals in the room opposite. Put the eye in the water of beauty and take the green crystal. Return to the gaurdian with the green eyes, face it and insert the green crystal. You can now take the first of the four orbs which you place on the relevant altar in the large room where the four altars are found. Your chest key fits the chest in the first trapped room, take the jug of infinity and the piece of coal, travel down to the second trapped room and put the coal on the crystal flame, take the black crystal. Fill the jug of infinity with water from the tap, then travel back down the passageway to the room where the warriors tomb is situated. Insert the black crystal in the gaurdian. You now have access to a small room, pull the lever which opens the door across the chasm and put the unlit candle on the fire stone to light it. The skull in here asks you for the password. Pick up the lit candle and return to the chasm room. Put the lit candle opposite the other candle and the chest opens. Take the red crystal, insert this in the relevant gaurdian to glean the second orb. Cross the chasm and don't forget to pick up the plank as inside the next room another chasm opens up behind you. Take the second gold symbol and a gem. Put the symbol on the second engraved altar in the room where the eye was and a door opens into a room where the infinity stone is found. Put the jug of infinity on here and take the gem of infinity. Operate the floor tile in the top right hand corner of the room and take the dagger. Go and find the skull that asked you for the password, face him then look at the gem of infinity. He allows you to enter a room where you find a stick and an altar with world war engraved onto it. Put the dagger on here and take the poppy. Give this to the skull that asks for rememberance, he rewards you with a parchment, this is full of clues to help you, so read it well. Put the stick in the hole in the room where you found the eye and operate it. This opens the other door behind which is very little apart from a stool. Place the rock on here to gain access to the room where the gaurdian with yellow eyes is lurking. Insert the yellow crystal and take the third orb. Put the parchment on the altar of sadness which is near the bloodstone and take the tears of loyalty, place these in the water of hope in the second trapped room and take the blue crystal. Go find the gaurdian with the blue eyes and grab his orb. You have found all four orbs and if you haven't allready, place them on their appropriate altars, the portcullis in front of the last gaurdian descends so you can insert the gem of infinity, your last task in level one.
