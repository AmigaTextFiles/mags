Help me make it through the Night
Do you remember the song Help Me Make Through the Night?

Here is the Glasgow Version


Take the rollers fae y'ur herr,
Shake thum loose, an' let thum fa',
Lie them doon wae your gless eye,
In that chanty by the wa'.

Tho' y'ur teeth ur black an' gaun,
An' y'ur breath smells oota sight,
Ah'll pit a claes peg oan ma nose,
tae help me make it thro' the night.
