Angus Toffee


Ingredients


  • 1 and half lb (675g)of sugar
  • 2 oz (60g) ground almonds
  • 1 oz (30g) of butter
  • Quarter pint (150ml) milk


Instructions


1) Melt butter in saucepan
2) Stir in ground almonds,sugar and milk bring to the boil
3) Boil for 7 minutes, stirring well
4) Remove from heat and stir for 2 minutes
5) When thick pour into shallow buttered tin and cut when cold
