Time never stands still, and neither does The Crypt . In the past we have always produced an Amiga disk version of our magazine, but as more and more people move on to other platforms that means less and less people could read the disk version. But one version that could be used by everyone, and is more versatile than DMC is the website or HTML version.

It has meant a steep learning curve for us learning to write pages in the new style, but we think it's worth it. And as Ian says in his editorial, we are just wasting precious time making 3 versions of every issue. So from now on The Crypt will be made in a version that everyone can read and enjoy and it also means we can bring you things we couldn't before, like animated pictures and full colour pictures, which I'm sure you'll agree is the best way forward.

Don't forget, if you still want to read the older Amiga disk versions of The Crypt then just E-mail me and I'll send you all the old issues on a CDR.

Steve Evans
Webmaster
