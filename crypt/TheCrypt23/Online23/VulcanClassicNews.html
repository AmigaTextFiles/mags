Valhalla Classics Episode 02

After the great war, Infinity used this old section of the castle as a prison for those he feared the most. The Shamen had always lived peacefully in the kingdom of Valhalla until Infinity took the crown. He promised them sanctuary but instead he tricked them, and using their own magic against them he turned them to stone. Years have passed, and the five most powerful Shamen are thought to still stand within the ruins, enslaved for eternity to protect the three legendary magic rings of escape. The little prince must outwit the Shamen and gain entrance to the rooms where the magic rings are kept. In each of these rooms there are (thought to be) electrocution devices, armed to maim anyone who tries to steal the magic rings. Only the bravest and most ingenious adventurer will be able to deactivate the traps and ultimately escape this dangerous and foreboding place.
