Vodoo Wars




Vodoo Wars : Cross Destinies

Vodoo Wars is an adventure/strategy game which is inspired by great names of the genre such as Warcraft/Starcraft, Settlers, Clivilization or Age Of Empire ...

You'll have to take the head of one of the four races proposed in the game and lead your community to success : the Humans, the Orcs, the Chibas or the Krakhen.

On a classical basis, Vodoo Wars try to be original with a very different style of playing.

By the way, 4 different game modes are proposed to you and the scenario is sophisticated and rich enough to give you many hours of play.

But be carefull, strenght is not enough to become a leader in the world of Vodoo Wars ! You must deserves Power, will you be wise enough to become it's only keeper ?

Vodoo Wars is developped by the Theory of the Dark Side crew (T.D.S.) which it is the first production.

Vodoo Wars is not a standalone product from Melina Softworks, it's a collaboration in which Melina Softworks invest a lot of efforts.

It is a very huge project which needs many many ressources and it is very difficult currently to predict any release date.

Anyway, all the latest news concerning the project will be available on that site so do not hesitate to come and visit us !

The specifications
Vodoo Wars is a demanding game and it's target is clearly not the low end Amiga configurations.

Even if the minimal configuration has not been clearly defined yet (it will come with the evolution of the game), the primary goal is clearly the high end Amigas et more particularly the AmigaOne.

Here is some stuff for you to have an idea :

  • High resolution graphisms (640*480 and 800*600 in 16bit minimum), no AGA support
  • 16bit sounds and MP3 musics
  • Around 120 units and 60 buildings
  • More than a hundreds of animation steps per units
  • TCP/IP support for network games
  • PowerPC (68k if possible)



You'll need a good amiga if you want to get the best from Vodoo Wars, that's good, the AmigaOne is comming so don't wait to cross the line, Vodoo Wars will fully support the AmigaOne.
