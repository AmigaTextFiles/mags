How It Works

Naturally Speaking is an incredibly powerful program that is amazingly easy to use. Simply install the Naturally Speaking software, and follow the step-by-step prompts.

You will be asked to perform a series of voice recognition tests, so that the software can adjust to your specific speech pattern. After set-up had been completed, you simply speak into your microphone, and the Naturally Speaking software will "type" everything you say, word for word. It's just that easy!
