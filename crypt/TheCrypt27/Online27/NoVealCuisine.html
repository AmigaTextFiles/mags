No Veal Cuisine


The ready meal, it's stated, provides a wholesome eat,
So while we were out shopping, we thought we'd have a treat,
Well you can just imagine, our horror and despise,
When opened right before us was a meal of meagre size.




We searched in deadly earnest to find elusive meat,
There wasn't enough of it for a sparrow to eat,
And yet the pack was big enough to indicate a feast,
Of spuds and vegetables, we had enough at least.

Now who ever's responsible for what was passed as tea,
I'd dearly like to take out, just for a day with me.
And show to him a truckers' cafe and the size of a meal,
Then ask of him "Now truly, how hungry do you feel ?"




Those people in Armani suits, they don't do work like this,
If you think that is a helping, let me put it like this,
You're very, very wrong pal, so once I will exclaim,
Buying your frozen products - I never will again !

By- Baz.
