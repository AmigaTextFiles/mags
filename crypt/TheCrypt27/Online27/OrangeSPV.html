Orange SPV Phone





This is the first mobile Phone to run a version of Windows, allowing it to send and receive e-mails and surf the internet and store appointments, to-dos and contacts that can be synchronised with a home computer so you're alays up to date.

SPV (Sound, Pictures, Vision) has a clip on camera, Stereo headphones (with Media Player so you can listen to digital music and even watch videos on the phone) and a memory-card slot for storing photos and other data.

To use its internet features, you need to buy its Access Pack, costing £6 a month for unlimited surfing and e-mail.
