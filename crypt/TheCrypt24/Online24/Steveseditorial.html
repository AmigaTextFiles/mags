STEVE'S EDITORIAL





The great thing about being the webmaster for the magazine is I get to read the articles before everyone else. And for this issue everyone has worked extremely hard making another wonderous Crypt.

Reading through the articles about Aliens I find myself still unsure if they exist or not, but I must admit there is some strong evidence to think they do exist. Have a read and see what you think. Then drop us an E-mail on what you think and we`ll print out the replies in the next issue.

Another article that caught my eye is from our resident PC Editor, Ian Urie, on his continual fight with BT trying to get Broadband in his area of the woods. I`m lucky where I live as I`m in an area where we can get the super fast connection, but many throughout the country want Broadband, but because of where they live they can`t get it for love nor money. Surely something should be done to force BT to provide what a growing majority of the populous want?

If an article catches your eye, or there`s something that you disagree with in the Crypt then write to us and let us know, we like your mails and will reply to all of them.
