About Computoz

Computoz is a major computer software/hardware retailer covering all the
latest platforms and consoles, including Gamecube, Xbox, PS2, PSone, PC,
Gameboy Advanced and N64.

Computoz commenced trading in Oct. '93, since then, Computoz has relocated twice on to larger premises.

We are open from 10am - 5pm, 7 days a week.
