MAGICAL SPELL TO BRING IN MONEY




You will need: Several Yellow or Red Onion skins (the dry outer skin) I go to the store and buy 1 onion and fill the bag with the skins that have fallen off.



1-green or orange candle & holder

1-pair of needle nose pliers

1-lighter or matches

1-baking sheet or large flat pan


Assemble all items together and then sit in a quite place with the items before you. Ask for your Spirit Guides to be with you and say a prayer of your choice. Be thankful and be sure to visualize yourself as already having your desires.

Now light the candle and place the onion skin in the pliers. Now you are ready to burn the onion skin.

See in your mind all the money you have to burn. I have given this spell out to many people and have many confirmations on how well it worked for them. You can do it as often as you like.

Always end your spell with SO BE IT, AND SO IT IS.
