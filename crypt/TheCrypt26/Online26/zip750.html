The new Iomega® Zip 750MB drive save and protect your important data.


Iomega Zip drives have, for quite a number of years, Been a excellent storage medium for your personal files or as a quick and easy back-up format. The original Zip 100, Often described as a Large Floppy Disk replacement, Was quickly overshadowed by the Zip 250. The latter allowed you to store a massive 250 megabytes of material on a single disk, and often used as an extra harddrive, Or a simple means of transferring files between computers.

With modern day Computers requiring more and more Storage space, It was parhaps inevitable that Iomega would have to increase the storage capacity of their Zip Drives.




Iomega- Zip 750MB


The Iomega® Zip 750MB drive has more speed and capacity to save, protect and organize your valuable data. Using durable Zip 750MB disks, you can backup and move your files at 7MB per second! Give your data ultimate security with the included Iomega Automatic Backup softwarestart saving the smart way.

Compatibility - Reads and writes Zip 750MB and 250MB disks; Reads all 100MB disks.
