STEVE'S EDITORIAL



After our last edition of the magazine, I went on holiday to Majorca, with the knowledge that another issue was still 2 months away, so I had plenty of time to relax and enjoy the sun. Then what do I find as soon as I return? Our beloved Editor wants to get the next issue out in 3 weeks! "What? Are you crazy?" are some of the things I said to him. :)

So here we are three weeks later and we managed it. Not only do we have another packed edition for you, but we also have another exclusive! (We're to good to you) Along with our feature section on Broadband Britain. (And thanks to those that took part in our questionaire) We have an exclusive interview with a company on the frontline of Broadband technology, SatDrive. We all know that BT are dragging their heals getting this service to all the Country, so what do you do if your not in the catchment areas that are fortunate enough to get Broadband? You turn to companies like SatDrive, who can get you a very fast connection no matter where you live, great news for the 40% of the population that BT thinks are not worthy.
