Brothers MPrint



Well It had to happen. In these days of Pocket technology, Where people in the Business World demand Computers that are small and Portable, Easily carried and containing better features, Someone somewhere would invent the perfect companion for the "Hand Held" device.

The BROTHER MPrint




The Brother m-Print micro printer is lightweight and can print from any laptop or Pocket PC. Slim, compact and easy to carry around, the m-PRINT is the micro answer to the massive problem of printing when out of the office.

Its 'palm size' means it can be carried effortlessly, even in a jacket pocket. It uses A7 paper that is automatically fed from its own cassette, eliminating the need to feed each sheet individually. With easy to handle paper that doesn't curl, you are ensured quality printing anywhere, anytime.

Product available from 1st October 2002


Weighs less than 300g
Printing speed - 4 ppm
Built in lithium battery
Prints from pocket PCs via infrared interface
Prints from laptops via USB connection
Windows driver reduces A4 documents to A7
6 paper cassette types available (A7 size)


The BROTHER MPrint retails at a whopping £249 ..But Obviously aimed at the Business traveller, A handy addittion to anyones Briefcase.
