Scottish Tablet Recipe

Submitted by ian Urie


Ingredients


2Lb sugar
Large tin of condensed Milk
1 large mug of milk
knob of butter.


Method


Put all ingredients in a laarge pan
Dissolve very slowly stirring occasionally
Let simmer at the same heat until mixture turns a brown colour and mixture thickens.
Should take roughly 40 minutes........
Take off heat and beat with wooden spoon vigourously.until it starts to harden.
Pour into greased tray and allow to cool..........

Voila ........TABLET!!!!
