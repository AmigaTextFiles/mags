POWERFUL LOVE SPELL TO BRING LOVE TO AND HAPPINESS TO YOU





1-pinch Catnip

1-pinch Chamomile

1-pinch Couchgrass root

1-pinch Ginger root


Mix herbs together while visualizing your wishes as though they are happening. Light a pink or red candle and the charcoal with the flame of the candle. Sprinkle your herbs on the charcoal and burn half of the mixture. Now take the other half and put it in a red Mojo bag with something of yours and something of the one you wish for. If you don't have anything of theirs write their name on a small piece of paper asking to bring in your true love.

Carry this bag with you until you obtain your desires.

Always end your spell with SO BE IT, AND SO IT IS.
