Squatters Tater Chowder



Ingredients:
4 large potatoes diced
1 large onion chopped fine
1quart milk
Salt & Pepper to taste
11/2 Tbs bacon grease or 1 Tbs butter
4 Tbs flour
2 hardboiled eggs


Method:

Dice the spuds and boil in salted water until tender.
Add milk and bring to a boil.
In a large fryin' pan or 12" a Dutch oven, brown grease and flour.
Avoid burning by stirring constantly.
Add to the soup while stirring and boil until it thickens.
Add a touch of favorite seasonin' if desired.
Add the chopped boiled egg last.
