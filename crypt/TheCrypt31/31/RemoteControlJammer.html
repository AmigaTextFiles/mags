FAQ

Q. Does the Remote Control jammer only work on Tv/Videos etc or does it work on any IR Device, eg : Car remote controls?

A. The remote jammer is designed to work on TV frequencies - it should not work on car alarms etc.



Q. Will this work in the U.S.? Does it support all formats?

A. The US does work on the same / similar IR frequency for the TV however it is not our core market and we have not done any research into their rules and regulations regarding this so we cannot confirm that it will work.



Q. What range will this work from?

A. It needs to be approx 2m from the infra red receiver.
