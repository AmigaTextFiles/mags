IAN'S EDITORIAL


The CRYPT 31 - The Gunslingers,  Well I have had fun doing this Issue.  As a child growing up, Cowboys were my hero's, Hardly surprising when you consider almost every second Film, TV Series and Book was/or had a western theme.

So what of the gunslingers?  Those men who bravely lived by the speed of their guns.  Facing each other across a dusty street, hands poised over leather holsters, waiting for the final tick of the town clock.  To live or die by the speed of your handgun.

Sadly none of this is true, most Gunslingers never faced up to others in a straight draw.  One however did have the honour of being the fastest gun, I will leave you to find that out for yourselves, but beware of the Dying Dentist.

So what else is incorporated into this issues pages.  Well for the first time for a long time, we have quite a bit of Amiga related articles/Programs for you.

If you don't know what an Amiga can do, Check out our "Rocket Scientist" Article.

Check out our reports on BareED, a New Amiga Text Editor with a surprising feature.  Sort out all your time troubles with your battery backed clock by using TimeSwipe or Facts.  Would you like to know exactly whar size that Drawer in your partition is, Easy with Drawersize.  Are you still using AGA and Workbench 3+, Then We have a real treat for you, Get your self SystemPatch and see the increase in speed  (Certainly when loading Web graphics).

We, of course, also have our PC helpful tips, a large collection of your Jokes and a brilliant selection of Guest Writers articles.

Something for everybody in this our 31st issue of the CRYPT Magazine.
