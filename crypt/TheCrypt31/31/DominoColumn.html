The Domino Column


Phew! .. Sure is hot this year!  You know you humans are lucky, In cold weather you wrap up well with thick coats, gloves and scarfs, in warm weather you wander about in shorts and T-Shirts.


    

Have you ever given consideration to us poor animals, I can't take MY coat off when it hot, Well all I get to wear, Summer or Winter is a black leather collar (a small strip of dead cow skin) and if I take that off, I'm liable to end up imprisoned in the Dog Pound.

Now don't get me wrong, I love all this warm weather we are having, Lying relaxing in the garden, and I am a lucky doggie, I don't get locked into cars like some do.  Still  it can get pretty uncomfortable at times, so remember to leave plenty water around for your pets.



Winter is a different "Kettle of fish" You lot put on thick socks, Rubber Boots, Overcoats, hats and gloves.  Then have the cheek to clip a lead on my collar and take me out.

I'm in my bare feet, for Christsake!  I'm Knee deep in Snow, I'm bluddy freezing to death ...And YOU WANT ME TO PEE!



Have you any idea how hard it is to relieve yourself when your shivering with the cold, and have frostbite in your important bits?

Natures Neutering ... Frozen Dangley bits.

I want a wooly jumper for my Christmas this year, and perhaps a pair of warm underpants.



Humans are not the only creature to suffer the weather extremes.
Take care of your Pets
