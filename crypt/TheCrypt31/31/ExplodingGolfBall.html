Exploding Golf Ball




Golf has a reputation as not the most exciting sport in the world. So is it a bore best left to men in diamond-patterned jumpers, or can it be livened up? Of course it can, with these novelty golf balls.

These golf balls can turn a humdrum round of golf into crazy golf; try the Cloud-Flite Exploder. This ball will go in the hole alright, but whether it comes out in one piece is another matter entirely.

Watch with glee the look of horror on your partner's face as he makes it a perfect hole in one, only for the ball to explode in a massive cloud of smoke! Cruel? Maybe a little - but we don't have a problem with that.
