Nokia 6800

As a Classic messaging product, the Nokia 6800 phone answers the diverse messaging needs of the customer; e-mail for lengthy messages, SMS for quick notes that need to be actioned immediately and MMS to enhance messaging with images. It's a phone - with a keyboard!




Available:

In the first quarter of 2003

Nokia 6800 Phone Messaging reaches new levels of ease and efficiency with the Nokia 6800 phone. This unique 2-in-1 product concept has been especially designed for easy text input and quick access to text-based applications, using the integrated full keyboard.
