SILVER TIME PROJECTOR




Here's a great gadget for lazy people

It looks like a torch and it does in fact have a very bright torch built in, but press another button and it will project the time onto the wall in digital format. In a dark room it can easily project an image over 4ft across! Ideal for checking the time in the middle of the night.

The clock is powered by it's own built-in battery which is supplied, the projector/torch requires 2 x AAA batteries (not supplied).
