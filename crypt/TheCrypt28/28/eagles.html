Welcome to the home of the Eagles MCC. We're a motorcycle club based in and around the Grantham, Lincoln and Boston area with around seventy members.

We're open to all bikers young or old. Frankly we don't give a shit if you ride a trike, cruiser, or a plastic rocket any one is welcome to join. Basically we're just a group of people wanting to have fun, party a lot, and ride bikes.



 

Feel free to browse around and enjoy our site. If you would like more infomation on us feel free to contact the club chairman.

 

Club meetings are held on the first and third Friday of every month at the Three Kings Pub, located at Threekingham on the A52 between Grantham and Boston. We meet up at around Eight PM for a few beers and a chat, why not pop in to see us. The pub is well sign-posted of the A52 about fourteen miles East of Grantham, so there's no excuse :-)
