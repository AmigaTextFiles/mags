Canon B180C - Printer/scanner/fax/copier




With the B180C you can have affordable fax communication and convenient colour printing from the desktop.

Replacing the popular B155, the B180C is a fully featured entry level fax that can also colour print via a USB connection to your PC. Using Canon's reliable Bubble Jet print engine the B180C delivers high image quality, a print resolution of 720x360dpi, 64 shades of grey halftone, and a speed of 5ppm (mono) and 2ppm (colour) - meaning you can have a versatile, affordable and convenient solution for your small or home office.


 
(NOTE: UK model comes with handset kit.)

Fax features include a full 14.4Kbps modem for a fast 6 second per page transmission speed that saves communication costs and time. The enlarged fax memory enables 42 pages to be stored if the unit runs out of ink or paper, and facilitates advanced fax features such as sequential transmission - so you can send the same fax to multiple destinations.

 

For added convenience, the B180C comes with a push button handset and an inbuilt fax/tel switch. 12 one touch and 100 coded dials can be stored for quick dialling, and the B180C incorporates automatic and manual redial, and an on-hook key. You can also choose to connect an answer phone to your B180C.  

Reliable paper handling features include a 100 sheet paper capacity - reducing the need to add paper so often, and a 20 sheet auto document feeder for added convenience when sending multiple page documents.

 

The B180C uses either the BX20 or BC21e cartridges - which are quick and easy to replace, and operate in conjunction with the B180C's ink saver mode so that you can keep costs at a minimum.
