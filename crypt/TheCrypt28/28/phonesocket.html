ONE FOR ALL PHONE LINE EXTENSION



One For All Wireless Phone Line Extender.


Turns any electrical socket into a phone socket!

·    The Wireless Phone Line Extender turns ordinary electrical sockets into telephone outlets (no extra wiring required)

·    These can be used to connect computer modems, video set-top- boxes, Internet appliances, phone or fax machine or other data oriented devices to the phone line.

·    The product consists of a base unit and an extension unit. Both units are adapter-type plugs, which fit the electrical sockets in the home. In each unit there is a facility for plugging into the telephone socket, close to the telephone.

At a second location (e.g. at the computer) the extension unit is plugged in and connected with telephone wire to the equipment which needs a telephone signal. The product has a Plug-In and Play concept.

·   No software is required.
