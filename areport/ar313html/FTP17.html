DESCRIPTION
    BattleDuel is a game like 'Artillery Duel' on the C-64:

    Two players fight against each other with cannons placed in a windy mountainous landscape. To hit the other they change the power and angle of the cannons. A duel is finished if one of the cannons is totally damaged.

    Some features:

    • up to 4 players
    • computer opponents
    • normal duel mode, 2 tournament modes, practice mode
    • ECS/AGA/GraphicBoard support (only one program)
    • nice graphics and sound effects
    • full multitasking
    • OS friendly
NEW FEATURES
  • Screenmode Requester for better support of graphicboards (tested with PicassoII and Piccolo)
  • Minor bug fixes
SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS
    Kickstart2.0, Harddisk, Flickerfixer recommended, at least 1 MB memory
