DESCRIPTION

Tiger's Bane is a combination of side-view action game and flight simulation. Using the keyboard, joystick, or a CD32 pad, you fly one of 7 different real-world combat helicopters (along with up to two computer controlled wingmen) through over 80 missions in 5 Operations, in addition to randomly generated missions. There are over 25 different allied and enemy ground and air units, and 5 configurable weapon systems to use to blow them up. Missions take place in cities, island chains, and deserts, and include weather effects such as rain, wind, fog, thunder storms, and variable time of the day and night. The game also features multiple skill levels, and big, flashy explosions.
