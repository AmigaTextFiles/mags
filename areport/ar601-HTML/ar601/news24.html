FUSION Mailing List

A new Amiga mailing list is now open! You can subscribe by following the details below, or by going to the FUSION Mailing List W3 site located at: http://www.cullasaja.com/Amiga/FUSION/

The purpose of the FUSION Mailing List is to discuss the Microcode product, FUSION, a software-only Macintosh emulator for Gateway Amiga systems. This list is maintained by Vince LaMonica <vjl@cullasaja.com> and has no official connection to Microcode. Technical questions about FUSION should be directed to the list, not myself.

To subscribe to the FUSION list, simply send a message with the word "subscribe" (without the quotes) in the Subject: field to fusion-list-request@cullasaja.com.

To unsubscribe to the FUSION list, simply send a message with the word "unsubscribe" (without the quotes) in the Subject: field to fusion-list-request@cullasaja.com.
