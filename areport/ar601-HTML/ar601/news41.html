Vulcan America Changes Operators

LATEST VULCAN NEWS


VULCAN AMERICA has changed operators!

Steve Ocepeck the former operator of Vulcan America in Ohio is transferring the business to Terry Fike in Texas.

Terry the owner of `Turtle Lightening Software` will now be responsible for supplying all Vulcans titles to American and Canadian Amiga users. Terry offers top quality `mail order` services throughout the region and will also be Vulcans main trade outlet in North America.

Visit the Vulcan website for more information, ordering details and current price lists. (www.vulcan.co.uk)
