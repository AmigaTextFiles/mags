Harv Laser, sysop of the 2.5 gigabyte Amiga Zone on the Portal online service, has announced that the services provided by Portal and the Amiga Zone will soon be available to customers through a World Wide Web interface.

Previews of this service are now available on http://www.portal.com/~harv/

This move would make the Amiga Zone the first Amiga online service with a Web interface-provided nobody beats them to getting it up and running.
