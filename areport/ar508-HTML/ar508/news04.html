DESCRIPTION

DanNews is a NNTP news unbatcher. It is much faster than RNews, and can cope with relatively small amounts of free memory.

NEW FEATURES

DanNews 1.6 copes even better than DanNews 1.5 with corrupt news batch files (corrupt news batch files commonly result from dropped connections to newsservers).

DanNews requires Kickstart 2 or later, and a news set up which stores news articles in UUNEWS:
