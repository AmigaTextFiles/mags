Cleveland Area Amiga Users Group (CAAUG) Now Online

CLEVELAND, OH, August 24, 1997 -- One of the nation's oldest computer support groups, the Cleveland Area Amiga Users Group (CAAUG), is now on the Internet. Their address on the World Wide Web is

http://www.ca-aug.org/

The CAAUG Website contains news of upcoming CAAUG events, membership and contact information, as well as suggestions for browsing other Amiga related sites.

Founded more than a decade ago, the CAAUG is dedicated to the advancement and understanding of the Amiga computer. The group is a non-profit organization open to all who support the Amiga and are interested in having fun with other Amiga enthusiasts.
