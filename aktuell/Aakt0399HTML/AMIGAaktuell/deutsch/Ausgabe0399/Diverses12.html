12. Finale Development kündigt Newsreader NewYork II an

»February 23, 1999
For Immediate Release:

Finale Development, Inc. is proud to announce the next generation of our popular usenet newsreader, NewYork II, which will debut at the popular Gateway Amiga Show in St. Louis, March 12th.

Over the past year we have listened to, and implemented many user suggestions and have arrived at what we believe to be the best newsreader on the Amiga platform.

If the Amiga press loved NewYork when it came out two years ago, then we can hardly wait for the reviews of NewYork II .. offline reading, mass- decoding of entire newsgroups at once, 'bookmarking' of important articles, powerful kill-file prefs to block spam .. it's all in there and more.

Wrap this up in a familliar, fully Amiga StyleGuide compliant interface that looks and feels like an Amiga *should*, and you have a powerful app which is also easy to master.

For a sneak peak, visit the new NewYork II page on our web site:

http://www.finale-dev.com/NewYorkII/index.html

You'll find the best of the old, supercharged with the brightest of the new in NewYork II.
