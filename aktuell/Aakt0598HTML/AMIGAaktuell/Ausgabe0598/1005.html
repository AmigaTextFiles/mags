DESCRIPTION
So what is Wingnuts? Well, bluntly stated, Wingnuts is the most popular combative sport watched and played on the planet of Holy Doobie. Each week millions of fanatical fans tune in to watch as the six pilots are strapped into their heavily armed contraptions, cheering thunderously as they take off into the arena and fly their dance of homicidal death. Their objective is brutally straight-forward - destroy the opposition and remain the last pilot in the air.
