»Engineering and Technology Jobs at Amiga

Please choose: San Jose, San Diego

General

Amiga is a highly entrepreneurial start-up company. We are headquartered in San Diego, and have development and technology groups in both San Diego (La Jolla) and San Jose (near the airport). The following positions are based in one of these two locations.

At Amiga we are designing next generation Internet-ready, consumer-oriented digital appliances. The Amiga name is associated the world over with user-friendly, low-cost, powerful computing. We are taking this philosophy into the next generation, enabling products from hand-held Internet appliances to high-end graphics computers that help the user rather than frustrate them. See www.amiga.com for more details on Amiga, our history and the community.

We are looking for senior engineering candidates who have a proven industry track record of developing and delivering robust, top-quality software and hardware on time. At Amiga we believe strongly in both teamwork and individual contribution, and all staff members are expected to drive his/her commitments to completion. We expect all candidates to be entrepreneurial, focused and fast moving. We are hiring only the best.

With our new revolutionary architecture we are looking for candidates with proven industry experience in object-oriented methodologies, technology and programming. In particular, we are looking for experience in Java, Java classes, C++/C, Corba, EJB, Internet protocols, encryption and authentication algorithms, transactional eCommerce systems, Linux/UNIX or QNX operating systems, real-time multimedia programming (audio, video and graphics), advanced user interface design, and hardware/systems architecture for digital appliances.

Key Positions in San Jose

Multimedia R&D:

The multimedia R&D team is responsible for the design and implementation of the Amiga multimedia architecture. Successful candidates will have a deep understanding of the state-of-the-art in multimedia algorithms and implementation.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience using C++ and/or Java, industry experience with object oriented methodologies and a solid understanding of real-time multimedia operating systems and services.

We have job openings in the following areas:

  • 2D and 3D graphics engines

  • OpenGL application programming

  • Implementation and application of Digital video algorithms (MPEGII, codec's)

  • Implementation and application of Digital audio algorithms (AC-3, positional audio, Environmental Audio, mp3)

  • Interactive gaming technologies

Systems Software R&D:

The Systems Software group is responsible for the design and implementation of system level software for the new Amiga computers. Candidates are expected to have a deep understanding of system level programming and the general design of operating systems.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience with system-level programming for real-time multimedia operating systems.

We have job openings in the following areas:

  • Linux kernel, device drivers and system internals

  • Linux multimedia support, including audio, vide and graphics

  • Embedded Linux

  • X windowing systems and their implementation

  • Java system technologies

  • AmigaObject implementation

User Experience R&D:

The User Experience group is responsible for the design and implementation of the Amiga's advanced human interaction paradigms. Candidates are expected to have a deep understanding of user interfaces, their implementation and their integration into an operating environment.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience using in the user interface field and an appreciation for today's real-time multimedia operating systems and services.

We have job openings in the following areas:

  • Human Factors/Human Interface with an emphasis on design, prototyping and test of advanced human interaction paradigms

  • Java based development of advanced consumer and desktop user interfaces

  • Java based application development of digital information appliances

  • Configuration and setup

  • Graphic Artists to provide design support for the Amiga software development team. creating artwork for user interfaces and prototyping UI paradigms.

Networking and Distributed Applications R&D:

The networking group is responsible for design and implementation of the Amiga networking and distributed processing architecture. Candidate are expected to have a deep understanding of Java based networking technologies (Sockets, RMI, Jini, Security) and/or standard network technologies (TCP/IP, sockets, Security).

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience writing network-based applications or network-based services.

We have job openings in the following areas:

  • Java-based distributed applications using Sockets, RMI, Jini and TCP/IP.

  • Object distribution using Sockets, RMI, Jini and TCP/IP

  • Security, authentication, and encryption using Java and C++

  • eCommerce protocols

Developer Support and Quality Assurance:

The group focuses on supporting developers and the in-house engineering team. This group works closely with the entire development team and the Amiga developer community.

We have job openings in the following areas:

  • Manager of developer support and relations

  • Developer support engineers

  • Quality assurance engineers

  • In-house build and release management

  • Development tools and release of tools

  • Technical writer for software documentation

  • Web master for developer support site.

Key Positions in San Diego (La Jolla)

Hardware Development and Project Management:

The hardware group is responsible for hardware architecture and design of the new Amigas. The group works closely with the other groups of the Amiga R&D team and our partners.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven hands-on industry experience in hardware design, systems architecture, and ability to work with external technology partners.

We have openings in the following areas:

  • Implementation of reference design

  • Project management working with external partners

Transaction Services Engineering:

The group is responsible for architecture and implementation of Amiga back-end services. The group works closely with the Networking and Distributed applications group.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience in electronic commerce, server systems and transaction processing.

We have openings in the following areas:

  • EJB and Corba

  • Java based services development

Advanced Technology:

This group of "Technologists" is focused on our tracking and evangelizing emerging technologies.

The successful candidate has 8+ years of proven industry experience in his/her respective field.

We have openings in the following areas:

  • Advanced Human Interaction Technologies, with a particular emphasis on digital information appliances.

  • Advanced Networking/Communications, including evolving Internet standards and wired and wireless home networking technologies.

  • Convergence/Broadband Technologies, including digital video and interactive television.
