Total Destruction 3D

I have just received news on a game that sounds amazing. It is a beat-em-up being written by a group called Deepcore Entertainment Software.

It isn't just any old beat-em-up though, have a look at Virtual Fighter and Tekken on the consoles and you'll have an idea of what this game is like. It is a true 3D, polygon based, texture mapped beat-em-up, something that has never been seen on the Amiga before.

Info on the game is quite low at the moment, however, I can tell you that you'll need quite a powerful machine to play it on. The game requires at least an AGA/CyberGFX machine with an 030 for the 320x200, 2x2 resolution and an 040 for the 320x200, 1x1 resolution. It will also require 2Mb Chip RAM and 8Mb Fast RAM.

The game is being scheduled for a Summer '98 release on CD-ROM only.

I'll have more info for you soon...
