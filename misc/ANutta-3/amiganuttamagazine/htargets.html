Hard Target
Screenshots

A couple of weeks ago I brought you news of an upcoming game called Hard Target from Vulcan Software. Well, now I can bring you Exclusive screenshots of what is billed as being Virtual Cop on the Amiga.

Going buy these screenshots, the game looks incredible. And I for one am very excited by this game. Could this indeed be Virtual Cop for the Amiga? Only time will tell.


Actual details on the game are low at the moment. One thing I can tell you however is the Amiga you will need to run it. The game will require an AGA Amiga with at least an 020, 2Mb Chip RAM and 8Mb Fast RAM. It will also require a 4x speed CD-ROM drive. The interesting part of this is the fact that it will require 8Mb Fast RAM, one of the first games on the Amiga to demand this.

Anyway, for now have a look at the excellent screenshots on these pages. Hopefully I will be able to bring you more news soon...
