Railroad Tycoon
Mircoprose
Bit of an oldie this one but still an excellent game. To increase your ban balance, press the CAPS LOCK key and type CHEATERCHEATERWIMP. You will soon find that you have loads of money and your land will be full of building blocks. You can do this as much as you want too. keys.

Formula One Grand Prix
Mircoprose
Another oldie but due for a CD-ROM re-release very soon. For a fail safe way of winning any race, go into the pits on the very last lap and press escape. Noow use accelerated time and when the finishing times come up, you will find yourself in first place, excellent!

Froniter
Gametek
An excellent tip for the follow-up to Elite. Fly to CEMIESS system (-2.5, -2.5) and go to the planet Emerald. Land it Patricks Exchange and keep buying Precious Metalsuntil your cargi hold is full. Now fly to the Cegreeth system (-1, -3.5) and sell all the precious metals for a massive profit. Keep repeating this until you have enough money for whatever you want.
