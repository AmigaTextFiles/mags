Trauma Zero

Trauma Zero is a new Shoot-em-up that is being developed by a new Italian team. Details are quite low at the moment but from what I can tell from the rolling demo which has been released, the game looks fantastic.

Like I say, the game looks very good. It contains 256+ colour graphics, and scrolls at a super smooth 50hz. Another feature of the game is that it will feature a simultanias 2-player option. The game is AGA and will also be HD-Installable.

I will hopefully be able to bring you more details on the game very soon..
