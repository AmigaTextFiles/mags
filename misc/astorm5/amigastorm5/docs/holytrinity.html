The Holy Trinity is based on the traditional lines of a war between good and evil. It was a thousand years ago when an army of demons destroyed the neighboring villages of Garandia in search of a hidden satanic power concealed for centuries by a warlock run amok. Many lives were lost at the hands of the execution squads deployed by the Evil One, the survivors of these attacks were forced to live a horror worse than death: enslavement in the Evil One's army of the damned. The Evil One uncovered the Soul Forge, a massive power source capable of leveling an entire village in the blink of an eye. However, unknown to the Evil One, a chosen one was being born into the world, chosen by the High One to vanquish the Evil One and his army of demonic souls. You play this Chosen One. You'll notice by the hand drawn design picture of Phoenix that The Holy Trinity is at the initial design stages but it is interesting to see the early beginnings of such as game.
