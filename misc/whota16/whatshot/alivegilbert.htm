Alive MediaSoft Sign Up Gilbert Goodmate

I have just received news that Alive MediaSoft have signed up the rights to Release Gilbert Goodmate and the Mushroom of Phungoria.

Gilbert Goodmate is a adventure game in the style of Monkey Island, I reported on it a while ago, but since then I haven't heard anything about it. This in until now.

New details on the game are low at the moment, however, I can tell you that it will be coming on CD only and will feature full speech.

Look out for new screenshots and info very soon!
