The Final Odyssey Released and Web Site Up

The Final Odyssey a puzzling action adventure has just been released in the last few days by Vulcan Software.

The game leaves you in control of Theseus the Hero to solve the complex puzzles and mysteries of the Labyrinth and eventually destroy the Minotaur as well as locating all the girls that have been imprisoned and getting them out safely. It contains five action packed worlds including many brain bending puzzles.

The price is £29.99 UK Pounds with it supporting English, German, Italian, Spanish, Norwegian, Finnish and Danish languages.


The maker of The Final Odyssey, Peter Spinaze has just made a web site dedicated to it which I would strongly recommend you visit as it contains much info about it including it's development, screenshots and more. So check it out now.
