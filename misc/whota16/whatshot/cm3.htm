Championship Manager 3 For The Amiga?

Their is a small chance that we could get Championship Manager 3 for the Amiga but it's up to you whether we can get this excellent title which is even still in development.

On the Amiga Championship Manager 3 Campaign web site you find a form which I urge you to fill in. The developers, Sports Interactive Ltd have shown their support for an Amiga version. There is the possibly that an Amiga Publisher could try to gain a License to develop and Publish this title for the Amiga.

It's now up to you to show your support and I hope with all the Games News Amiga Flame has provided for you for over a year, you could take some time out to fill a form in. This form can be found at the web address below.
