F1 Software Titles Now Available From Two Outlets In The US

F1 Software who have the largest collection of Amiga Licenseware in the world are giving US Amiga owners the chance to purchase this software and receive it far quicker than ever before due to F1 Software making a deal that will allow two known companies to distribute this software in the US.

For more information read the Press Release below:-

F1 Software now available from two outlets in the United States. This new move allows American Amiga users to get their hands onto hundreds of top titles without having to spend a small fortune on shipping costs to Europe.

Sagittarius Software have sold our 5th Dimension range for over a year and welcomed the move into F1. Steve Ocepek the owner of Sagittarius said "The new range is brilliant and should appeal to all users, young and old".

Our newest distributor is Andrew Kellett of Mushroom PD. Andy is famous for his AMOS PD library, which now boasts the largest collection of AMOS titles and source code in the world!! He is also an active member of Team Amiga, supporting the machine 100%.
