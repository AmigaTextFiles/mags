New Screenshots of Hard Target

Hard Target, the first ever Virtual Cop type game for the Amiga has become a game that many people will looking forward to hearing more of in the new year.



Vulcan Software are working hard on this title, with new screenshots revealing that much of this work at the moment is aimed at getting the locations to look realistic to create a atmosphere for what will be full of action. Vulcan Software have even got a Light Gun in development which will work in conjunction with Hard Target, something I will be peculiarly interested in trying out.

Hard Target will be a high spec game, with you requiring 8MB Fast Ram, 4 x Speed CD Rom drive and will be AGA only.
