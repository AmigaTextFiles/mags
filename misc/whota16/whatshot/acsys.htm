ACSYS


Unique are developing a shoot'em up game inspired by the classic Turrican called ACSYS.

The story is set in the year of 2074 and mankind is exploring other worlds in deep space, the reconaissance cruiser XPOSE discovers a strange structure on the 5th moon of Scarus. They send down a team to check it out but they never return and so the first deployment of A.C.SYS (Autonomous Cybernetic System), a special droid constructed and programed for crisis areas is used. It's objective consists of rescuing all the survivors and to eliminate all forms of aggressive enemies. That means it's time for you take control.

There will be up to six different weapons, some upgradeable with two weapons being able to be carried simulaneously. It'll have multidirectional parallax scrolling and 360° shooting in every position with plenty of intelligent enemies to content with. ACSYS will also have special music and soundFX with animated sequences between levels.



The current requirements are at least 4MB Fast Ram and it'll need a 4x speed CD- Rom drive.
