Wanna Help With Fubar

Below is an advert from Andrew King of Q-Group:-

    We at Q-Group are looking for a graphics artist to draw graphics and art for a new game due for commercial release in 1998.

    Our new game is called fUbAR and details about the game can be found at http://homepages.nildram.co.uk/~oondy. It's an action/strategy war game which has taken the best bits from many classic and modern games, pulling them into one. It's been set to be big by people just looking at the website and talking to me direct.

    You must be extremely talented graphically wise, and have a year or two at least of game graphics experience. You will be able to use colours to the maximum and be very creative and original in your drawing.

    You also must be in the United Kingdom, speak english (it helps :), have an internet connection and a phone. Communication like this is very important, since Q-Group members are distributed around the country. IRC capabilities would greatly benefit.

    To give you an idea of the workload, there are tiled background graphics, static images, animated images, sprites and bobs (static and animated), full motion video (since fUbAR is due to be a strictly CD-only release), and anything else that would be appropriate.

    Send your queries and artwork demos or portfolios in lha/jpg format (but ASK before sending anything over 64K!) to..
    aking@coventry.ac.uk

    Thanks
    Andrew "Oondy" King, Q-Group leader and main programmer.

Anyone who is interested please do not hesitate to contact them and be part of what is looking to be an excellent game.
