Dimensions Computers Announces Web Interface, Current Specials

December 08, 1997 Dimensions Computers, an Amiga mail order dealer selling a full line of global Amiga products, announces the official launch of their World Wide Web Amiga shopping interface and a bunch of great deals for the occasion.

http://nrex.net/dimensions/

The catalog is available in FRAMES and NOFRAMES, and features a secure shopping cart system which supports the SSL encryption supported by the most popular Amiga browsers. Dimensions vows to routinely update their catalog daily, and provide the best prices available to Amiga customers in North America.

Now Amiga customers will be able to browse through a comprehensive listing of Amiga products, look at product pictures, view current specials, and find links to actual manufacturer's specifications for each product, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week on a fast T-3 U.S. server. Ordering is possible via COD, Visa, MasterCard, and Prepay.
