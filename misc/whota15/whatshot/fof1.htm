Fortress Of Fear Help


Fortress of Fear an action RPG like Diablo by Digital Reality Softworx and they are looking help in its production.

If any of you have done a complex and professional looking 3D model of something they could use for Fortress of Fear (e.g. dragons, humans, monsters, castles, weapons, etc.), you can send them to the Developer for possible use. You will get full credit and will receive the full game free.

Try to send the objects in multiple formats like dxf, Imagine, Lightwave, Maxon Cinema etc. as these are the best ways for them to use the object. This also applies to Audiofiles (.mod, .wav, Midi, mpeg-3 or whatsoever), fonts, etc. Just send in everything they can possibly use for a game like Fortress of Fear and the possible reward can be a free copy.
