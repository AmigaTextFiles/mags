News In Brief - 16th - 30th November

Welcome to the News In Brief Section. This page contains all articles from 16th - 30th November. Enjoy.

Latest Alive MediaSoft Info

Alive MediaSoft have revealed that their second game will be called Arcangel, which will be a very high spec with it requiring 16 Fast Ram, CD-Rom only and will be made PPC compatable. Currently the game is all on paper and they have only one programmer dedicated to bringing it to our faithful machine, but being locked away in the broom cupboard all on his own is not doing him any good, and he is lonely and in need of a friend.

The Haunted, another game being developed by Alive MediaSoft is expected to be out early in the new year.

Latest On Quake For The Amiga

News is just coming in that Quake for the Amiga was being run at the Computer 97 show in Cologne. It was believed to have been run on PPC and was said to look good although a little slow.

There were no details on who intends to port it fully for release, and at present this News has not yet been confirmed. If anyone is aware of any information relating to Quake being shown at the show then please e-mail me.

VaporWare & NetConnect News Update

As of 19th November, a new Vaporware FTP mirror is online for UK users. The FTP space has been provided by UKOnline, and is located at vapor.ukonline.co.uk and a new beta version of VoyagerNG, version 2.92v, is now available for registered users to download and use. Version 2.92v addresses some more bugs that were found in previous releases. Version 2.92p is still available, and a new public evaluation version will be released soon. But, from 18th November, NetConnect Update #11 is available, which will updated Voyager to Version 2.92v. Expect more news as soon as I get it.

Latest Miami News

As of 9th November, Miami version 2.92c was released, a beta version of Miami 3.0. Expect Miami 3.0 to be out very soon.

Latest YAM News

YAM 2.0 has been mentioned before in an article here, but the latest is that YAM 2.0 is expected to be out before the end of this year. I will inform you as soon as it is.
