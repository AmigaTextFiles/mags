Sixth Sense Investigations

The following article is from the Amiga Flame web site.

Developer/ Publisher:CinTech/ Islona

Requirements:AGA

Upon loading you will get a short Intro showing an amination of a kid sitting down in his room playing a computer game, namely Sixth Sense Investigations.

The storyline tells of a crazy young man who has the ability to communicate with the spirit of a sarcastic man. A friend who thinks of himself as a detective, profits from the psychic abilities of this crazy young man by using his skills to solve the most bizarre problems of the rich. In this demo you take control of the detective O`Cheeser.

The controls are easy to master, the left mouse button is used to select objects, characters and exits. The right mouse button automatically selects default action for an object. Both buttons will pause the game with the left resuming play. The bottom part of the screen is where you choose what O'Cheeser should do, from opening things, examining objects, using objects, talking to other characters and so on.

The cartoony colourful type graphics are absolutely designed brilliantly. Although only music is in the demo, but full speech and sound effects will be included in the full game.

In the demo you are only able to go to places like the Hall, Laboratory, Toys'nu, Cheese Store but it gives you an excellent impression of what to expect. Inside of each individual building has been well designed and laid out and so much is going on the screen to take in. The Laboratory is a really good example, before you get in you have see the Doc talking talking to his fish, an Giant Ant is in the corner of the screen and then Doc is playing computer games. It is wonderfully detailed and the developers CinTech have paid close attention to the graphics and it makes it a joy to watch and play within them. It contains some good amination of characters and objects throughout the entire demo.

Sixth Sense Investigations is worth trying out and the full game will be released soon, so nows your chance to try it out.
