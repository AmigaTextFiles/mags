Note: AGA Users

AGA users will need both the FoundDemoAGA.lha and FoundMission.lha archives. It is also advised that you download the FoundSamples.lha otherwise things will be kinda quiet, however, the game will work without it. The FoundMugsAGA.lha is completly optional and is not required by the game.

Note: Graphics Card Users

You will need both the FoundDemoRTG.lha and the FoundMission.lha archives. As with the AGA version the FoundSamples.lha is recommeneded but not required. Also the mugshots archive FoundMugsRTG.lha is completly optional.

It is important that you download the right versions as the AGA version will not work on Graphic Card machines and vise-versa.

The author will be uploading documentation (Which was missing from the upload) and other bug-fixes, patches in the future.
