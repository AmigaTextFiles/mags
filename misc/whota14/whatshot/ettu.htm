Screenshots And More Info On Escape Towards The Unknown

Escape Towards The Unkown was mentioned in a previous article on Hurricane Software and now here is some more information on the game.

Escape Towards The Unknown, is a graphic adventure in the type of style as the Lucas Arts' © ones. It revolves around a guy called Mike Clifford who owns a Computers and Robots factory and goes on holiday to Scotland. When Mike arrives he decides after being suggested by Tom Mac Madson to take out a plane to see the Highlands. When Mike reaches the airport he is approached by three strange individuals who want Mike to with him. Mike runs away and takes out a plane but the strange individuals pull out their guns and fire upon the plane damaging the fuel tank. He manages to parchute out but injures his head on landing and wakes up to find himself in a castle with a girl that repeats him a question: "Who are you?" - Escape Towards The Unknown (The secret of the Mac Ryan's castle).


At present no other details are known about Escape Towards The Unknown but it appears Hurricane Software have quite a line up of games in development.
