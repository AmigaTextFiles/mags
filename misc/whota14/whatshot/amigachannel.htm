Spanish Electronic Magazine 'AmigaChannel' Releases 10th Issue

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The totally free Spanish electronic magazine called 'AmigaChannel' has reached its 10th issue. And we do it in style.

Along with numerous news bytes and briefs (this month bigger than ever), product announcements, we bring you our usual columns ('Padre Newton', Scene, Games,...).

But the most strong point of this issue is an exclusive interview with Petro Tyschtschenko presidente de 'Amiga International'.

Our reporters have been really bold. It is the first interview in which all the questions that worry to the Amiga community have been asked: the relationships with 'Phase 5' and David Pleasance, the next developments,

...

Taking into account the importance of this interview, you can read it (both in English and in Spanish) in the SAU ('Spanish Amiga Users') WEB (http://www.arrakis.es/amiga/). Visit our WEB today and I assure you that you won't be disappointed.
