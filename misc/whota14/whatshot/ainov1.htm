Amiga, Inc. Invites Developers to Conference in Cologne


November 7, 1997

From: Amiga, Inc.

To: All Amiga Developers

We would like to invite you to the Developers Conference, being held in Cologne Germany. Jeff Schindler will speak about Amiga, Inc.'s roll in the future of the Amiga. Due to the limited 300 seats available, we regret to inform everyone that this will be by invitation only. Amiga, Inc. would like to apologize in advance.

To request your invitation, come to the Amiga booth at the show. Present your business card to one of the people there. Sign the register and obtain an invitation. The show time and location will be printed on the pass.

Media coverage will be addressed by Darreck Lisle of Amiga, Inc. Contact him for the details pertaining to the Conference. Darreck can be reached at the show. Please no calls or E- mail. :)

The Conference at the Midwest Amiga Expo was a success. The information that was compiled will be shared, and issues that were brought up will be addressed.
