Below is a press release from the author of 'The Rim' - a new Amiga game in development.

    The Rim (working title)

    I have currently started development on a game using a Diablo style engine set in a futuristic setting. As I'm still a student though and I have a lot to do I haven't got much time so I'm looking for people to assist me. I need people in the area's of Graphics, c/asm coding, and music. I already got one Renderer, but as the game will be quite large I sure could use more people there, especially in the modeling department.

    I have permission to use the The World Foundry games background also used in Explorer, M&M and licensed out to 2260 Designs for New Horizons. The World Foundry team will also assist me with some things but they-re quite busy with their own projects.

    If you want to assist me in this game, be it as a programmer, gfx man or even a head-programmer, then contact me on this address: martijns@sci.kun.nl With "Game" in the subject line. At the moment we're still in the design stages but if all goes according to plan this could be a great release.

    The royalties will be split, we will make out the details at a later stage. Martijn Sanders,
    AKA Denan on Irc
    martijns@sci.kun.nl

Anyone interested please direct any mail to the address above.
