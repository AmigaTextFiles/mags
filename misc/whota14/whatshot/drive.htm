Drive U'Crazy - A New Ridge Racer Style Game

Drive U'Crazy is being developed by Daztechnique Promotions and is a new high spec game being developed for multiple platforms including the Amiga, specificially on a 060 processor with a good quality graphics card.

This game consists of 7 tracks and 3 Bonus tracks, in a new Ridge Racer style, a first for the Amiga. They are currently trying their best to work this game on the minimum Amiga possible, but its unlikely that the 3D mechanism will work 100% on anything less than a 060 processor with a good quality graphics card as reported earlier along with it being CD-Rom only. It will also include some really excellent Techno music written by their in-house DJ's for this game.

Daztechnique Promotions hope to have a demo ready for early in the new year so we will see more then, but for now enjoy the screenshots shown here.
