Vulcan Selling CD-ROM Drives.

Vulcan Software have announced that they will now be selling a CD-ROM drive package. The package will consist of a 4x SCSI CD-ROM and a Squirrel interface to connect it to your A600/A1200.

Vulcan who are considered to be the biggest Amiga publisher in the world have made this move on the back of announcments that nearly all of there future games will need at least a 4x CD-ROM drive as a requirement.

The whole package will cost only £149.99, this also includes delivery.

Hopefully people will who don't already have CD-ROM drives will take Vulcan up on this offer. If not you may miss out when games like Genetic Species, Hard Target and Wasted Dreams are released.

For more information please contact Paul Carrington at Paul@vul-soft.demon.co.uk.
