Diversia: New Screenshots

New screenshots of the excellent looking Command & Conquer clone Diversia have been released and are available on this page for viewing. Diversia is Ablaze's second Amiga game, Strangers there first game was released a couple of weeks ago by Vulcan Software. The screenshots on this page are taken from two different modes, Low-Res and Hi-Res, I'm sure you can tell between the two, the Hi-Res ones look brilliant.


As I have told you before, the game features 256 colour graphics and is viewable in a number of different screenmodes. It will also be supporting Network play allowing players the fight eachother over the Internet.

As I sure you can see the graphics in this game are quite excellent, they are extremally detailed colourful. One part of them I particually like is the explosions, they look brilliant.

As for requirments, the game need at least an 030 with 4Mb Fast RAM. It will also require at least a 4x speed CD-ROM drive. The game will hopefully support PowerPC processors and already supports GFX boards.

The game is currently over 50% complete and there will be a demo for people to try out very soon. For now take a look at these excellent screenshots.

For more information about this and Ablaze's other Amiga games check their howmpage out at:- Ablaze Entertainment Webpage.
