Gateway 2000 President and COO Rick Snyder addresses ITEC Expo

S.D.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--April 2, 1997--Rick Snyder, president and chief
operating officer of Gateway 2000, Inc., presented the keynote address
today at Information Technology Expositions & Conferences (ITEC) Expo in
Seattle, WA. Snyder's speech, entitled "The Gateway 2000 Story,"
discussed the company's corporate direction and market segmentation
strategy. "Our approach to segmentation is `all customers are not the

enthusiast, the major account customer and the consumer. Our goal is to
provide total value of ownership to all three segments. We believe we
can do this by adhering to our core Gateway values and dedicating
ourselves to building long-term relationships with our customers."
