New Trapped 3 Screenshots

A while ago I brought you news on the follow-up to Trapped 2, well, now I can show you some new screenshots of the game that give an insight to how good the gameis looking.


Trapped 3, which is otherwise known as 'fratzengeballer' is a completely different to previous Trapped games. Gone is the magic that was found in earlier games and in is more of a kill everything style of game.


Improvements on Trapped 2 include more enemies, more weapons, improved grahical effects such as smoke, translucency, dithering, realtime rotatable may and much more. One thing that is surprising is the system requirements, all you'll need is a 020 with 1Mb Chip, 4Mb Fast RAM and a CD-ROM drive.


The game is due for release sometime in the summer, there will be a new demo out on the Aminet very soon.
