WANTED: Dialogue writer for Gilbert Goodmate

We at Prelusion are currently producing an adventure game for the Amiga called Gilbert Goodmate and the Mushroom of Phungoria. The game will be published by Alive Mediasoft Ltd. We are looking for someone to help us write character dialogues. You would get a very central position in the creation of character conversations.

Information about the game

Gilbert Goodmate is quite similar in style to the Monkey Island games. The original story line is very humorous and entertaining. More information about the story can be found on our website at: http://pln.home.ml.org We have recently released a small playable demo of GG. You might be interested to try it in order to get an impression of the style of the game. It can be found on our site as well. If you have troubles connecting please visit AmigaFlame at: http://www.amigaflame.co.uk/amidem22.htm They have a list of servers where the demo can be found.

Six people are currently involved in the development of this title:

  • Mike Hiltunen, code
  • Daniel Nilsson, graphics & animation
  • Tobias Andersson, graphics
  • Per Hemmingsson, sketches & additional gfx
  • David Engdahl, music
  • Joel Ygberg, script

Requirements

  • You should be very familiar with adventure games, i.e. you should have played lots of games in the genre and have a good understanding of how they are structured. It's an additional advantage if you have played recent titles on other platforms as well, such as the PC.
  • You should have a good imagination.
  • You should be a very humorous person (or at least write in a humorous way) and a also be a good writer.
  • It's highly preferred that english is your native language.

What you will do

Your work will be structured in the following way: First we'll send you detailed information about all the in-game characters and the storyline of the game. For each dialogue you are to write, we'll give you some brief information. Most importantly what the conversation is supposed to "lead to". It's then your task to write a good dialogue. You get very free hands in this process. As long as the text is entertaining, appropriate, and leads to the correct event, you are free to write practically whatever you want.

Contact us

If you are interested, don't hesitate to contact us. We will then ask you to write a sample dialogue in order to see if your writing is suitable for this project. We will also supply you with other necessary information, such as payment, deadline, etc.

Please state where you live, your age and any experience you might have.

We look forward to hear from you!
