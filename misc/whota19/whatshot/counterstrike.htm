Counterstrike

CounterStrike which has joined the ever growing list of C&C clones currently in development for the Amiga, was previously mentioned in the News In Brief Section around October last year, with a limited demo version appearing on the Aminet, round about the same time.

Details are low as it is still in the early stages of development, but from I've heard it will have very detailed maps with plenty of animation and full speech. There will be tonnes of different units and buildings with the buildings changing in look for each world. Each team will also have there own unique buildings and units, so you don't get the usual blue and red as seen in other games.

It'll feature TCP/IP support for playing it over the Internet and support CybergraphX. The full version will also include special HiRes graphics, 040/060 optimized routines and will be CD-ROM only.
