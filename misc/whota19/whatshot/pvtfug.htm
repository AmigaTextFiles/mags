Professional Video Toaster/Flyer Users Group Announces the San Diego Symposium

The Professional Video Toaster/Flyer Users Group announces the SAN DIEGO SYMPOSIUM on March 6,7,8. See the latest versions of software/hardware. Hear from Mr. Newtek-Tim Jenison, Flyer Guru Joe Tracy, Aussie from OzWare, Bohus Blahut from Legacy Maker, Tom Lively from The Lively Computer, Jeff White from Visual Inspirations, Dan Wolf of Megegem, the Nova Design team on Image FX, the ProWave team, and other developers for the T/F.

Learn about the T/F in a classroom environment. Over 40 classes and more to be added. See how Powerful the T/F is for producing Video for film, TV, industrial, and event videography. Meet the developers and ask the questions you want to ask. Learn when to use 3rd party software to save you time and make your editing easier. Enter your Flyer or Lightwave video for the awards dinner on Friday, attend the Social/Party on Saturday.
