Olofight

Olofight is a good looking beat-em-up, which should have a playable demo available in a month or so from the Real Ologram.

The game will be AGA only, but suprisingly will run on an unexpanded A1200. The most impressive part of this is that it will keep up a frame rate of 25fps on this.


The graphics play a big part in this game, with the author claiming to have the game running with nearly 5000 colours on screen at once. This is made possible by some special copper routines that the author has written. Other graphical effects include all the fighters being 100% rendered, animated backgrounds, parallax and even shadow effects the actually shadow the fighters moves.

Also the game will include 10 different fighters with more than 150 animation frames for each of them with 10 special moves (attack, defense, other) with varying strength for each of the fighters and power-ups to strengthened fighters. There will be objects moving on different parallax layers, behind or in front of the fighters and a floor with 3D perspective motion.

The game will support four languages (English, Italian, German and French) both in the manual and in the game. The game will also contain some excellent music that changes depending on how the player is doing.

Like I said earlier there will hopefully be a demo available in the beginning of October. The full game will be released around Christmas time on disk only (Around 8 or so, which will of course be HD installable), there may be a CD version to follow this. At the moment, the authors are distributing the game themselves, however if there are any publishers out there who are interested, why not give them a call.
