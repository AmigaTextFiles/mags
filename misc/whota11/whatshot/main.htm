With this being the first issue, more or less all of September's news has been included, but hopefully, What's Hot On The Amiga Magazine will become a bi-weekly after this issue.

It's hard this month anyway to give any site, the title of Site Of The Month, but this month, that title goes to Nova Sector Engineering on the grounds of interest. So if you read this issue's News In Brief, you will find out that Nova Sector Engineering have obtained a license to produce Amiga compatible computers and have big plans for the Amiga and their vision of a future of the Amiga comes in the form of the CorDel Alpha and CorDel Nitro systems, which have PowerPC processors for starters. The site is regularly updated with information on these new systems.
