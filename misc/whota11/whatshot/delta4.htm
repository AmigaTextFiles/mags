Delta 4

Delta 4, is a new big all-in adventure somwhere in the far distant future is being developed by Hellhound Software and is currently in the early stages of development, with a demo coming out before Christmas.

The game involves you taking command of all Police operations related to the security system from the Delta 4 Space Station. Currently, the graphics are being redone and they include rendered pictures of the parts of the Space Station, Spaceships and robots.
