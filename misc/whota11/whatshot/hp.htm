Hell Pigs

Hell Pigs is another game that is being brought to us by Vulcan Software, and is bound to be a very startling Platoon/Aliens type game, which hopefully will be available for Christmas or the New Year.



Hell Pigs will be compatible with all Amigas, with a 020+ processor, 2Mb RAM (1Mb Chip RAM & 1Mb any), a 4x CD-ROM Drive and will also utilize if available, the AGA chipset, extra Chip RAM, extra Fast RAM, faster processors and faster CD- ROM Drives.

The game itself will come on 2 CDs which will be Workbench executable, include Full Audio Speech with 2Mb Chip, Full Motion Video with AGA Chipset (2Mb Chip RAM & 4Mb Fast RAM), complete Rotor Scoped characters with full animated actions covering 1000's of scenarios, superbly Hard Drawn backdrops with full animated interactions, 4 play Grunt Mode for maximum infiltration, extremely complex and logical plot based puzzle solving, extremely violent and action based combat sequences with Alien warfare and adult orientated humorous language and dialogue. Support is also included for the following languages : English, German, Italian, Spanish, Swedish, Norwegian, Finnish, Dutch and Danish.
