Desolate

Vulcan Software have just announced another new title that they are adding to their ever growing list of games.

This game is called Desolate, which Vulcan Software have classified as a mission objective scrolling platform puzzler shoot'em up adventure. At the moment, very little is known about this game, except that it will require any Amiga, a Hard Drive, an 020, 2Mb Chip RAM, 4Mb Fast RAM, 4x CD-ROM Drive, but it will also utilize if available, extra Fast RAM, faster Processors and faster CD-ROM Drives. The game itself will come on 1 CD which will be Workbench exectuable and will also include the entire Vulcan Demo Collection. Support will also be included for the following languages : English, German, Italian, Spanish, Swedish, Norwegian, Finnish, Dutch, Danish and Slovak.
