Over the past month or so, a number of Amiga magazines have been falling to the wayside, but now a new full colour Amiga Magazine called Totally Amiga has opened up giving North American Amiga owners, the chance to get sometime this month. The magazine will contain News, Reviews, Previews and plenty more, including a CD-ROM for subscribers filled with many new game demos, utilities, emulators, etc.

For further information and details on how to subscribe, read the Press Release given below.

Totally Amiga - Press Release

Neo-Media Publications is pleased to announce Totally Amiga, a new full color Amiga magazine for the North American Market. This magazine will cover several topics including reviews, tutorials, and much,much more!. The publication will be quarterly and the first issue will be out later this September. The prices are as follows:

$3.95 Per Issue

$15.80 Per subscription USA
$19.80 Per subscription Canada
Foreign orders,check our web site for prices.
