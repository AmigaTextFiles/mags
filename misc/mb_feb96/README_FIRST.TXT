                               IMPORTANT!
                               ==========

THE MIGGYBYTE MAGAZINE IS SPREAD IN DMS FORMAT. DUE TO AMINET NOT ALLOWING
DMS FILES, THE AMINET VERSION IS IN LHA FORMAT.

FLOPPY USERS
------------
SO YOU WILL NEED TO FORMAT AND INSTALL A FLOPPY DISK AND UNPACK/COPY THE
FILES ONTO IT.
THEN RE-BOOT YOUR AMIGA AND THE MAGAZINE WILL AUTO RUN.

HARDRIVE USERS
--------------

MAKE A DIR ON YOUR HARD DRIVE AND UNPACK/COPY THE FILES TO IT.
THEN CLICK THE MB ICON TO START THE MAG.
