Editorial

by John Corigliano

<jcorig@strauss.udel.edu>

Welcome to ExecBase! The new online magazine for Amiga programmers. This is a volunteer organization! We rely totally on the help of Amiga programmers to write our articles. Currently the staff is a bit small but it's growing!!

I hope that this will be the first of many issues. It's been a slow start but I think things should go a little more smoothly from now on. My schedule (work full time and go to school) has been pretty hectic lately, so this issue is late. Sorry 'bout that.

Even though all but three articles were written by me, please don't get the impression that this is a magazine dedicated to the genius that is me ;) The only reason so many articles are by me is because the other people on the staff haven't had enough time. Next month I hope there will be more articles by other people.

Tell us what you think. Feel free to send us your questions and opinions. Maybe we'll put your letter in the next issue.

Thanks!!

John Corigliano
