Tips

NOTE: If you have any hints and tips on the latest games then don't hesitate to send them too me.

Castle Kingdoms
Mutation Software
Heres an excellent tip for Mutation Softwares last Amiga release. If you pause the during play and press the keys A, R ,C and press the left mouse button and the fire button on your joystick all at the same time (You do have three hands, yes?) you will be able to skip the levels by using the function keys.

Minskies - The Abduction
Binary Emotions
At the very level called 'Boo Cake' type SPIRAL, the screen should now flash. The level skip cheat will now be active and you will be able to play any level at any setting. Also try typying 'FOREVER' instead and you will have infinitive credits
Champ. Manager 93/94
Domark
Select New Game, select Tranmere and Arrogant. Call yourself 'Mr Bulgaria' and you will have £34 million at your disposale.
Burnout
Vulcan Software
On the options screen hold down the left mouse button (Or joystick fire button 1) and press the following keys to activate the cheats:-

F1- Gives 10000 for a head to head instead of 5000.

F2- Activates the slide mode, in which none of the cars have any friction.

F3- Stops the somputer cars using there weapons.

F4- Turns off all the bonus stages.

F10- Turns off all the cheats and resets everything to normal

Cannon Fodder 2
Sensible Software
When you save a game, type 'JOOLS'. Cheat mode active will flash. The ranks of your current soldiers will now be increased.
