Collins Encyclopedia Galactica.

I meant to bring you this information last week but it unfortunatly it slipped my mind, anyway it's here now. =) (Shall I claim an exclusive on it like other electronic magazines do even though it was in another magazine much earlier, naaa..(No names mentioned of course) :)

The Collins Encyclopedia Galactica, quite an interesting name, huh? Well, it is the official encyclopedia for the Explorer 2260 game for the Amiga written by Chris Page / Digital Anarchy Software. It details the Explorer universe giving info races, planets, ships and weapons as well as other information on the mining and the military.

When finished the Encyclopedia will be bundled on the CD along with the game when it's released. At the moment it is at quite an early stage with new information being added to it all of the time. It also contains a diary of the game, which is also at quite an early stage. This will show the development of the game including screenshots of the game and the development tools that are being used for the game.
