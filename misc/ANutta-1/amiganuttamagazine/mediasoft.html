MediaSoft Release New Amiga Magazines.

Below is a press release that was sent to me, it would seem that MediaSoft is continuing its support for the Amiga with a new magazine devoted completly to games. This can only be good news as the Amiga needs all the publicity it can get at the moment....

    --MediaSoft Magazines release two new Amiga magazines--

    MediaSoft Magazines, publishers of UK based, mail order only Amiga paper magazine, Amiga Review, have split the magazine into two individual magazines.

    The original Amiga Review has now been halved in price to 1.60 and is being geared towards only serious aspects of the Amiga market.

    Meanwhile, the second magazine, which has been born out of Amiga Review, is dedicated entirely to the games section of the Amiga market. It is called Amiga Gamer and is priced at 1.50.

    Existing subscribers to the original Amiga Review will receive both magazines.

    This `divorce' has been brought about due to the great demand that exists for individual games and serious aspect magazines, and shows our continued commitment to the Amiga market.
