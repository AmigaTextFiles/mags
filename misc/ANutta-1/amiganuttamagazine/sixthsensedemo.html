Sixth Sense Investigations Demo Out Now...

Yes, thats right, the Sixth Sense Investigations demo has finally been released. It is on the September issue of CUAmiga CD.

The game which is being published by Epic Martketing is due out very soon on CD-ROM only.

Since it is currently only available on the CU-CD you'll have to buy this magazine to try it out. I can, however, if you want send the demo to you via EMail or over IRC if you see me. (Please note: The demo is about 1.8Mb archived.)
