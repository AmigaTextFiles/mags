Hello,

Welcome to the first issue of the Amiga Nutta Games Magazine.

Hopefully you will find this issue interesting. The aim of this magazine is to bring you the latest Amiga games news. The print magazines try, but they are always a month or so behind the latest news.

News this issue includes some info on Championship Manager 2, news on Sadeness signing OnEscapee, Fubar, Sixth Sense news and much more.

As this is the first issue I don't have any letters and am also a bit short on cheats. If you have anything to say about the magazine or have any questions that gove me a shout and I will put it on the letter page. Also if you have any cheats for recent then please do send them to me.

David Flaherty
Amiga Nutta
