Sixth Sense Investigations Out Soon...

I have just received news that Sixth Sense Investigations will be released by Epic Marketing in Late August.

The excellent looking graphic adventure which is based on the Monkey Island series will be available on a CD only format and will cost £29.99.

The game contains AGA 256 colour graphics and full character voice overs. The game itself takes place over 3 worlds with 32 locations.

The game will run on any AGA Amiga with 2Mb Chip RAM and a CD-ROM drive.

On another note, which will be of more interest to UK readers, Epic now have Flyin' High in stock. For more information call +44 (0) 1793 432 176.
