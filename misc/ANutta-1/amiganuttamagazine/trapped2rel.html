Release Draws Closer

Trapped 2 is now finished and is due for release in September.

This excellent looking RPG is the follow-up to Trapped, it has however been improved in many ways.

This game contains some excellent light effects and boasts many other features such as speels, polygon monsters and much more.

There will hopefully be a new demo version available on Aminet soon
