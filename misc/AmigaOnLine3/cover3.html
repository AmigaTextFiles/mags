Editoriale in italiano English Editorial



nother year has passed for us and Amiga, the future of our beloved computer is once again uncertain. VISCorp didn't buy Amiga Technologies until now and the future of projects like the set top box and the new desktop Amiga is not sure. From Germany there are at least some good news infact Phase 5 continues developing the Power up project and the A/Box. 1997 will be the year of the definitive relaunch of Amiga or the year of his death? Surely the new year will see the birth of the Power Amiga with the Power Pc board and the CyberVision 64 3D.
The only thing we can do is wait and see, or ( and that would be better ) continue ( or begin ) supporting our computer creating new software and spreading worldwide his qualities. Happy new year from...


Nicola Morocutti
