The game of the year has come and it's fantastic. WORMS : TDC the follow up to the best-selling WORMS is the Xmas present that Andy Davidson and Team 17 decided to give us, and belive me it's the best present I've received from ages! On the glorious number 1 we previewed a game called WORMS : The Next Generation saying that it was brilliant, now the game has arrived and apart the name nothing has changed from the preview so the game remain FANTASTIC. I think that every amigans know what is WORMS, so I will not tell you that 2 or more teams fight together trying to defeat the other team(s), and also I will not tell you that WORMS is one of the best games ever for its superb playability. WORMS:TDC has all the features of the first WORMS but now there are also 300 colours on screen, dozen of new weapons ( from Super Sheep to Old Women ), a lot of new scenarios and the new Graffiti mode to create your own levels without using Paint programs. The playability is out of this world, the gfx enhancements are unbelievable, the sound is still fantastic, well WORMS : TDC is THE BEST GAME I'VE EVER SEEN not only on Amiga but on all computers or console. A MUST for every owner of an A1200/4000.
