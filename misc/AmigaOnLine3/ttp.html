Tiny Troops

This game was announced a year ago by Mindscape for A500, A1200 and CD³², but we never saw it, now Vulcan claims that it will be avaible for the end of 1996 only for A500. Tiny Troops is a cross between Cannon Fodder, Dune II and Lemmings in wich your task is to complete a lot of mission killing enemies, defending your base, killing enemies, taking over your opponent's base and killing enemies, you will also have to decide wich types of Troops to take into each level. Graphic is colorful and well drawn sound is good and playability is expected to be very addictive. Tiny Troops could became a classic only if missions will be well calibrated in difficulty. A review is expected for next month.
