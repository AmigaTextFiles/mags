Subject ........: Delitracker dead for good?

Hi folks!

It seems that DeliTracker has bit the dust due to the fact that people think
it's funny to test out ReSource and then sell it as own product - Eagleplayer
had done this (it seems they don't do that anymore), Aplayer still does.
Well, I think it's pretty lame to copy other people's stuff.

Today in #amigager, we had some ideas like sending another load of money to the
authors as little incentive to coninue work on DeliTracker. Maybe if everybody
who registered for DeliTracker would send another 20 DEM/20 US-$, or maybe some
chocolate (a BBS coder in here has some esteem for Swiss chocolate...), or if we
have anybody good at producing Indian/Tamil pastry, we could have some of those
damn Tamil sweets that ruins my weight limit - quite a few people will probably
have some fancy idea...it doesn't have to be money, but something that makes
the authors rethink their position.

Also, if can make rethink Frank and Peter their decision, we'll probably have
wait for some real shareware, where you can only get those fancy new S3m players
and other goodies after you've paid the fee - no cripple version, just something
individual, probably sent via encrypted email.

Ideas, suggestions anyone?
