Sensible World of Soccer '96/'97

SWOS is one of the best games ever written for Amiga, its fantastic playability made it a must for every amigans. Now Sensible Software bring us SWOS '96/'97 another sequel of SWOS. It's vital to point out that between this one and the version before there isn't any change (except for the updated teams). So, in short, you MUST buy SWOS '96/'97 if you haven't any of its predecessor, but if you already own another title in the SWOS series download from Aminet the excellent SWOSEd and update the teams without having to pay nothing.

Please note that the global refers to who haven't got any other SWOS, for other people subtract 10 points!
