More information on the upcoming PowerPC-oriented computer show

For those of you who may be interested in the upcoming PowerCon '97 show, dedicated to PowerPC-based computer systems, you might find this latest information interesting. It has intentionally been left unedited.

PowerCon Press Release

Here is the latest news concerning the PowerCon'97 (July 19-20, Montreal). For all other info. and the latest news you can visit our Web sites at http://www.moebius.qc.ca/PowerCon97/ (it is now fully operational!)

PowerCon'97 NEWS UPDATE (97/04/14)

----------------------------------
  • CUBE the first Be User Group in Montreal, will be present in the user section to show several aspect of the BeBox/BeOS!
  • GfxBase a resellers of Amiga products will demonstrate the PowerUP boards from Phase5.
  • BeatWare a company developping BeOS software will present their BeSuite (Reggae Paint/Draw/Write). And also give a seminar on how to devellop application with AppSketcher.
