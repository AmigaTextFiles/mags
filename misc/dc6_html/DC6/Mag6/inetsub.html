Get DoubleClick by Email!

We now present two new services for those with email addresses (and if you can get regular Internet access, but don't have an email, then just apply to Hotmail!)

For the first service, we send you each issue as it is completed by email. Just email Mark on mdwh2@cam.ac.uk, requesting to be sent each issue, and please state whether you want the HTML or Amigaguide version.

But we realise that not everyone wants 700k emails cluttering up their inbox! Maybe you would rather download at a later date, or perhaps you would like to view the issue online. In this case, it is still convenient to know when a new issue is ready. With this second service, we will send you a quick email telling you when each new issue is produced. Just email Mark on mdwh2@cam.ac.uk asking us to do this.
