Getting Hold of DoubleClick!

DoubleClick is available from various sources now...

  • Aminet - both html and amigaguide versions will be uploaded to docs/mags as soon as possible, under the name 'DCx_Guide.lha' and 'DCx_HTML.lha', where x is the issue number, so check those recent lists! The html versions will from now on need a web browser (but just download a freeware one). Also, the amigaguide version on aminet requires you to decompress onto hard disk; if you only have a floppy drive, then get the disk version.
  • Subscription - the best way for readers without internet access. Again, from now on the full/html version really requires a web browser, but there's also the guide version needing just WB2.
  • Transmission Impossible BBS - DMS files of both versions will be available here!
  • CD ROM - It ought to find its way onto the Aminet CDs of course, and we may send them in for AF/CU CoverCDs, but this takes time, and the issues will be out of date by a few months at least.
  • PD Libraries - of course may get hold of it. In particular, Stephen will probably send copies to Amicom PD.
  • Other People - Remember that DC is completely freeware, so feel free to copy it to all your contacts, and spread it everywhere!
