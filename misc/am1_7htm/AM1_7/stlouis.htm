The Gateway Computer Show - Amiga 97

A major upcoming event in St. Louis

By Michael Webb, Editor-in-Chief, MikeWebb@CompuServe.COM
"Oh, take me to St. Lou-eee!"

In St. Louis, Missouri, USA, on the weekend of March 15-16, 1997, a major Amiga event will take place. Companies and organizations that will be present include QuikPak, Oregon Research, Silent Paw Productions, Nova Design, Intangible Assets Manufacturing (IAM), SoftLogik, Wonder Computers, Compuquick Media Center, and Amiga Report Magazine.

Admission Tickets are $10 per day or $15 for both days if bought in advance, and can be obtained from the Gateway Amiga Club at the following address:

Gateway Amiga Club, Inc
c/o Amiga 97
P.O. Box 811
Bridgeton, MO 63044

This should prove to be an interesting event for anybody who can attend.

For more information, see http://www.cucug.org/gateway97.html.
