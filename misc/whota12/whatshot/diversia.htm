Diversia Screenshots

I have obtained the latest screenshots in Low-Res and Hi-Res, which are available here for viewing, which shows that Diversia does look pretty good.


As mentioned in the Command & Conquer Clones article from Issue 1, the game will feature 256 colour graphics, be viewable in a number of different screenmodes, and also supporting Network play.

It can be seen from the screenshots that Diversia has some very colourful and are extremelly detailed and the explosions are nothing short of brilliant.

The game itself will require at least an 030 with 4Mb Fast RAM, at least a 4x CD- ROM drive. The game is planned to support PowerPC processors and already supports GFX boards.

The game is just over 50% complete, with the demo about 97% complete. So, in the meantime, enjoy the screenshots.

More information about Diversia and Ablaze's other Amiga games, can be found at:- Ablaze Entertainment.
