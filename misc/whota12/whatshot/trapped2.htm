Trapped 2

Trapped 2, an 3D-role playing game described as a Doom clone is now available. According to the developers, Oxyron Software, the full game features a full 3D world with 15 levels containing sloping floors and stairs.

There are weapons like swords, daggers and plenty more to defeat anything in your path. You also have several shot weapons such as ice storms and fireballs and there are over 20 spells you can cast.

Special effects in Trapped 2 include fire routines, mirrors and lens flares which makes this game sound pretty impressive.
