New Starfighter Standard

Starfighter an space action game from developers GeoSync Media has gone through major changes with a new graphics standard. Andy Hawkins of GeoSync Media has annnounced that this will be the new graphics standard for all it's titles.

The demo from their web site includes Battleships, Cruisers and tonnes more of which you can destroy or protect if they are yours. This game has been well designed and thought out with the gameplay that really makes a good game. The new graphics will definitely add to this excellent Shareware game.

According to GeoSync Media we should also look for more games coming from them in 1997 and into 1998.
