The Child Of Darkness

News has just come in on a game called the Child Of Darkness. The Child Of Darkness is a new top-down RPG game with great graphics and full Manga style animated film sequences in development by Desktop Corruption, a group of dedicated Amiga coders spread arounf the world (UK, USA, Sweden) who have come together on the internet to form a new coding group.

The Child of Darkness mixes the old Zelda type game to modern Beat-em-ups with good old RPG characters with speech and puzzles to provide a very interesting mix.

Its characters features are controlled by sophisticated AI routines, so the game will always play differently, even if you completed it once before. It runs along a detailed story with each level viewed as a chapter of which you can read the prologue, the script for the animated introduction in the game.

At the moment, the requirements aren't fully available yet, but it will support AGA.
