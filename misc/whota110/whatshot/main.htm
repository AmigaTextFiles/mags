Hello and welcome to the tenth issue of What's Hot On The Amiga Magazine, which you can see will be appearing about once every two weeks and now there is an on-line edition available.

The survey is here and ready for to fill in. I've decided to split this new survey into two parts, one for what the next generation Amiga should be like in terms of hardware, interfaces, etc and the other for what you think Workbench 4.0 should be like. This survey will be in the next copule of issues of whota and will also be available on the web site. So, fill it in and have your say.

I'm also looking for nominations for the What's Hot On The Amiga Magazine's Site Of The Month, so if you know of a site that might be worthy of the title, inform me at my usual e-mail address.
