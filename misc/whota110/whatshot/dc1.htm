Desktop Corruption Want Graphic Artists

Desktop Corruption who are currently developing Dafel:Bloodline which mixes the old Zelda type game to modern Beat-em-ups with good old RPG characters, speech puzzles, etc; are now looking for Graphics Artists to help them.

More details are given below on this great opportunity:-

WANTED! GRAPHIC ARTISTS TO JOIN UP AND COMING CODING GROUP!

Desktop Corruption are looking for dedicated graphic artists to join our group. You will be working under contract from Sadeness Software to work on our Dafel:Bloodline project. Since you will be working for Sadeness, there is the possibility of more projects to work on in the future besides Bloodline, however, you will primarily be working on our current project.

Your jobs will include:

1) Designing map blocks for various levels such as snow, forest, swap, volcano, desert etc.
2) Character animations for walking, fighting, various "doing" actions, for our main character Dafel and various enemy shapes such as, goblins, orcs, trolls, humans etc.
3) Designing shapes for items, switches, objects etc.

We are a friendly group :-) who work mainly off the internet. Our team members are based in the UK, Ireland, Sweden, Norway, USA and elsewhere. You don't need an internet connection but it does help.

This is a once in a lifetime opportunity to work in the computer game industry on a game that will change the way people play RPGs on the AMIGA!
