Pulsator - New Screenshots

As I have told you before, Pulsator is an excellent looking shoot-em-up coming from FullSpeed Developments. I now have some new screenshots of the game, and very good they look to.

The game boasts many feature such as over 512 colours on screen at once and 50 frames a second scrolling. It also boasts a massive weapons system that will put all the other games to shame. It isn't decided how many levels there will be in the game yet, but one thing I can tell you is that each level will contain lots of technical effects such as parralax scrolling, rain effects, fog and some excellent light effects.

There will be two versions of Pulsator, one on disk (Which will require a Hard-Drive) and also a CD-ROM version. The CD version will contain extras such as animated movie sequences, extra levels and music played directly from the CD.

Both versions will be released in January next year with a demo coming soon.
