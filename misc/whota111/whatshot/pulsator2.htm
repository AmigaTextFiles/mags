Pulsator Latest


Development on Pulsator, a shoot'em up is going well but it has been slowed down as they are currently seeking a Gfx Artist to increase development. If you can help them, their e-mail address is at the bottom.


Pulsator's list of features is expanding with it having three modes in which to play in, a 2 Player GUN Mode, 2 Player Classic Mode, and a 1 Player Mode. There will be 6 different Worlds with many levels in each. It'll have very big levels and hundreds of different Enemies to encounter along with big end of level Monsters. You'll have a big Weapon System to use against the various enemies you come accroos.

It will have up to 64000 colours on screen, with it running at about 50 FPS and have Parallax scrolling. There should special in game effects such as Water effects, Lightning and so on to create a atmosphere and build up tension. The Pulsator will have animated "Movie" sequences with 16 Bit CDDA Music and 4 Channel SFX.

Pulsator should be out in the summer with the help of GFX artist with it getting published by Islona. It will require an AGA Amiga, 4 MB Fast Ram, 2MB Chipram, 030 processor, Hard Drive, CD-Rom drive.
