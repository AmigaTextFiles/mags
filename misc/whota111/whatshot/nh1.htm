2260 Designs Seeks A Coder

The development team 2260 Designs who are developing New Horizons, a Space Station Management Simulation set in the The World Foundry Universe are looking for a coder to help them in it's development.

For more info, a Press Release is below:-

2260 Designs Seeks A Coder!

2260 Designs is looking for a talented programmer to join our team. The person must be experienced in system friendly utility/games programming, GFX card support, 3D coding and if possible TCP/IP expierience.

The person should be able to dedicate a good amount of time to the project, be self motivated, and willing / able to accept constructive critism. An example of any previous projects would be also a great advantage, although not absolutely required.

Duties will include doing the majority of the coding for our upcoming game, New Horizons. Another duty will be to co-ordinate a group of coders who will help.

Payment. This is always a sticky issue. Payment will be in the form of a percentage of royalties (please contact us for additional details). The accepted party will also become a member of the team and have a full say in the future plans of 2260 Designs.

Only dedicated people need apply, a lot of work lays ahead of us, and it is not for the meek of heart.
