Mutation Software Release Mutation Gold Compilation

Mutation Software have just released a Compilation of all of their releases last year along with two other classics they worked on in the past. Mutation Software's games have been well noted for having colourful cartoonly style graphics and gameplay.

The games released include Tommy Gun, a shoot'em up, Tin Toy, a platformer , Castle Kingdoms a bit like Gaunlet (on the Spectrum) along with classics, Doodlebug a cute platformer and Cyberpunk a space action adventure.

The Mutation Gold Compilation has been released as CD-Rom only and is a Limited Collectors Edition, priced at £19.99 UK Pounds. You have to pay an extra £2.00 UK pounds if sending from outside of the UK.
