Engine-X


Engine-X is a new system of that allows up to eight players play up against each other on a range of courses. Each course consists of randomly selected levels from different games. This has been described by the author as a "new concept" a bit like Games Master (TV show) or a kind of Gamers' Olympics.


There are six games altogether in Engine-X each influenced by various games such as Double dragon II, R type 1, Un squadron, Ikari Warriors, Forgotten Worlds, Dragon Ninja, Renegade, Smash TV, Gryzor and Midnight Resistance. Each one of these games has roughly the same format of progressing through a stage with middle bosses and a big boss at the end.


Engine-X requires an 060 processor, Hard Drive, and 4 MB Fast Ram. To play it with eight players you'll need two Amiga's connected through a serial cable and two four player adaptors along with joypads. At present it will take another several months of development with it requiring a Publisher.
