Homegrown Software Announces First Game Release, Moonbases

Homegrown Software are a new developer who have now made their mark on the Amiga Games scene with their announcement of an exciting new game influenced by Dune II, Command & Conquer and Warcraft II with their game called Moonbases.


Moonbases is a real-time strategy game which is nearing completion for a release in early 98 with a potential publisher already evaluating it. It features raytraced in game graphics along with music and sound effects including speech.


It's got a flexible game interface for controlling your forces and the enemy have artificial intelligence that provides plenty of challenge. But if you want even better challenging action you can have a 2 player link up via serial cable or modem. The one player action goes along the lines of a mission based game, there should be plenty.


Moonbases requires at least an AGA Amiga, with 1MB of Fast Ram and 5MB's of hard disk space.
