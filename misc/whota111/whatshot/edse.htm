Evil's Doom Special Edition


Evil's Doom is an Fantasy RPG from Olympia with the original version getting released as Shareware in 1996 but it really never hit the headlines of Amiga Magazines and the ones it did get into really liked it. Many Amiga owners never got the chance to get this game but now you have a second chance with Titan Computer snapping it up and planning to release it comercially in the summer with it also being a Special Edition.

The graphics as you can well see are highly detailed and are hand drawn with it having dozens of characters and locations. It will feature full motion video sequences along with a soundtrack to set the atmosphere.

It has a 256 color HighRes display and requires AGA or a graphics card, an 020 processor, 4MB Fast Ram, a double speed CD-Rom drive and at least 30MB's of hard disk space.

According to the publisher, Titan Computer a new demo will be released in the next few weeks and I will be looking forward to playing this demo for the last one was really entertaining.
