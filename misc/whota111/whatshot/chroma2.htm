Chroma Announces Specials on A1200 Towering Accessories

Chroma is pleased to announce the first in a regular series of monthly special offers available from the Chroma website.

"Mad March" specials include do-it-yourself (DIY) A1200 tower cases, A1200 tower keyboard adapters, Lola genlocks, CDR media and A1200 IDE interfaces.

A taste of things to come is available on the same page, where details of upcoming releases are included. The first new release will be the "T-I-Y", An A1200 Tower-It-Yourself kit consisting of a PC keyboard interface, an LED adapter and a conversion guide with cutting templates. It`s aimed at A1200 owners who wish to "towerize", but have access to their own full-size PC tower case.

Watch out for a complete redesign of the Chroma site over the coming week or so.
