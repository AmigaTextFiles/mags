User Group Network (UGN) to Host IRC Conferences From Amiga98

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - 11 March, 1998

Contact:
Beth Wise (inky@csrlink.net)
Wayne Hunt (wayne@amiga.org)

The Amiga User Group Network (UGN) is proud be bring you a series of IRC conferences directly from the floor of the Amiga '98 show in Saint Louis. The channel will be open during the show hours and there are activities planned for late afternoon/evenings.

Tentative activities include question and answer sessions with Amiga Inc's Darreck Lisle, Jeff Schindler, Joe Torre, and Amiga International's Petro Tyschtschenko (all appearances are based on availability and scheduling conflicts).
