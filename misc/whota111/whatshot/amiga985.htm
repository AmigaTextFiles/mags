Subject: Consignment Table at Amiga98 Computer Show

The Gateway Amiga Club Inc. is happy to announce that their Consignment Table is back by popular demand. Yes, we have been asked to run the consignment table at the Amiga98 Show in St. Louis this weekend. Here's how it works.

You bring in any original disks, manuuals and hardware, NO COPIES OF DISKS WILL BE ACCEPTED, and fill out a form. We will place them on the consignment table. At the end of Saturday or close of Sunday, you may pick up the unsold items at no charge. If they do sell, you only pay 10% of the price they sold for.

You can't beat a deal like that. Only 10% to sell your Amiga items and only if they are sold.

Please note that although there is security scheduled for the show, we will not assume responsibility for lost or stolen items.

See you at the show. The Gateway Computer Show-Amiga98
