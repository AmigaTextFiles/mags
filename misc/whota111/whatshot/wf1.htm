World Foundry Seeks 3D Graphics Artist

Development is continuing of Maim & Mangle with the developers Deimos Design / The World Foundry seeking a talented 3D graphics artist to join their team and help out.

For more details a Press Release is below:-

The World Foundry Seeks A 3D Graphic Artist!

Deimos Design / The World Foundry is looking for a talented 3D graphics artist to join our team. The person must be an experienced 3D artist, and have a good sense of tasteful colour usage, lighting, etc..

The person should be able to dedicate a good amount of time to the project, be self motivated, and willing / able to accept constructive critism. An example of your work would be also a great advantage, although not absolutely required.

Duties will include creating various textures (landscape, vehicles, structures, people, etc.) objects, etc. for our upcoming game, Maim & Mangle. The ability to work in full 24bit colour is a plus (all graphics will be rendered down to 8bit / 16bit colour sets for the final version).

Tools of the trade should include a paint package, and a rendering / modeling package of some sort. The ability to load/save or convert to LW objects is desirable, but again, not a mandatory requirement.

Payment. This is always a sticky issue. Payment will be in the form of a full share of all royalties earned (please contact us for additional details). The accepted party will also become a member of the team and have a full say in the future plans of the Deimos Design team.

Only dedicated people need apply, a lot of work lays ahead of us, and it is not for the meek of heart, or visit our websites.
