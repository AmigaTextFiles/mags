Great Nations

Great Nations is a strategy game in the style of Civilisation which is under development for both the PC and Amiga platforms. It's a multiplayer game so you can play other human players on the same computer or playing across a network, something many Amiga owners have always wanted in Civilisation is now being done for Great Nations.



It will require AGA or a graphics card, 020 processor or higher, with at least 6MB of Fast Ram. A GFX card is highly recommended.


At present a demo has just been released and I would suggest you try it out. The game is at the moment planned to be released as Shareware.
