Claws Of The Devil

Claws of the Devil is the first Tomb Raider style game for the Amiga with it having horror-adventure parts which follows the adventures of Teri Fletcher. Titan Computers are planning to release it sometime in the later half of 98.

Details are light but it'll have a real 3D engine with polygon bad guys and heroine, and realtime calculation of light sources to create realistic scenes.

You'll need a 040 processor, 8MB Ram, AGA, Hard Disk, CD-Rom drive, but they recommend you have a PPC board which will please all those who have one.
