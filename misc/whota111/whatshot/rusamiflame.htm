Russian Mirror Of Amiga Flame Now Opened

Amiga Flame has Mirrors in the US, Italy with the Base site being in the UK, but now with the help of Amiga World in Russia - a Russian Amiga Flame Mirror of the UK Base site is up.

This will allow Russian Amiga Users easier and faster access to the Amiga Flame web site along with Amiga users in countries close to Russia. Thanks must go to Alexey Emelyanov of Amiga World who have kindly provided this Mirror and it is good to see that there are even Amiga users in Russia.
