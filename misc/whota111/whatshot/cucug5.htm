March 6, 1998 - If you are not yet a member of CUCUG time is running out for your chances to win your very own copy of the absolutely fabulous, graphically rich, tremendously popular game Myst published by clickBOOM.

Five copies have already been awarded to lucky CUCUG members and another five will be chosen on March 19th.

"We are very excited to be able to give away ten free copies of what is probably the most popular title available on the Amiga at this time," said Kevin Hisel, CUCUG's web master.

Hisel stated, "We really want to thank clickBOOM and all of the other Amiga companies which have supported our membership with their generous donations."

To join CUCUG, Amiga users are invited to use the membership form found at their web site at http://www.cucug.org/supporta wd98.html . CUCUG membership is only $20 and is good through December. The club offers many other benefits to members. Details are available at that web address.
