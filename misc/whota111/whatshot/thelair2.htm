The Lair Announces Latest Issue

Dear Fellow Amigans,

We here at The Lair are proud to announce the release of issue 12, which is now online, at http://amigaworld.com/thelair/ .

This month, we have another issue packed with top reviews, articles and opinions concerning recent developments, releases etc. in the Amiga community.

In this issue, we have an interview with Fleecy Moss of the ICOA, and an article on the Amiga Formula One scene. We also have a Javascript tutorial, and you can contribute your ideas to the OS3.5 wishlist initiative. The Aminet dig and forum pages are all there, as is Gatebert.

This months' issue also boasts a completely new interface featuring frames, and we are also delighted to be able to announce a completely new service: The Lairs' very own message board, where you can let off steam about the mag, or any Amiga-related issues. It can be accessed through The Lairs' pages, at the address below. Please read the instructions and FAQ before posting to the board. We hope this new board will become a fun way to interact with The Lair, and other Amiga users across the globe.

In short, we have another great issue for everyone to read this month- as always, absolutely no subscription is necessary.

Best wishes to you all,
