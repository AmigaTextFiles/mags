UCM is Coming...

Those of you who are heavily into the PD scene, and also buy Amiga Format CD edition regularly, will no doubt have heard of Bernard Cain's B-Card, Business Card Creator. Well, this is about to get some competition from us here at Trogladite Software.

UCM (Ultimate Card Maker) will feature things which are not available in B-Card such as multi language support, 16 colours and not 8, print one card, or tiled on the page, 500 level undo, place text anywhere on the page, along with boxes, lines, and other shapes. You can use any font on your computer and there are many more features besides!

UCM has a friendlier interface which imitates that of Workbench, and there is another unique feature which will really aid the creation of cards, and that is the 'Wizard' server tool built into UCM. This allows people to create a simple script and UCM can run this, and users of UCM can usee the wizard script to create cards simply by typing things in which the wizard can use!

There are many example cards available, and lots of wizard scripts too! We are looking for Beta testers, and the best source of information is the UCM webpage, which you can find at:
