Doubleclick! Issue 8 - What to expect


Well, by the time DC8 comes out, hopefully there should already be some new Amigas hitting the shops. We will have some news concerning all that there can ever be to do with the Amiga, plus the usual mixed bag of News, Reviews, Hints & Tips, plus of course, those all important Quizzes!!

Also, read the report of the WOP, the biggest event of the year. In case you thought I was being racist, WOP is the World Of Pentrisoft show, which got its first showing sometime in August 1998. I know that DC7 will be released sometime after the event, but we will all have stinging hangovers, so will not be able to type. Coupled with this, we have been run off of our feet trying to write this issue! In case you wonder what we mean - two of Pentrisoft's more active members (Mark and myself) will be meeting up for the biggest party and boozefest since England won the World Cup (and since Scotland got knocked out - again - just to show I am not bigoted!).

Doubleclick! Issue 8 will be released (provisionally) sometime mid to late 1999...

See you there!!.

Stephen Graham
