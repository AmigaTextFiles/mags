Hi,

This is the third editorial for the What's Hot On The Amiga Magazine, and a lot has happened again since the release of the second issue, as an on-line version of What's Hot On The Amiga Magazine is now available at http://www.koyote.com/ users/jbb/whota/main.htm and many thanks for the web space which was donated by BCo. Hopefully, this site will be updated at least once a week.

This issue contains a lot of articles on the Phase 5 and the Haage & Partner WarpUp dispute which has dragged on for almost all of this October. But, there is a still of other news out there, with the collection of articles split more or less evenly by game news and the more serious stuff. But, in this issue, there are a couple of exclusives such as the Latest On Explorer, Nova Sector Engineering's New Amiga Compatible Computers, Foundation Screenshots and YAM 2.0 articles.

A number of new sites have made their way into Links, since the last issue and are all worth a look.

Now this month's, Site Of The Month is Amiga Flame, who have just recently move their UK mirror to a new server and in the process, Amiga Flame has undergone a number of changes. Amiga Flame is one of the best site for finding the latest news on Amiga games and is definitely one site to keep going to on a regular basis.

In the next issue, there will be a number of articles on more games, along with other stuff, and maybe something on the MidWest Amiga Exposition.
