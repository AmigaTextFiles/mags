Shadow Of The Third Moon Released

The Amiga's first Voxel engine Flight Simulation, Shadow of the Third Moon has been released by Titan Computers.

It is like a mix between a Comanche-like flight simulator and will have a mission system similar to Xwing. The full game contains 6 different campaigns with up to 48 missions feauring realistic fog, smoke, shadow and water effects and most important of all, Action.

Shadow of the Third Moon is CD-Rom only and will require at least 68EC30 and 4MB Fast, Cybergfx or AGA, CD-Rom and HD space of around 16MB.
