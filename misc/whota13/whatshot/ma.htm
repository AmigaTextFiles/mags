Midwest Amiga Exposition Just Days Away

The date is fast approaching for the 2nd annual Midwest Amiga Exposition. If you're planning on being there and didn't get advanced tickets, no need to worry, we will have plenty at the door for $15.00 each. The tickets are good for both Saturday and Sunday, and kids 12 and under are admitted free!

The dates and times once again are: Saturday, Nov 1st 10:00am to 6:00pm, and Sunday 10:00am to 2:30pm. It's being held at the Concourse Hotel at the Port Columbus airport. For directions and schedule information, please visit our website.

Don't miss out on meeting Petro Tyschtschenko, President Amiga International, Representatives from Amiga Inc, and other great Amiga celebs like Dale Larson, Andy Finkel, Jason Compton, and many more.

Door prizes, hourly give-aways, the Amiga Developers Conference, educational seminars, and the great people who make the Amiga more than just a great computer, make this one of the most dynamic events of the year!

We have an impressive array of vendors and user groups, over twice as many as last year. Many newer Amiga companies will be exhibiting their wares along with a number of long-time Amiga supporters. For a complete list of exhibitors, visit our website.

Since our announcement of this year's show, the response from the Amiga community has been amazing! We've been flooded with requests and questions about the event, but more than anything, we've seen positive and dedicated response for supporting this event from vendors to individuals.

Many, many thanks go to the Amiga community for their help, support, and upcoming attendance of our show.

We look forward to seeing each and every one of you there!
