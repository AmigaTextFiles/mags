Construction


Construction is a new game from a programming team called 'The Hidden'. They have been around on the Amiga scene for a very long time developing games such as Donk and were working on The Big End and The Hidden Golf to name just two. Recently, however, they have been very quiet and nothing has been heard about these or any of there other games. However, things have now changed and I have been told that they are still writing a number of games for the Amiga, of which Construction is one.

Construction a platform game (something which we don't get much of these days) with some very colourful and well drawn graphics. The game contains a very big puzzle element. You take the part of two builders (more on this later) called Sharkey and Snork who have to build urrmm, buildings :). The aim of each level is to complete a certain percentage of the building without getting killed.

The gameplay is kinda similar to tetris in a way. Whilst dodging the nasties on each level you have to finish off the building, for each part you do you get cash, however, everytime you get hit by the nasties you loose some cash, all you have to do it break even at the end of the level, if you don't then you have to play it again. It all sounds kinda complicated but when you understand it, it is a very nice idea.



Going back to the mention of taking the part of two builders, well, this is where the fun begins. The game is designed as a two player game with each player taking the part of one builder, it takes real teamwork to complete each of the levels. However, one player can play by thereself as the other player will run using its own unique personality controlled by the computer.

The game will run on any ECS Amiga With at least 1Mb Chip RAM. This may sound a bit low spec by todays standards, but don't let this fool you into thinking the game is not any good.
