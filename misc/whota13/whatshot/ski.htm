Skillo Interactive Developing Games For The Amiga

Skillo Interactive are a new Development Team formed by some University students in the UK who have two games in development.


The first is called Radioaction and is an Pseudo-3D blaster in the Super-Stardust mould, it's based around the frantic action tunnel bits. You will battle against the Alien menace through 11 varying levels, each with unique rendered GFX and enemies. The type of sections will include Nuclear Bunkers, Sunken Palaces, Hyperspace, and the final battle on the Alien's Homeworld.

The second game in development is called Martian Tales an Warcraft II inspired strategy game.

Details are light but it will feature Limited intelligence (eg. fear), the ability to mine the three substances found on Mars (Fungus, Lichen + Stone), 2 Sides with very different trooptypes - the evil Greys vs. the innocent Smeglings, different landscapes, many Building types, with Warcraft II inspired resource management and battles.



The screenshots of Martian Tales and Radioaction appears to show that they have included plenty of detail and they have said that they will be concentrating on what makes a game good - Gameplay. Currently they have no Publishers, so if you're in the business get in contact with them.
