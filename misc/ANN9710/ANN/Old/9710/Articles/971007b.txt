September 30th, 1997

               First native multitasing kernel for PPC-Amiga models


     Haage & Partner Computer GmbH, Germany, an innovator in Amiga software
     technology, is pleased to announce the release of WarpUP. WarpUP is a
     hardware independent PPC native multitasking kernel (EXEC). It is
     designed for use with PowerPC Amiga models. WarpUP currently supports
     the PowerUP(tm) boards of Phase5, and it is also upwardly compatible
     with future PowerPC based Amiga systems. As part of Haage and
     Partner's continued support of the Amiga Community, WarpUP is freely
     downloaded from their corporate website.
     More information can be found at: www.haage-partner.com
