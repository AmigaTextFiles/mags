
                        Amiga's now on sale.
                        ====================

Escom shops in the UK are to sell Amiga's and Amiga Tech assure all Amiga
users that the staff will have knowledge of the Amiga line.

The A1200 is to come in two main flavours,

Amiga Magic pack. £399
A1200 2meg Chip running Workbench 3.1
Wordworth V4 SE + Print Manager
Datastore V1.1
Organiser V1.1
TurboCalc V3.5
Personal Paint V6.4
Photogenics V1.2a SE
Pinball Mania
Whizz

There  will  also  be  a version with a 170meg Hard drive and Scala MM300
costing £499.

Now onto the A4000T, what a beast but what a price! £2,230
A4000T running Workbench 3.1
25Mhz 040
I Gig Hard drive
6 megs of ram
The standard software pack and Scala MM300

There  is  due  to be an 060 based A4000T by the end of November and also
there  is  due to be a new A1200 pack that might or might not include the
following, A1200 + CD-ROM and extra megs of ram.  Rumour has it at 4 megs
of ram a double speed CD-Rom and an 030 processor.

This   new   was  gathered  from  the  Amiga  Press  release  from  Amiga
Technologies UK.
