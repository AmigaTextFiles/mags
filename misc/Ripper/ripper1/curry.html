SPICY CURRY!

INGREDIENTS.

8oz OF CHICKEN OR PORK ETC..
2oz BUTTER OR MARGARINE
1 APPLE EATING OR COOKING
2 MEDIUM ONIONS
4 TEASPOONS OF HOT CURRY POWDER
1 TEASPOON GARLIC PASTE ( OPTIONAL)
6 LARGE MUSHROOMS
SALT AND BLACK PEPPER TO TASTE
MILK
CORNFLOUR (APPROX 5 TEASPOONS )
ONE AND A HALF PTS OF CHICKEN STOCK

METHOD.

IN A LARGE SAUCEPAN MELT THE BUTTER
TO THIS ADD THE MEAT AND COOK FOR 2 MINUTES
ADD ONE OF THE ONIONS ( ROUGHLY CHOPPED )
AND COOK FOR A FURTHER MINUTE
ADD THE CURRY POWDER AND STIR TO ENSURE THE
MEAT AND ONION IS COVERED
ADD THE CHICKEN STOCK ( OR HAM OR BEEF )
BRING TO THE BOIL AND THE SIMMER FOR AN HOUR
PEEL, CORE, AND DICE THE APPLE AND ADD IT TO
THE PAN
PEEL AND CHOP THE 2nd ONION COVER IT WITH
COLD WATER AND PLACE IT IN A FRIDGE
ADD SALT,PEPPER AND GARLIC PASTE TO THE CURRY
SLICE THE MUSHROOMS AND ADD THEM TO THE CURRY
COOK FOR A FURTHER 15 MINUTES
MIX THE CORNFLOUR WITH A LITTLE MILK TO FORM
A WEAK PASTE
REMOVE FROM THE HEAT, AND SLOWLY ADD THE
CORNFLOUR, STIRRING CONSTANTLY UNTIL THE
CURRY IS THICK AND CREAMY
IF DESIRED MORE CORNFLOUR AND MILK MIXTURE
CAN BE ADDED TO THICKEN THE CURRY FURTHER
TASTE AND ADD MORE SALT IF REQUIRED
JUST BEFORE SERVING, DRAIN THE CHILLED
ONION (WHICH WILL HAVE CRISPED UP IN THE
FRIDGE) AND ADD THEM TO THE CURRY,
STIR WELL AND SERVE. (SEE AUTHORS NOTES)

AUTHORS NOTES!

A QUICK AND EASY CURRY! ALMOST IDENTICAL
TO WHAT YOU WOULD GET IN A CHINESE RESTURANT
