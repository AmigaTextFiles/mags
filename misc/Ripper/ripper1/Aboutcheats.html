If you use this list as a source for your own list or print any part of this list in a newsletter(for a user group for example), I only ask that you credit this list and its editor (ME).

DISCLAIMER: There is no way I can guarantee that all these cheats WORK as written. A cheat/backdoor may work on the EUROPE release of a game and not the USA release and vice-versa. Cheats/backdoors may or may not work across versions/upgrades of the same game. They also may or may not work on OTHER computers unless specifically listed as such(for the Atari ST, as somebody asked me once.)
