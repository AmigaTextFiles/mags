New Screenshots Of The Game Formally Known As Alien F1

It's a F1 type racing game that caused a real impact when a demo of it appeared in Aminet, it used to be formally called Alien F1 but its real name will either end up as "Grand Prix Simulator" or "Virtua GP".

"Grand Prix Simulator" or "Virtua GP" - which do you prefer?

The author has been very busy, with the game now under heavy development after he was out doing other things for a year. The game currently runs at about 12-15 fps on a '030 25MHz Amiga, this is off course depending on the board and on the Fast Ram speed, and the author Paolo Cattani has said it simply Flies on '040/'060 Amiga's.

At present it doesn't have a publisher. Enjoy the latest screenshots for now.
