Caveman Species

Caveman Species is a real-time strategy wargame set in prehistoric time, under development by newly formed Black Flag whose capabilities seem to be outstanding if they continue creating graphics like the ones seen here. Black Flag aim to support the Amiga fully along with the next generation Amiga's in the future.

Black Flag's first game is Caveman Species, the story is based on small planet where intelligent lifeforms have emerged. Out of this is formed four tribes each fighting for territories including the conquest of the entire three continents where life has spread. Only the strongest will survive this evolutionary rage for power and destruction and so it's up to you to be the strongest or face defeat.

You'll have three different maps with over 90 territories to conquer and be able to build cities and control your armies in the battle field. It will also have several degrees of evolution to help expand the environment and more. The developers are considerating a two player mode with direct cable connection with the possibly of modem support too.

It'll feature full screen 256 colour hires in-game graphics with low resolution in battle sequences and tonnes of megabytes of pre-rendered 3D animations with incredible quality. Hundreds of highly detailed character animations are planned with lots of digitized sound effects and music in full stereo to set the atmosphere.

Caveman Species will require an 020 processor or better with it needing 4MB Fast Ram and is CD-Rom only. It should be released in Spring 1998 and I'll get you more info and screenshots when they come in.
