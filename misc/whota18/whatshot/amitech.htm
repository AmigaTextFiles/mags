AmiTech 97 An Amiga Event For Scandinavia

Scandinavian readers will be pleased to know that between the 3-5 of April 98 in Stockholm one of the biggest Amiga shows for Scandinavia will take place, AmiTech 98.

Petro Tyschtschenko will be in attendence along with companies and magazines such as Amiga Info, Svenska Amiga Magazine, Vidamus Multimedia, Tricom Data Vision AB, Micsam Data AB, Comprex AB with more expected to attend.

Language is not expected to be much of a problem, so if you speak English you may want to attend.
