Aurora Works Taking Orders

The release of Aurora Works first title, H-Bomb is imminent with Aurora Works now taking pre-orders. Anyone who orders early will get the limited special-edition version of H-Bomb, available only when stocks last so if your interested in the special version, then order now.

H-Bomb if you haven't already heard is a multi-player blast fest" with it being based on the hugely successful Bomberman series. It'll feature support of up to four players playing 10 uniquely constructed playfields were you will be given the opportunity to settle old rivalry and vendettas or just kill anything that moves. Each player will be able to pick there own four different characters, Firecracker, Mr Mean, Turbo, and Razorback.
It can display at 50fps 120bobs simultaneously with up two players getting the chance to play at once. It will work on a plain A1200 with it being released around March. Currently they're looking for a publisher for this game and a second game.



It will be CD-Rom only and have IP/TCP support for playing it over the Internet.
