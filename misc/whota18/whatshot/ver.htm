VeroSoft's Games



VeroSoft are a new German Games Publisher who have a number of titles lined up for release in 1998.

Wheels On Fire

Their first game is called Wheels On Fire which you may remember it was intended to be Shareware until VeroSoft snapped it up and will be releasing it commercially. It's a racing game with steep turns, high jumps, fixing and tuning of your car, different weather conditions, various landscapes with it also containing sound and speech. A new demo is available from their web site.

Powder

Their next games are called Powder which is a action shoot'em up and Beambender of which I know nothing more about at the moment.
