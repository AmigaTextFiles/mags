PIOS ONE News (07-Jan-97 )

    Apparently, the PIOS One will be not just be an inexpensive PPC machine. Dr. Peter Kittel says it has some expensive features in its I/O design, has a non-standard form factor motherboard and thus needs expensive, custom casing. Also it's not easy to upgrade, you need a motherboard swap. These are the issues which have been taken care of in the PIOS ONE design.
    He furthermore describes the PIOS ONE as quick, simple start, hinting that there may be a "TWO" later. He says that both PIOS and the users need something soon.
    The ONE is expandable at large. You can have everything what you get now from an Amiga accelerator card, plus everything from the PCI (and ISA) world, drivers being the only problem.
    He is also skeptical that Phase5's PowerUp program will be able to transform any A4000 into a generic PPC machine, he thinks that the PPC in that board will only act as coprocessor to the old 680x0.
     · Posted by Dr. Peter Kittel in comp.sys.amiga.misc.
