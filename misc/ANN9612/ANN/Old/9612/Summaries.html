Who is Hembach? (29-Dec-96 )

    Hembach is the official Escom liquidator, and as such he is responsible for all the delays. However, he can't be replaced or forced to hurry, because of German law. Such a liquidator is something like a small god, nobody can command him once he is chosen by the court.

No NT for PPC (21-Dec-96 )

Rick Parks deceased (16-Dec-96 )

    Famous Amiga artist Rick Parks died this morning. He was notably known for doing pictures that looked like raytraced or scanned, but that were hand-drawn in only few colours. About the most well-known of his creations was the 1989 Mickey Mouse glass.
