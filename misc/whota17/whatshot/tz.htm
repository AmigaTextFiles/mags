Trauma Zero

Trauma Zero is a new horizontal scrolling shoot'em up under development by a new Italian development team.



A preview of this title is available from Aminet and is well worth the download with it showing the game in action and some very nice intro animations along with some excellent rock music. It will have a two player mode and contain an array of weapons and enemies as seen in the various screenshots.
