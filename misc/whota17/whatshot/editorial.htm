Hi,

This issue's editorial and this issue of What's Hot On The Amiga Magazine, concentrates on Quake, with numerous articles appearing in this issue's News In Brief with some images thrown in, but there is still no word offically on it. Also Doom gets a mention in an article with a Amiga port of Doom now available as a beta version.

This issue exclusive articles are on Stratego and the Jay Miner Society.

Other than a couple of other articles, there isn't that much else in this issue, but I'd like to thank Alan Crandall for his pointing me in the direction of the JMS, which allowed me to put the article together and also a few new imagelinks have been added to the News Section. The promised article on PCI slots for the Amiga which was supposed to be in this issue will hopefully turn up in the next issue.

On the subject of the next issue, it is possible to maybe out a little later than usual, due to other commitments that I've got on in the 2nd and 3rd weeks of January, but there will be at least one issue out in January and that is for sure.

This will be the last issue in which the survey will be in, but will be on the web site, for a little while longer.
