Editor's Notes

Partly Cloudy

By Michael Webb, Publisher and Editor-in-Chief, See staff list for e-mail address

"Partly cloudy" has many possible meanings. First, let me say that I have been insanely busy lately. There has been almost no time to work on this magazine, which is why it's somewhat late. I must admit that my mood about having to throw this issue together quickly is cloudy at best.

"Partly cloudy" might also refer to the state of Amiga users' attitudes about the platform. Or about the future of developers. The list goes on.

But I'm going to keep things short for this month. I'll just leave you with a greeting from my new location, where I've put this column for the sake of streamlining the magazine.

We'll see you next month. Maybe we'll catch some pleasant weather for a change.
