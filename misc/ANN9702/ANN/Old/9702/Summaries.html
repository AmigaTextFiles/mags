The One, browsers and gfx cards (27-Feb-97 )

    The PIOS ONE will come with net stuff, actually the BeOS includes a good browser and other net software. PIOS will include one of the cards that is already available for the Be; you can find a list on www.be.com. According to Dr. Peter Kittel, the PIOS ONE is intended as computer for graphics, audio, animation applications, i.e. multimedia.

PC ports (27-Feb-97 )

    Andreas Kürzinger asked Blue Byte about Amiga versions of Albion and Battle Isle 2 and they said "yes". The idea is that an Amiga programmer does the port on its own (without financial support), but that he only receives money on the copies sold. This could also work for other companies.

Haynie statement about PCs (20-Feb-97 )

    According to Dave Haynie, PC's are not that bad. Basically, if you're having problems with the OS on a PC, IT'S THE OS, NOT THE HARDWARE. If AmigaOS came out for the x86 PC tomorrow, you'd love it.. He adds that in Windows, here is just too much "magic" stuff going on, and that it doesn't allow you as much control over the system as the Amiga does. He finishes the article with the words Microsoft wants you to be their dog..
