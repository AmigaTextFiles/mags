Bograts : the Puzzling Misadventure

Again Vulcan and again a Lemmings clone. This Bograts is a game with a classical concept but with a good tecnique behind. The task is to save little sons of Bog from 60 treacherous levels. Bograts is well programmed with very smooth animations for the characters and a good scrolling with parallax. Graphic apart the animation is good if you like Valhalla' style and sound is simple but effective. Playability is very good with 60 levels and a lot of ingenious puzzles. This is my favourite Lemmings clone, another good one from Vulcan.

Bograts
