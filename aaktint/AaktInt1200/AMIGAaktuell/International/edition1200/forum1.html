1. Contribution for discussions: Sales prices of iFusion (by Andreas Volkmann)

Well, believe it or not! I hadn't bothered for a while with what is going on with the Mac emulation and the PPC. But what I read now sounds like the joke of the century!

Who is going to pay 149.- Dollars for a pre-order? And even 245.- Dollars plus collect-on-delivery fee for the normal version? This indeed is more than usury. For that much money I could get a used Powerbook in an auction. It may not be the fastest, but it is an original laptop.


I would not buy an emulator for appr. 500.- DM only to be able to use a few PPC programs. The 604 on the Amiga boards is not the fastest and is not adequate for the new Mac programs. 
 

And you just know that later on they will state, again, that they were right to do nothing for the Amiga system because the number of sales was too low.

Andreas Volkmann <andreas-v@gmx.de>
