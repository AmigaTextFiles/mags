»Amiga Arena News
(04.11.2000)


Amiga Arena Special Price Deal
Apano Sin and Turbo Racer 3D

The Amiga Arena in co-operation with Alfred Knoetig (Alf's Corner) offers a new special price deal.

Apano Sin - The Classic Shooter may be ordered exclusively at a special price of 45 DM (appr. 23 Euro) in the Amiga Arena.

Apano Sin is a classic slot machine shooter, originally developed in 1993 for ECS machines. But development was cancelled and at last at the Amiga Fair in Neuss Alfred K. was able to present the Final Version, after he had taken over the development and the distribution. Apano Sin will run on any Amiga model and is available only on disk.

An installer is being prepared and will be available soon!


A Preview of Apano Sin is online! 
 
Turbo Racer 3D - The Voxel Racing Game Version 1.5 
  

The last 10 original CDs in a box of the racing game Turbo Racer 3D available for commercial sale may be ordered at Amiga Arena at 15 DM (appr. 8 Euro)!

Turbo Racer 3D is a racing game in the style of "Flyin High"! Turbo Racer 3D needs AGA Processor 68020-68060 with 4 MB Fast. These are the last ones in distribution. Original sales version 1.5.

More info in the "Actions" range!

An interview with Alfred K. and further info about future developments will follow!

The special prices are exclusively available in the Amiga Arena with the order form found there!«

Amiga Arena Special Price Deal

GeoWorld V1.12 can now may be ordered at a special price in the Amiga Arena. »Amiga Arena News
(11.11.2000)


Amiga Arena - Keeps The Flame Burning!

The Amiga Arena is pleased to be able to announce further deals taking place in the near future. The full version of "Das Blaue Auge II" ["The Blue Eye II"] and three more new special price deals are just the appetizers of what you may expect in the next few days.

In all those deals please don't forget that this is a "NON profit" homepage, which is handled by me single-handed without any advertisement. Therefore I want to say "Thank you" to all users that take advantage of the special prices and by doing so assist developers. On the other hand, the number of visitors and the number of orders coming to the developers bear no relation to each other!

Yours, Olaf K.

Amiga Full Version Action

The Amiga Arena presents the role-play "Das Blaue Auge - Die Schicksalssäge" in an unrestricted full version! Timo Kloss of Inutilis Software, presently developing the adventure "Ermentrud", has placed his role-play "Das Blaue Auge II" for distribution exclusively at Amiga Arena.

Amiga Arena Special Price Deal

In co-operation with Bernhard Lindner Amiga Arena offers a special price for T.U.R.D. II. T.U.R.D. II is the relais board control software for the Amiga! It allows control of an 8-port interface board at the parallel port, with or without Centronics protocol, and offers different control techniques: Listing, timing, music and random control, and of course manual inputs.«


»Amiga Arena News
(19.11.2000)


Amiga Arena - Special Price Deal!

In co-operation with Christian Effenberger Amiga Arena offers an exclusive special price for the shareware bundle Siteway 1.0. The registration fee is 30 DM (appr. 15 Euro) instead of 80 DM (appr. 40 Euro)!!

Siteway is a software package employing integrated site and content management. Siteway is reported to have following features:

  • Databank based Layout- and Content-Management.

  • Automatic creation of websites according to preselected rules and contents.

  • Automatic creation of navigation according to preselected rules.

  • Automatic conversion for webcontent file formats.

  • Global and local formats, rules and contents.

  • Precast templates and default classes.

  • Complex template editor.

  • Central and peripheral administration of all resources.

  • Preservation of the originals of all resources.

  • Complete project administration.

  • Intuitively operating GUI

  • Extension of functions with external programs via DOS and/or ARexx.

The program package is very large, additional infos in the Amiga Arena!«
