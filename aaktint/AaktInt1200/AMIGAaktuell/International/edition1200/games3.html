3. Foreword

Dear readers,

This is the AMIGA aktuell international games section of December in the year 2000. 2 Years ago I took over the games section, most of you might remember, from Jens Schröder who had´n't enough time for it. Although I thought I would always have the time for the games section, three month ago everything changed and so I have to end my work as chief editor. So this is my last games section.

Someone should take over the games section because it has been a part of AMIGA aktuell international for a long time and might enjoy wide popularity. Anybody who is prepared to do the job should send a mail to Carsten. Please read also the official announcement in the foreword of this AMIGA aktuell international.


Last of all I would like to thank all who joined the AMIGA aktuell games section. 
A lot of people should be mentioned but I will only name some of them: 
Andreas Koch for the Mac corner, Manni Froitzheim for his Napalm test, Heiko Garelts for the PD tests and also Malte Mende for the PD tests, who hopefully will continue the tests. And of course Carsten Schröder who often had to wait for the games section and then had to change the format ´cause I am fussy in the choice of my texteditor. 
 

So, that´'s it for this year and for the games section.


I wish you a merry Christmas and a happy new year and I hope that there will be someone taking over the games section as soon as possible so that there will be no break. 
 

All the best!
