First, the meaning of the lines:

(A) - Ranking 
(B) - Ranking last month 
(C) - Program-name 
(D) - Version number (not in the CD-ROMs Charts) 
(E) - A source, where you can get the program. DFUE and Aminet will 
 only be stated if there's no disk or cd-rom known that contains 
 the program. By the FD CD-ROMs charts the name of the distributor 
 is posted. 
(F) - Points, the program (or series) has reached. The first rank gets 5 
 the following ones one less each. 
  

About the sequence: All ranks are ordered by points. If the points are equal, the number of single votes decides. If this is impossible the alphabet will do, or I for myself :)

How to participate on the FD charts ?

The FD charts appears with every AMIGA aktuell. Placements have to be with me by the 24th. Later arriving mails will be posted within the next edition.

Simply post a mail, letter or whatever you want to my postal address:

It's also possible to vote online on the AMIGA-aktuell URL in the www. You should post your address and vote in 3 or more categories.

If these are not part of the Top 10, I need some further information: If possible, please post the names of the authors, where you got the program and the latest version number.

To get a vote for the raffle, you'll have to give your voting in 3 or more of the categories. The winners and prizes will be announced within the AMIGA-aktuell lottery.
