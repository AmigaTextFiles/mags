Games section

4. Games news from all over the world

  • ACSYS publishing unsure

Christian Sobotta of the ACSYS developer team announced that only 50 have said they would buy ACSYS when 500 persons were needed. Christian pointed out that it is not a preorder but a statement of interest in the game. ACSYS is a very good Turrican clone, tested in the last edition of AMIGA aktuell international.

  • Hyperion Software announced that they will make more PC games playable on the Amiga

Nearly every month there is an announcement by Hyperion Software and so it is this month, too. This time it is the PC game Majesty that Hyperion are bringing to the Amiga, in the first quarter of 2001. It will run on a PPC Amiga with WarpOS, 48 MB RAM and a graphic board. A version for the 68060 is unsure at this stage. Hyperion said they would not have a representative at the World of Amiga fair but they would show several games.

  • Bubble Heroes for graphic boards

On the internet pages of ArcadiA Products is a patch to let Bubble Heroes use graphic boards. The patch will run on all systems and is recommended to graphic card owners. 
 
  • Payback demoversion

This month Apex Designs has published a playable demoversion of Payback exclusively for game magazines. You can read a preview in this edition.

  • Exodus - The Last War

As Amiga-News.de reported, the developer team of Exodus told a contributor that there will be a PPC version of Exodus - The Last War. 
 
  • SpaceStation 3000 stopped

Many have supposed or suspected what Digital Images have finally announced: 
SpaceStation 3000 will not be published. Reasons mentioned were mistakes in co-ordinating the project and difficulties with the developer team starting the project. 
In future, Digital Images will concentrate on the AmigaOne. 
 
  • New games at AmigaLand

AmigaLand has secured the licence to publish more commercial Amiga games on their homepage. These are Hanse - the expedition AGA, Caribbean Disaster, Whale's Voyage and The Clou! the Profi-Disk. You can find AmigaLand at http://www.vivaline.de/matt/ .

  • HighAnt Development leaves Amiga

Marco Frischkorn of HighAnt Development has announced that Battle will be the last game for Amiga. The reason quoted was that HighAnt Development do not think that the plans of Amiga Inc. are realistic.

  • Das Blaue Auge II (the blue eye) for free download

Timo Kloss of Inutilis Software gave Amiga Arena permission to make available the game Das Blaue Auge II - Die Schicksalssäge for free. You can find the Amiga Arena at http://online-club.de/~ARENA/ .

  • Ermentrud demo version

Inutilis Software have published a first demo version of the adventure game Ermentrud for free download. This version only runs on CyberGfx system so that I cannot present a preview.

  • Tales of Tamar

Version 0.32 of Tales of Tamar was released on the 5th of November.

You can download it with the client and main archive from http://www.tamar.net/ .

  • Nightlong Goodies

On the support site of Nightlong under http://www.clickboom.com are some Goodies for the game. You can download some background pictures and video sequences.
