3. Distribution

Currently there are just 3 ways of distribution:

  • Get it from the Aminet: wuarchive.wustl.edu/~aminet/doc/mags/. You will find them in archives of this type AaktIntMMYY.lha, where MM means the month and YY means the year. In opposition to the German edition there is no AmigaGuide version of Amiga aktuell international. The graphics are within an extra archive which is AaktIntMMYYGFX.lha. If you enter AaktInt in the 'Aminet searches' you will get all editions listed on the screen.

  • Get it from our homepage: http://www.aakt.de

  • Get it per eMail subscription: Just send an eMail to aakt@aakt.de or use the form on our website.

If you would like to include AMIGA aktuell international with your own disk 
magazine or put it on your BBS please send an eMail to aakt@aakt.de 
(Carsten Schroeder).
