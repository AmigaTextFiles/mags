9. Emulator news

In the Aminet there has been published a version 0.76 of ASp, which now supports RTG, better sound and beamsynching.

Furthermore the internet site of the "Godfather of Amiga Emulators" Juan Antonio Gomez Galvez has a new design. With this redesign came a new version of the MSX emulator AmiMSX on the homepage - version 3.1, mainly bugfixes.
