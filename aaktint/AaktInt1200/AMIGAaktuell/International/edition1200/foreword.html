+++ AmigaOS 3.9 has been released at the World of Amiga +++

With this welcome news, welcome to the new edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

November was really quiet and without major news. But not so in December: the Cologne/Germany »World of Amiga« fair, and the launch of the new AmigaOS version 3.9. More information on the WoA and the AmigaOS update are in this edition of AMIGA aktuell International.
