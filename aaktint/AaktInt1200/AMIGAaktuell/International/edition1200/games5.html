5. Preview: Payback

About 2 years ago a PC game named Grand Theft Auto hit the streets. This game had you doing politically incorrect things like pilfering cars. The novelty was 3D sight from above.

Payback brings this sort of gameplay to the Amiga. Payback is more than similar to Grand Theft Auto but this doesn´'t matter. Many programmers had tried to produce this sort of game before Apex Design, but none of them managed to get it right or playable due to the enormous demands on computation. Payback runs smoothly on my 68040 and the graphics recall the original game - 3D and among the fastest I´'ve ever seen, with the sound fitting the game. You have to get used to the handling but that will be no problem.

Conclusion: Payback is a new concept for Amiga gamers. The demo version of Payback gives you a good taste for it (several levels!) and the full version should be a real hit. I can only hope that Apex Design will release the demoversion for everyone soon so that y'all can see what a good game Payback is.
