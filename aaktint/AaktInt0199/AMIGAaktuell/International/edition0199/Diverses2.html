2. Warp3D 1.0 and StormMesa 3.0 available

Hans-Joerg Frieden, one of the Warp3D authors has informed in a newsgroup posting:

»Since this day Warp3D 1.0 and StormMesa 3.0 are available for download on the Haage&Partner homepage. Warp3D is a hardware independent driver system for 3D hardware. In the moment it supports the CyberVision64/3D while a driver for the Permedia2 boards of phase5 and one for the 3D/fx from Village Tronnic will follow soon.

StormMesa is a port of the Mesa-GL-Library by Brian Paul. The 
new revision 3.0 supports Warp3D as Rasterizer, soit is able to use 
3D hardware. 
 

Both is available on the Haage & Partner homepage in the new 3DWorld under www.haage-partner.com resp. per FTP under ftp.haage-partner.com/3DWorld. You really should read the "Readme.txt", if you want to use StormMesa, to find out which files you have to download...

Best wishes Hans-Joerg Frieden«
