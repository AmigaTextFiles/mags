4. New AMIGA show in autumn 1999

After the rang of the AMIGA show in Cologne decreased from year to year the Falke-Verlag, publisher of the new magazine amigaOS announces a possible solution of the problem:

»Falke-Verlag increases its activities in the Amiga area: In autumn 1999 we will mount an own Amiga show titled "Amiga-World-Show ´99". The show will be located in Hennef near Bonn with more than 3000 squar metres (approx. 3585 square yards). Informations for exhibitors are being sent these days, further information you will find in the next weeks.«

(Source: Falke-Verlag - http://www.amigaos.de)
