Pre-Preface

Basically the international version is a translation of the German version, but some articles of German interest only have been remove and, if possible, replaced by articles of international interest. AMIGA aktuell international will be available approx. two weeks after AMIGA aktuell. So, I hope you have fun with AMIGA aktuell international.

And now our editor in chief has the word:

This time you have to be satisfied with a quite lean edition. It seems that after the "headline month" November the firms, developers and users have to regain strength... Maybe this was the silence before a new storm. Maybe, and that is more likely, it is just the X-mas month December.

At least one theme played a major part in December: 3D! And there were three major events to happen:

  • The release of BVisionPPC, a graphics board with the 3D-chip Permedia2
  • the announcement of Village Tronic, to bring a 3DFX add-on for the graphics board Picasso IV on the market, soon

and last, but not least

  • the release of Warp3D, a driver system for 3D hardware. Even in this moment a number of game developers announced their support, which means the breakthrough for 3D hardware for the Amiga is not far away.
