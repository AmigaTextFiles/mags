8. Haage & Partner news

»01-Dec-98: ArtEffect 3.0
We are still working on the new release of ArtEffect 3.0, but it will be
finalized soon. Expected Date: end of January 99.
01-Dec-98: ArtEffect 2.6 in 7 languages
ArtEffect 2.6 is now available in 7 languages, two more are on the way.
01-Dec-98: Free FontMachine 3.20 Update
All FontMachine 3 users can get a free update to version 3.20 from our ftp
site.
04-Dec-98: AmigaWriter 1.2 Manual
We just finished the English translation of the manual of AmigaWriter 1.2.
Registered users can request it now.
14-Dec-98: STFax 3.6b Update
The free update STFax 3.6b is available for download. It offers ScanQuix
support, improved USR support and some bugfixes.
17-Dec-98: AmigaWriter Mailing List
We just installed an AmigaWriter mailing list.
18-Dec-98: Release of Warp3D and StormMesa 3.0
The first version of the hardware independent 3D driver system Warp3D is
available. Finally there exists a way to use the power of 3D graphics
boards, as it is done on other platforms for a long time.
20-Dez-98: Wacom Intuos Drivers
We just released the new Tableau drivers for the new pressure sensitive
Wacom Intuos tablets.
21-Dez-98: Updated StormMesa 3.0 Pages
We added a lot of information on StormMesa to the 3D World site, including
download pages, Q&A area and a gallery.
22-Dec-98: Voodoo will come!
3Dfx Voodoo Addon module for PicassoIV will come! That is the official text
of the VillageTronic homepage.«
