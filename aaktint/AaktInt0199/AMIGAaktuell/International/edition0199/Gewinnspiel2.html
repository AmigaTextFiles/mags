»Wildfire is the special effekt system for animations, picture sequences of and pictures. There are many new program features - some of them are really unique - but the most important thing is that Wildfire7PPC is very easy to understand and it is completely userfriendly.

Some of the new features are:

  •  Easy-to-use MUI user interface with many preview pictures and extended drag 'n' drop operations
  •  Fully developed converter (MPEG/QT/AVI/YAFA/IFF/picture sequences)
  •  Linear and non-linear effect system
  •  Real 3D effects (including light sources, phong shading, transparency, alpha rendering)
  •  More than 120 plug-ins
  •  Direct PPC and CyberGraphX support

    • It is even more powerful than Wildfire5 has ever been.
