PowerUp-to-date

3. News in brief

  • The graphics board CyberVisionPPC by phase5 is delivered with different SG-RAM modules, what causes the driver of the CyberGraphX version 3 (revision 70a) to work no longer. You have to use the driver delivered with the board. Version 4 will contain one driver for all revisions of the CyberVisionPPC, soon.
  • PerSuaSiVe SoftWorX, Andreas Kleinert, created a WarpOS-plug-in of his akJFIF datatype using the EGCS compiler. It can be downloaded via the homepage http://wdo.de/ark/.

    Andreas Kleinert will vom Feedback abhaengig machen, ob er auch seine uebrigen Datatypes und die SuperView-PowerPC-Module auf WarpOS umsetzt.

  • The sound card software AHI by Martin Blom will be released in a PowerPC version. A notice is on the homepage http://www.lysator.liu.se/~lcs/ahi_ppc.html .
