9. Haage & Partner announces ArtEffect 3.0

»ArtEffect 3.0
Now better than before...

ArtEffect 3 offers even more fun of painting: as many levels as you like, sensational colour run and new effect filters leave you creativity any freedom.

The well-known functions for natural painting are completely integrated, of course. The picture manipultating functions of ArtEffect are improved in quality and speed. The memory requirements are noticeable lower. The correction of pictures and scans is even more comfortable and the results are better. New is the support of indexed colours. So you can edit 256 colour GIF-pictures for the Internet and at last save them.

The overview about the new features:

  • more levels
  • more effect filters
  • improved colour runs
  • more loaders/savers
  • lower memory requirements
  • improved VMEM
  • improved tray support
New: complete Internet support
  • GIF- and JPEG-saver
  • indexed colours (e.g. 256)

System requirements: Amiga with 68020-Prozessor, AmigaOS 3.x, minimum of 8 MB RAM, hard disk

Press comments: Best picture manipulating program in 1997 (elected by the readers of Amiga Plus, Germany), 9 of 10 points (Magazyn Amiga 1/98, Poland), 89 %, "very good" (Amiga Magazin 3/98, Germany), "The reproduction of natural paint effects is first-class.", "...a program to recommend..." (Amiga Special 11/98, Germany)

ArtEffect 3.0 will be available in the end of January 99. The price for the update is at 69 DM (approx. 35,40 Euro; remark of the editors).«

»Special offer II: ArtEffect for 198 DM For the early new year there is a very low price of 198 DM for ArtEffect 3 (approx. 100 Euro; remark of the editors). This is for revision 2.6 incl. free update to 3.0 (end of January 99). ArtEffect 1.5 Classic is available for 49 DM (approx. 25 Euro; remark of the editors) incl. manual.«

(Source: Haage & Partner)
