» o St. Leon-Ro May 06, 1999 
 
 New clock port prolongation! 
  
 Due to multiple wishes of our customers, we offer the HyperCOM 1 interface card in a second type, which prolongs the A1200 clock port for approx. 10 centimetres (approx. 3.94 inch). 
  
 With this new type there are no longer problems with extension boards like a BVision graphics board or too large Zorro boards. As an additional use the useage of a BuddaFlash contoller of Individual Computers makes use, that also offers an Amiga 1200 compatible clock port. 
  
 So a HyperCOM 1 on a BuddaFlash is the currently cheapest multi I/O card for Zorro-Amigas. When making an order please watch out for the correct entry of the card type you wish! 
  
 Best wishes 
  
 VMC Harald Frank«
