11. Beta-Testing of AmigaOS 3.5


The first beta testing phase for the new AmigaOS 3.5 will start soon.


Note to the registration for OS 3.5 beta testing (17 May 1999)

We got over 2.500 applications for the beta test. The application phase has ended now - please do not send us applications any more. Thank you very much for your help. Every applicant should have gotten a confirmation by e-mail during the last week.

The people that will take part in the beta test will be informed by e-mail the next days. A second draft is planned for June/July.

The beta test will be run solely by HAAGE&PARTNER. Please do not ask Amiga Inc., Amiga Intl. or HAAGE&PARTNER any questions concerning the test. It is impossible to answer to them and it would slow-down development of OS 3.5. Thank you very much for your understanding.
