8. Product information: 'Future Drive Accelerator' v1.4

»There's a mew evaluation revsion 1.4 of 'Future Drive Acceslerator', that now also supports writing! This results in less disk accesses.

The most important features:

  • Accelerates any media like e.g. hard disk drives, floppy disk drives, Compact disk drives, ZIP drives, MagnetOptical drives or SyQuest drives nearly as a RamDrive.

  • Uses the free memory as a cache: this results in a maximum speed of your drives due to the full automatic reaction on changes in the memory ressources. Even "memory consuming" programs are satisfied that way.

  • It's the fastest cache program. Accelerates hard disk for factor 2 - 20, floppy disks for the factor 2 -997.

  • Write accesses are collected to let the write head "gliding" from the lowest to the highest address.

  • Softer, parallel writing and reading in multitasking.

  • Smart-Power-Technologie: Minimizes the number of disk accesses.

  • SmartCache: Read-ahead of blocks.

  • Supports DiskSafe (no more invalid disks).


  • Supports DiskExpander, Virtual-Memory-Manager (VMM), XFH, Temporary files and World-Wide-Web browsers.


  • Supports FileSystems with a variable block size.

  • Supports hard disks with a capacity of 4 GB or more.

  • No fragmentation (cutting) of the memory. (In opposition to DynamiCache).

To cut a long story short: It is the best disk cache program of the whole computer world.
