2. News in brief

  • On the Internet homepage of Microcode Solutions you can advance book PowerPC extension modules for the emulators fusion and PCx. The manufacturer is going to put the Addons to the market 2 months after 500 binding bookings were made. The fee of 154.95 US-$ (approx. 150.36 Euros) per module are to be paid in advance. Those who don't book in advance have to pay 199.00 US-$ (approx. 193.11 Euros) plus post and package.

    According to Microcode Solutions 320 orders for PCxPPC and 473 for FusionPPC were received. Further informations can be found on http://www.microcode-solutions.com/amiga/new.html.
