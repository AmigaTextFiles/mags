     ILBM (HAM6, HAM8, ILBM, IFF24), BMP (Windows-BMP, OS/2-BMP), PCX,
  GIF, Datatype, JPEG, TARGA, PNG, PPM, LPG, Amiga-Icon, NewIcon, RawRGB,

     54 Filters and 55 amimierted filters help you with the creation

Curving, spraying, brighten, darken, smearing, earthquake, emboss, edge-detect, invert noise, oil, smoothing, funky, pixelize, wave, replace colour, blurring, XOR, masking, create button, light flash, progressive picture, motion blur, antique, balance and many more...

Melting, radio light, fading in, fading out, flashing, noise, fade in lines, fade out lines, pixelize, waves, spraying, normal -> white, white -> Normal, smearing, blurring, earthquake, progressive, picture lackhole, light flash, moving, rolling, spin and many more...

Scaling, turning, colour reduction, anim publisher - allows the combination of up to 100 objects of these types: text, effects, picture/animation, light source, fire, particle system, area, alpha-channel, clipboard copy, clipboard paste, bump (bump mapper for effects similar to the ones of "Candy factory"), flashes, fractales, running colour, paintings, plasma and many more...

Optimized versions for

68000/010, 68020, 68030, 68040/060, PPC+68040/060 non FPU, PPC+68040/060 FPU, 68K + WarpUp

Demo versionen of:

ScanQuix, FFNews, VLRec, MultiLiveCam

AmigaRulez for animated 24bit Workbench colour backdrops, JPEG datatype with PowerUP support, Extensive developer documentation about UltraConv, translations of the program from the ATO, extensive textures, pictures and animations, extensive example projects for UltraConv, the APP plugin for Drag&Drop in UltraConv, an XFA player, third-party-filter for UltraConv.
