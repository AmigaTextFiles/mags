AMIGA appoints Dr. Rick LeFaivre as Chief Technology Officer (09-Apr-99)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

According to the AMIGA internet hompage the company has appointed Dr. Rick
LeFaivre as Chief Technology Officer (CTO). It must be assumed, that this
is "the" Dr. Rick LeFaivre, who formerly had had managing Positions at
SGI, Sun, Apple, Borland and other well-known companies.

Contact informations:

»Rick LeFaivre, Ph.D.
Chief Technology Officer
Telephone: +1 (619) 799-2518
Fax +1 (619) 799-3425«

(Source: AMIGA)
