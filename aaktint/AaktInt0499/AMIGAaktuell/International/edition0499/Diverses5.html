5. Informations regarding the meeting of the Amiga User Group Switzerland (AUGS)

» +++ *AUGS - MEETING* +++ 
 

Amiga User Group Switzerland

April 17 and 18, 1999

Bildungszentrum BZZ in Zofingen


Sa: 11 a.m. to 11 p.m.  
Su: 9 a.m. to 7 p.m.  
   

Free entry

Take your computer to the meeting!

The traditional AUGS meeting will be for the 14th time this year. Also again this year the news around the Amiga will be presented. You can participate on workshops with many interesting themes like an Internet introduction, search and find in the Internet, Linux and Amiga, KDE Desktop Environment under Linux and more. In addition to Amiga other computers are welcome. You can make a little look into the world of various other platforms with Linux like Silicon Graphics Workstations and other systems. On our Occasion market you can sell no longer needed hardware and software and find things you searched for a long time. Another climax is the traditional hard disk throwing and CD discus flinging on Sunday afternoon. Come too, it's worth it!

Latest infos under http://www.augs.ch

Workshops on Saturday:


12 a.m. Candy Factory Pro Marius Eckardt 
1 p.m. Internet Introduction Peter Moeckli

3 p.m. Linux and Amiga Matthias Kreis 
4 p.m. KDE Desktop Environment under Linux Caspar Schlegel 
5 p.m. QNX Claude Mueller

Hard disk throwing and CD discus:
Sunday, 2 p.m.

The small print:

Kontaktadresse: AUGS c/o D. Rueegg, Im Burgfeldenhof 43, 4055 Basel, Switzerland Tel. 061/383 22 31, Fax 061/383 22 32, info@augs.ch

Overnight stay: You can spend the night in Zofingen (SFr 7.00 (approx. 4.29 Euros) for AUGS members and SFR 15.00 (approx. 9.18 Euros) for others), a Zmorgae is there, too. Bring your sleeping bag with you!

Food and drink: The possibilites of food and drink are limited. But there are good restaurants in Zofingen.

Computer: Assembly is possible on Saturday from 9.30 a.m. Don't forget extension lead and plug strip...

Noise: To avoid disturbing the running workshops and talks headphones are recommended...

Internet: If you want to connect your computer to a network during the meeting you show bring your network card and cables (best is twisted pair) and if possible a Hub, 10Mbit. Please report to the Info booth on the meeting.

Journey: per car or train (map on the AUGS-HP)«
