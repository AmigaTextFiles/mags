Hey! Only 35 readers had attended at the last competition (and 10 at the FD charts)!? I don't see any reason, why the participation was that low, so please would sombody kindly explain it to me? I sincerely hope, that the number of participants will be clearly higher this months.

The solutions and results:

Standard question: What is the name of the new president of Amiga?

Answer: Correct is, as was known by everybody, 'Jim Collas'.


Master question:  The headquarters of Amiga are in San Diego, 
 California. In which cities the former individual enterprises 
 Amiga, Inc. and Amiga International were situated? 
  
Answer:  AMIGA International, Inc. resided in Langen, Hessen/Germany, 
 the department for development AMIGA, Inc. in Sioux City, USA.
