»Kniffel might be one of the favorite dice game. There are various ports, but none of them could satisfy my wishes.

My parents like this game so much, that I decided to give them a Kniffel program as a present I wrote myself, shortly before christmas 1993.

After the program has proven its worth in "daily use" I would like to give it to others. When programming the game I did it without much useless rubbish as playing against computer opponents or sound support, but attached great value on stability and following the guidelines of Commodore. How much I was successful in this matter you have to find out yourself.

The program was completely developped in "Amiga Oberon".«
