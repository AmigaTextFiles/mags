2. Maxon CAD for the PC required (by Niels Nolte)

»Since quite a while (since the 1980th) I own an Amiga 2000 with a 68040 turbo board Fusion-Forty. Because of professional occupations I mostly work with a PC, fitted with a Siamese board for Amiga. With this board it is possible to switch to and fro between PC and Amiga and by this way transfer data from one system to the other. I use the Amiga especially because of the powerfull CAD program by Maxon. This program is easy to operate and I wouldn't like to change it against a PC program (e.g. Turbp-CAD). The printouts come perfectly and exactly fit the scale. I tried to get the program for PC, particularly because Maxon at that time (?) transformed that program for PC. The sale was stopped resp. suspended. Could it be possible, that there is someone among the Amiga-friends, who may offer me this program for the PC?

With best regards

Niels Nolte

Ni.Nolte@t-online.de«
