Various Amiga news

6. Now, BlackIRC is freeware (and more news from Innovative)

Innovative (http://www.innovative-web.de) informiert:

»March 10, 2000 VHI-Treiber for GVP IV 24 available

A VHI driver for the GVP IV24 digitizer can be freely downloaded from the VHI site. For using the driver we recommend VLRec NG 4.0 :)

March 10, 2000 VHI homepage updated

The VHI homepage was updated and got the latest infos. The new areas are the logo-area and a page with pictures and informations about most of the supported boards.

March 14, 2000 BlackIRC 1.1 Freeware Edition released

Our business activities more more and more to the graphics area and the development of online applications for servers and companies. This is why our capacities are not enough to keep BlackIRC under that further as it would be required and so we release it as freeware. The integrated news service BlackNEWS is continued by amiga-news.de. At the same time we cancel the support for BlackIRC, because it is freeware, now, and it is well-known, tha support costs money. If a company or private person is interested in a continuing the development of BlackIRC we licence the sourcecode for 5000 DEM (approx. 2551.02 Euros) and more.

BlackIRC is an IRC client for the AMIGA, which has unique features. There are some extras like XDCC, Bots, CTCP-Flood protection, etc. built-in the client. Furthermore BlackIRC is the only IRC client, which allows videoconferencing.

March 14, 2000 ANet 1200 sold out

Due to the fact that the ANet 1200 cards are sold out we take them out of our product range.

March 21, 2000 VHI Studio 5.0 and new digital camera software

Innovative announces new software for digital comaeras and a new revision of VLRec NG. This combination will make it possible for owners of a digital camera to use their digital camera not just for making photos, but also for using it as WebCam, time-laps motion recordings and creating video clips and much more. But Innovative has still need of beta testers and asks for wishes for drivers for digital cameras. Mor about this can be found on the homepage of Innovative.

March 21, 2000 New pictures in the fxPAINT gallery

Now, there are also the impressive pictures of Dirk Baeyens in the gallery. There are also some new ones from Jochen Abitz.«
