14. Emulator-News

Recently a new link directory about the Amiga emulation opened. The project is called A.G.N.U.S. and can be found on http://www.agnus.co.uk/ There you find links and interviews about Amiga emulation e.g. about the further development of the Amiga emulator Fellow.

Again, Mathias "AmiDog" Roslund offers quite a lot on his homepage this month. In addition to new revisions of DarcNES there is SOPE, an emulator of the Sony Playstation for download. SOPE was updated twice this month and requires a PPC as well as a graphics board. There are no infos about the compatibility of the emulators, but according to rumours it is quite good.

A new revision of A\NES for CyberGraphX has been released. In the latest revision there were mainly bugs removed and various new things implemented.
