3. News from the CD³² alliance

CD³²-Allianz (http://home.t-online.de/home/amiga-freak) informiert:

»Help forum for CD³² users made available!

Unfortunately there are little places for users of the CD³², if you need help. This is what the CD³² alliance is for and created help a forum on their CD³² corner.

If you have got problems with the CD³², CD³² games or hardware or if you are looking for something or you want to sell something, this is the right place. Use it, because it is the only "CD³² only" forum, we do not know about any other.

The help forum is also for CD³² users of other countries, because there are many English users, who use a CD³² and they will also need help.

In addition to that we would be very happy, if you supported our work by making a link from your homepage to the CD³² corner, because WE do not leave the CD³² alone!&lauqo;

»CD³² alliance presents the CD³² developer kit

For those software programmers of ree will there is the "CD³² developer kit" for download on the developer site of the CD³² corner. Our thanks goes to Amiga, Corp. who gave us the permission.

We hope very much, that you use the chance and download the developer kit. Because now the software programmers have the chance to make their software bootable on the CD³² resp. to port their software to the CD³².

The developer site is expected to be completed in April, so you will find a manual how to make you own game bootable for the CD³².

Hotlist entry for the CD³² possible, now

Unfortunately the CD³² corner could not be put into the hotlist of the web browsers since the latest update in February. This has changed with the latest update.

We are looking urgent for an English translator!

More than ever the CD³² is looking for people with knowledge of the English language.

The CD³² zone (www.amigaemulation.com/cd32) offers an updated FAQ about the CD³² called "CD³² FAQ 2000". Now, the CD³² zone wants to have the FAQ translated into other languages, including German.

The CD³² alliance would like to do that. But we work on other projects and so we do not have the ressources to care about the FAQ. This is why we are looking for one person or more persons, who could do this for us.«
