Welcome to the new edition of AMIGA aktuell.

While AMIGAs president Bill McEwen made a very large Executive Update, which you find in this edition, the news of the first days in April were led by the Amiga show in St. Louis. There the Amiga developer system, which is used for programming the Amiga of the next generation, will be presented as well as the partners of AMIGA (Sun Microsystems, RedHat and Corel) and many details about the future. All this you find in this edition of AMIGA aktuell International.
