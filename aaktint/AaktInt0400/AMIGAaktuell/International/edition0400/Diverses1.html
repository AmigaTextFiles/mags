1. New German Amiga show announced for June

After the sad decline of the computer show in Cologne, Germany, the publisher Falke decided to make an Amiga and Atari show, titled »World of Alternatives« in Neuss, Germany on June 10 and 11. Up to now, the companies AMIGA Germany, Vesalia Computer, KDH Datentechnik, Epic Marketing Haage & Partner, Schatztruhe GmbH, Titan Computer, RBM Digitaltechnik, Individual Computers, Dreieinhalb-Computer and APC&TCP announced being on the show.

Further informations can be found in the next edition of AMIGA aktuell International resp. on the (German) show homepage (http://www.amiga-messe.de).
