Various Amiga-News

8. News in Brief

  • From now on, the German MIDI version of Camouflage-B 68k is freeware and can be downloaded on http://www.camx.de. But a German manual costs abou 30 DEM (approx. 15.31 Euros).

  • The Aminet server of the University of Paderborn, Germany has been out of order for some weeks since a hard disk crash. The result was, that the American server ftp://ftp.wustl.edu became the new main server, again, after loosing this status in 1999 - also due to a HD defect. The server Paderborn is supposed to go online as a mirror.

  • Unfortunately the British Amiga magazine Amiga Format was cancelled.
