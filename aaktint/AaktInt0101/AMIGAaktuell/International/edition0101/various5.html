5. News from Airsoft Softwair

Airsoft Softwair (http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de) informs:

»The sequel to the 'The Best of Airsoft Softwair' CD-ROM which was released last year by APC&TCP is available now. The Gold Edition is filled with 640 megabyte and contains all releases which have ever been made by Airsoft Softwair. All Shareware programs are contained in full versions, of course. Some are even exclusive, like CD32 Games Install Kit v4.0 and Rainboot v3.1.

The CD-ROM is available for 29.95 DM (20 US$) from the author.

If you own the first edition, you can order it even for only 19.95 DM (15 US$).

More information on http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de/«

»The support sites for the The Best of Airsoft Softwair Gold Edition CD-ROM are now online on the Airsoft Softwair homepage <http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de>.

You can get much information and soon also screenshots on that page and additionally the CD-ROM can also be ordered from there.

The The Best of Airsoft Softwair Gold Edition CD-ROM is out now for a price of 29.95 DM ($20 USD).

Finally, Airsoft Softwair wishes all Amiga users a merry christmas and a succesful healthy new year 2001!«
