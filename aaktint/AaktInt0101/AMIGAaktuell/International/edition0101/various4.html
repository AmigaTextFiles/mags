»Amiga Arena News
(03.12.2000)


Amiga Arena - World First!

The Amiga Arena releases "Bubble Stones2" for the first time under own label! Bubble Stones2 is a "Bust a Move" clone and is now available in 256 colours version.

This version was developed exclusively for the Amiga Arena! The playground was designed by Amiga Arena and this looks like the first development Amiga Arena is connected with.

Amiga Arena - Schlachtfeld V2.16 World First! The Amiga Arena presents exclusively in cooperation with Peter Weigoldt "Schlachtfeld V2.16" [Battle Field].

The version 2.16 is a registered Amiga Arena Edition with map editor.

The map editor is exclusively contained in this archive and will not be available on Aminet or on the homepage of "Schlachtfeld".

"SCHLACHTFELD2" is a game of strategy for one to four players. It is a mixture of "BattleIsle" and "Battleforce". Here are some highlights:

  • max. four teams may fight against and with each other

  • teams may be controlled by the computer

  • based on rounds and action

  • many different fighting units

  • more than 50 different systems of weaponry

  • victorious teams proceed to the next fight

  • game scores can be saved

  • starting with SCHLACHTFELD2 V2.1 voice output

  • starting with SCHLACHTFELD2 V2.1 null-modem game

Schlachtfeld is SHAREWARE (20 DM/10 Euro) and I call on everyone to register at Peter Weigoldt and/or give feedback.

»Amiga Arena News
(Dec. 18, 2000)


Amiga Arena - Bubble Stones 2.1!

In cooperation with Guymay the Amiga Arena presents version 2.1 of "Bubble Stones", the freeware clone of "Bust a Move"!

New in version 2.1:

  • new play mode "Hot"

  • keyboard support

  • English tutorial

  • improved gameplay

Now you can design the view of the playground for yourself! If you do make playgrounds please send them to me, so that a Bubble-Stones homepage with playgrounds and highscores can be created. Please send ideas for improvements and suggestions to GuyMay, the developer of "Bubble Stones"!
