Software
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

UnLZX has been released in version 2.16 and gained some speed and a percentage display.
[http://drake.here.de]

The program ProStation Audio T8 has been released and is said to be priced at 235 Dollars (US).
[http://www.audiolabs.it]

The stormamiga.lib V45.00 in version alpha 4 is available.
[http://WWW.CyberdyneSystems.de/stormamiga_lib/index_d.html]

The CDDA-to-mp3 converter SecondSpin has been released as V2.12 beta.
[http://personal.inet.fi/bailu/zuulikuuli/secondspin.html]

Version 4.2 pre8 of CyberGraphX has been released.
[http://www.vgr.com/cybergfx]

Meanwhile the 12th update for GeoWorld has been published.
[http://GeoWorld.AmigaSoft.de]

A new version of the Mustek driver BetaScanMustek is ready for download.
[http://ftp.wustl.edu/~aminet/aminet/gfx/show/VideoMPEGA.lha]

The popular MPEG video player Frogger has been released in version 1.64b1.
[http://frogger.rules.pl]

The new version of the assembler ASM-One, V 1.45 rev 454c is available.
[http://www.euronet.nl/users/jdm/ftp/asmonev145.lha]

The new version of the open source operating system NetBSD, Version 1.5, has come out.
[http://www.netbsd.org/Ports/amiga/]

The spreadsheet software DataM II has been published as version 0.11 beta.
[http://www.amclust-net.de/software/Kirsch.html]

The colour correction software for scanners ICS may be downloaded in version 2.
[http://www.ics.coloraid.de]

The update to V4.8.4 for EASys has been published, among other things problems with OS3.9 are avoided.
[http://www.eternity-computer.de/easys/pages/news.html]

HTMLread appeared in version V.48.
[http://www.htmlread.50g.com/]

The office package Papyrus, originally written for the Atari, will with assistance of Titan Computer be ported to the Amiga.
[http://www.rom-logicware.de]

The 3D modelling software "Realsoft 3D" is said to be ported to Amiga, too, by Hyperion.
[http://www.amiga-news.de/archiv/001211.shtml]

The stockexchange software Bonds is ready for download in V1.78 beta 3.
[http://www.programmed-excellence.de]

Another update of Digital Almanac III has appeared.
[http://members.tripod.de/achimste/download/da3xmas.xpk]

The screengrabber SGrab has been published in version 1.14.
[http://stephan-rupprecht.de]

MainPrefs in version 2.0 may be downloaded.
[http://www.martin-elsner.de/]

New drivers for the ISDN-Surfer have appeared V 1.938): capi20.device and isdnsurfer.device.
[http://www.jschoenfeld.com/jumpnews.htm]

StrICQ has been published in version 0.1733.
[http://www.multimania.com/aubioane/stricq/stricq.html]

The probably most popular MP3 player for the Amiga, AmigaAMP, has finally been released in V2.8.
[http://www.amigaamp.de/]

JST has been published as V 4.3.
[http://jffabre.free.fr/amiga/bintools/LATEST/jst.lha]

Hand in hand with JST, WHD-Load in version 13 is available, too.
[http://www.whdload.org]

The web browser Voyager has been released in version 3.3.56 alpha.
[http://vapor.meanmachine.ch/voyager/v_33_alpha56.lzx]

Martin Mason Merz has put a new version of his website onto the net and simultaneously delivers new versions of his excellent icons.

The version of AmigAIM, the unofficial AOL Instant Messenger for the Amiga, jumped to V0.9405.
[http://www.missingpiece.com/AmigAIM/]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hardware
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The work for a new soundboard with name "Martina" for the clock port of the Amiga 1200 and later on for Amiga 3000/4000 is under way. It offers two 16-Bit input- and output-channels each with maximum 96 kHz and in addition an extension port e.g. for an MPEG decoder.
[http://www.realdreamz.cz/amiga/local/1_12martina.html]

The PCI board Mediator is said to be available for the Amiga 4000 before long. It will host five PCI 2.1 and seven ZorroII/III slots.
[http://www.elbox.com]

Meanwhile the soundboard Prelude Z2 has become available.
[http://www.act-net.com]
