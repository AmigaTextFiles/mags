Preface for Amiga aktuell international only

Welcome to the new edition of AMIGA aktuell international.

As you might have noticed, there are now 5 editions of Amiga aktuell international. But I didn't get any feedback yet. No "Well done!" or "You could do better...". Due to the fact that it is a lot of work to translate a complete edition I decided to do only one more edition, if there is no feedback, because I think, that there isn't any interest in Amiga aktuell international then. But you can try to convince me that there is interest for Amiga aktuell international.

Just write something like "Please keep translating Amiga aktuell", send me ideas, workshops, test reports or anything else for Amiga aktuell international.

Yours

Martin Henke
