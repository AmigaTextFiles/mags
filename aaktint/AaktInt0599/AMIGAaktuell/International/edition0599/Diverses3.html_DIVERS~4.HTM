»Pixel64 ZIII


These are some extra informations about the new board: 
 
Graphics board for A3000 and A4000, 
based on Cirrus GD5434 chip, 
4MB graphics memory, 
Flash-ROM for later upgrades (upgrade can be made in the Amiga!), 
Contains a complete adaptor for a real PCI V2.2 , 
3 PCI connector (5V and 3.3V can be used), 
Support of the PCI auto configuration (Plug & Play), 
Support of any PCI-3D accelerator, 
Support of any 100 MB PCI Ethernet cards, 
Support of any PCI sound cards, 
 
customer's price: 1500 bis 1900 Frs (200$ bis 260$, 360DM-470DM, approx. 183.67 Euros-239.80 Euros) 
 
Available soon!«
