AMIGA

17. News in brief

  • AMIGA announced an A1200 tower with G3-PPC board of Phase 5. The computer will be made available for a good price, soon.

  • According to Derrec Lisle of AMIGA the design company Pentagram (http://www.pentagram.com) will design the case of the AmigaNG computer. There were planned five different case version.

  • During the last weeks a number of newspapers, magazines as well as electronic services reported about AMIGAs future plans. Due to that the Amiga is present in the media as it hasn't been for a long time.

  • On the AMIGA homepage (http://www.amiga.de) you can download an actualized version of the report regarding the y2k problem with the Amiga. Author is Olaf Barthel.

  • Michael Reese, who was listed on the homepage of AMIGA for a short time, seems to have left the company.
