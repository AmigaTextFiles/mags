Welcome to the new edition of AMIGA aktuell international.

In April AMIGA was in the headlines again. Now the company has e.g. a Chief Technology Officer (CTO), who was employed in a leading position in the most known computer companies of the world, like Apple, Borland, Silicon Graphics and Sun.

In the PowerPC hardware range there are also interesting announcements:

  • Titan Computer, the turbo board manufacturer ACT as well as Haage & Partner want to join their efforts to put a G3 board for the Amiga on the market.

  • GVP-M also plans G3 boards

Negative news are to hear from the German magazine market: Unfortunately one Amiga magazine is gone. After there were rumours before, that the Amiga Fever was to be cancelled, it was announced in the last days of April, that the magazine is integrated into the amigaOS without delay.
