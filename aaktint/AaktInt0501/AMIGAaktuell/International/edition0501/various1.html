»Aminet 42 CD-ROM is available!

»Product information

The Aminet CD 42 - April 2001 - contains more than 800 MB (unpacked) of software in nearly one thousand archives. Since Aminet CD 41 uncountable MB of novelties have been added. All programs have German descriptions; many have a German documentation. As special highlight the Aminet 42 contains the full version of the SuperView Productivity Suite II.
