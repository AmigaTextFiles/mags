1. Titan Computer announces MotionStudio for MorphOS

Titan Computer (http://www.titan-computer.com) informs:

»Ladies and Gentlemen,

Titan Computer Company is pleased to be inform you that we have also decided to support MorphOS in the future. One of our first projects will be the video cutting software Motionstudio, developed by the software producer of the same name in Bremen, Germany, which previously has been responsible for the programs known as Elastic Dreams and Artstudio Pro.

Motionstudio will be released in the 4th quarter exclusively for MorphOS.

The software will offer non-linear video cutting, plugin interface, more than 40 video and audio effects, innovative GUI and Firewire support (e.g. bplan Pegasos computer). Under the link

http://www.titan-computer.com/motionstudio/motionsnap.jpg

you can have a preview of the GUI.«
