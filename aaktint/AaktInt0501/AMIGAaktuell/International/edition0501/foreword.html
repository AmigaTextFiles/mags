++ PowerMac-Emulator iFusion released +++

With this, welcome to the latest edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

Rather quietly passed the month of April: apparently the Amiga community fell into some kind of lethargy after the latest change of course by AMIGA. Only the release of the PowerMac emulator iFusion, long promised, made a stir.

Two items about the mag:

  • All (German speaking) readers are invited to join the new AMIGA aktuell discussion mailing list. More information about that in the Forum of this edition.

  • Because Stefan Funke in the future only will make updated issues of the PowerPC programs list from time to time, to save the space these will be published only when a new version has appeared.

And now dear reader:

  • To our regular summonses:

    I would like to ask you: Any writer of articles is happy to get praise, but also constructive criticism - both are motivating. So, please don't hesitate to send us your comments about the various articles to AMIGA aktuell at aakt@aakt.de.


  • We are always looking for staff members who will regularly, sporadically or just once contribute to AMIGA aktuell International. Nearly no limits on topics! If you are interested please make yourself known at aakt@aakt.de.

Have lots of fun with this edition,

yours

Carsten Schroeder
