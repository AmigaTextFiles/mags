Frank Weber gave his program CDCat for the competition:

»CDCat Release 3 (MUI) is the PPC/68k CD-ROM catalogizer for the Amiga. It also archives hard drives, floppy disks and directories (e.g. with many graphic files).

It is also quite valuable for Aminet-CD's because age, size and archive descriptions are considered. CDCat offers a super fast search mode (PPC; 68k 85% inline assembler!), in addition your archived CDs can be browsed without putting the CD into the drive.

CDCat is also able to unarchive, view pictures, etc. But you can also configure your favourite program.


More features: XPK support 
 Notepad for management of found files 
 DiskIDs for quick identification of CDs, etc. 
  

A demo version of the program is available on the Aminet. The current revision is 3.0. More information about CDCat can be found on

http://www.amigaworld.com/support/cdcat/.

New in revision 3 are e.g. PowerPC support, the catalogue manager for reorganizing catalogues, a filter function, etc.
