»Amiga Arena News
(May 01, 2001)


Amiga Arena Interview Action!

Schlachtfeld2, a rounds-based strategic game, was developed in Amos by Peter Weigoldt and distributed as shareware. I enjoyed Schlachtfeld2 quite a bit, but I lost interest due to the not-so-well done graphics and the quite complex handling involved in managing the units! A while back a new team assumed the development and from the first previews it appears that Schlachtfeld will become a really good strategic game! For Amiga Arena this was the occasion to ask Horst Diebel of Amiga Society for further details! Please read the extensive interview under the header "Aktionen" ["Actions"]!

Addendum: Opinion of Amiga Arena!

The team with Horst D. is very keen for you to share the development with proposals, graphics etc.! Thus you can influence the development of the game! Be creative and support actively Amiga developers, everyone will benefit from this!«

database??

  • Selectable print (bold, italics and underlined) for record fields and data.

  • Asynchronous multitasking.

  • Fully configurable: fonts, screen mode, options, palette and language.
