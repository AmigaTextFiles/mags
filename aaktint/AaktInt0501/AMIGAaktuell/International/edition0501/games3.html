Games section

3. News

  • Exodus Update

Exodus now has an update for owners of graphics boards.

According to No Risc No Fun the speed of the game is clearly increased with this update.

  • Tales of Tamar News

The ToT team is now cooperating with the "Rüstzeug-Team" ["Equipment team"] Rüstzeug is a manufacturer of middle-ages weaponry and armour for Live Action role-plays, that means role-games copied from reality. Furthermore the team is looking for another 50 beta testers.

  • CD32 Allianz News

The CD32 Allianz recently conducted a survey to satisfy as well as possible the wishes of the readers. Additionally a users list is made up. You can sign on under home.t-online.de/home/amiga-freak

  • Shogo Demo released

A demo of this shooter based on a 3D-story has been published on the Aminet. The game, converted by Hyperion, requires at least 64 MB RAM and a PPC/WarpUp and Warp3D. The game is said to appear in the final version in early May. A test you already can find here.

  • Battle cancelled

The game "Battle" by HighAnt Development has been cancelled due to private and personal reasons of the programmer.

  • Hyperion hail plans of Amiga

Hyperion welcomes the plans of the AmigaOS 4.0 (AaktInt had reported).

Ben Yoris stated that they will port all games recently planned for AmigaDE as well to AmigaOS 4, and additionally bring OpenGL for the OS with Mesa 3.4.

  • Aqua Demo v1.1

There appeared a new demo of the adventure game Aqua. Now AHI sound is supported, too, and a bug, when opening or closing screens, has been fixed.

  • Crystal Software now Clearwater Interactive

The games manufacturer, well known from Bubble Heroes and Land of Genesis, has changed the structure and now is named Clearwater Interactive. The homepage www.cw-i.com received a new design as well.

  • AmigaFlame game charts

At AmigaFlame again the 20 most popular games were voted for. As expected the ranking was: 1st Quake, 2nd Napalm, 3rd Payback

  • dynAMIte CD-Version?

It is planned to release a CD version of the popular Bomberman Clone bdynAMIte, if there will be 150 pre-orders made.

Please report to www.amisource.de/amisource

  • Payback WOS

It is planned to publish a PPC version of the top game Payback in summer. And the best of it: All users, who already own Payback, will be able to download the update free of cost.

  • Commercial game for MorphOS

Epic Interactive has announced the release of the graphics adventure »The Feeble Files« for MorphOS.
