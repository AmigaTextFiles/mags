Amiga PowerPC

2. In a Nutshell (by Oliver Tacke)

A new version of the ilbm datatype (V 45.6) for WarpOS has been released.
[http://home.wtal.de/stephanr/download/ilbmdtPPC.lha]

Version 0.8h of the ppclibemu.library has appeared. It permits the execution of powerUP programs under WarpOS.
[http://ftp.uni-paderborn.de/ftp/aminet/pub/util/libs/ppclibemu.lha]

GhostScript,, a well known viewer for pdf and PostScript files is ready in version 6.50 for WarpOS.
[http://www.muenster.de/~tomjoad/]

A new beta-version (V3.3.95) of the browser Voyager for MorphOS has been published.
[http://v3.vapor.com/]

In addition there is a new beta-version of the image decoder for V3.3, which is NOT compatible with V3.2.
[http://v3.vapor.com/]

Again there are new updates for the datatypes for WarpOS by Oliver Robert: WarpJPG.datatype (V44.20) and WarpPNG.datatype (V44.15).
[http://www.nanunanu.org/~oliver]

The iMac emulator iFusion after a long wait is redy and will be delivered.
[http://www.microcode-solutions.com/home.htm]

Already available for iFusion is an update to V1.1.1.
[http://www.blittersoft.com/cgi-bin/ams/shopzone30.cgi/iFusion.html]

The update to version 3.1 for the program Digital Almanac III immediately can be downloaded.
[http://www.soft-ware.de/dalmanac]

A new version (V3.89 alpha) of the mp3-encoder Lame for WarpOS has appeared.
[http://www.honeypoet.net/audio/]
