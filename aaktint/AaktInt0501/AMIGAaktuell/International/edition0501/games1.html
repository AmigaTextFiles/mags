AMIGA aktuell International Games Section (by Malte Mende and Jürgen Theiner)

1. Foreword

Good morning, dear friends of computer games!

Again a month has passed. Time runs faster and faster, if you consider, that recently it was New Year's eve ... and, yes, the Millenium ended a few days ago, too, didn't it? Oh well, maybe time is speeding up because nothing is happening. At least nothing worldshaking.

And so we while away our existence in the shadow as editors without sufficient fodder for typing-sore fingers, wistfully staring out of the window waiting for supply.

But this will arrive, as we all know from Worms, not at all, or too late.

Oh yes, I received a mail from moneyless (AmiSource, dynAMIte). He complained about the test. Only small vanities, at least I believe so.

But ... what do our readers think? Time and again at games@aakt.de the only mails that arrive are complaints from programmers, but we hardly (or never?) hear a word from the readers.

May we conclude, that everybody is content? Or is it of no interest? Or doesn't anybody read the Games Part at all?

Questions upon questions, but no answers in sight.

Perhaps we will see the light next month, now read the Game's Part, hand-needled by Jürgen and Malte.

Be seeing you,

Jürgen&Mac
