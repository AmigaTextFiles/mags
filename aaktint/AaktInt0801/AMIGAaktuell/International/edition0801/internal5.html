5. AMIGA-aktuell-Diskussionen-MailingList

With this I'd cordially like to invite all readers to enter the AMIGA-aktuell mailinglist. Note that postings to the mailinglist must be made only in the German language. If a reader does not speak German and has an urgent problem, he should directly turn in English to Gernod Schomberg <g.schomberg@t-online.de>, to Carsten Schröder <aakt@aakt.de> or to me <christian@aakt.de>. We will in your behalf post in the mailinglist and return the (translated) answers to you.

Everybody, who wants to participate in discussions about AMIGA and AMIGA aktuell should send an e-mail to:

aaktdiskussionen-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

Leave the Subject line blank and the e-mail needs no further text.

In this list it is not possible to add attachments to mails. Please use the special file area made available by yahoogroups for this purpose. There is a webspace of 20 MB for all participants. Usually this size should be sufficient.

The ads attached to the end of postings are not connected to AMIGA aktuell (International). The editor of AMIGA aktuell uses this service free of charge, but the service is financed by advertisements.

For those who want to get additional information about this list, I warmly recommend following internet-links:

http://de.groups.yahoo.com/ http://de.groups.yahoo.com/group/aaktdiskussionen

Send your questions or problems preferably to me and not to Carsten Schröder.
