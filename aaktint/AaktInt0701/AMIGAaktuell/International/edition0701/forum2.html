First of all, thank you for your e-mails and the nice compliments. The topics you raise will certainly be interesting to other readers. If there is someone with experience in Internet phoning, Euro on the Amiga or Socks5 configuration ... come on, hit the keys and let's have it ;)! We at AMIGA aktuell are also looking for authors who could write something about the various PCI extensions from DCE (G-Rex) and Matay (Prometheus). And some time ago a reader expressed the wish to read a direct comparison between Opus Magellan, EASys and Scalos. The topic of networking (Ethernet, Router, Hubs, Switches, TCP/IP, ADSL and more) is way up there in readers' top top lists of wishes.

We have considered that it might be a good idea to publish this list of wishes regularly in AMIGA aktuell (International). Every reader would be invited to inform us about his wishes for articles. I would be ready to collect this list of wants and wishes and publish it in the Forum.

But this would depend upon the resonance of the reader (that means, of you, who is reading, and only reading, these lines ;)). If we receive only readers' requests, but no appropriate articles, the compilation of a list of wishes would be unnecessary, as you will understand.

Christian Aichinger <christian@aakt.de>
