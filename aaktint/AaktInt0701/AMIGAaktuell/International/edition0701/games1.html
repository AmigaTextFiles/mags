1. Foreword

Hi, People!

Yes, you feel it, summer is approaching relentlessly. In TV only reruns are to be seen, the hot-dog booth near the station overnight changed into an icecream stand and, most important for the games section, there are nearly no new games.

In all the world, programmers and players rather sit in the (beer-) gardens than in front of their computers. The only ones sitting at their computers are the deplorable editors, who every single month have to care for the making of the Games Section of AMIGA aktuell (International) ...

I think, because of this we have earned some praise for ourselves. I mean, what would you ever you do without us? Get it? So send your love-letters, poems and hymns of praise to:

games@aakt.de

The mails will be forwarded to Jürgen and me, and we will then answer them. Word of honour!

Have fun,

Jürgen&Mac
