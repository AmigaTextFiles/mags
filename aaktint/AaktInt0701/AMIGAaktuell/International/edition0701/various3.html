3. Haage & Partner announces »ArtEffect Plugins Collection«

»Press release

Highlights of the effects are Glow and DarkGlow (which lets objects glow), solar, neon and alien (which alienate images), classics like PageCurl, (which curls a corner of an image), as well as OilPainting, which transforms every picture or scan into a perfect oil painting. Thanks to the many user-definable settings, the effects offer countless combinations.

For post-processing scanned images and pictures, there are fast, high-quality filters, such as SmartMedian, which removes distortions within pictures without making the image unsharp, as well as many blurs such as Average Blur, Gaussian Blur, Smart Blur and Softener. Of special interest is the highly optimised version of the "Unsharp Mask", a sharpening technique which leaves nothing left to be desired.

The PostScript PlugIn offers complete PostScript-output, which is very well suited for direct printing to a PostScript-Level-2 printer. In addition, EPS-files can be created, which are well suited for use in other programs such as PageStream.

A new novel feature is the frames-effects, which can give all images a picture frame. Dull images are now a thing of the past, because with these effects anyone can make a real piece of art from any picture.

System requirements:
Requires ArtEffect 2, 3 or 4 (does not run with 1.5 or the SE-version)
