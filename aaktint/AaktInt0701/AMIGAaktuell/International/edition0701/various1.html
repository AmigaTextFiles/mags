1. Announcement: Göttingen User Meeting »Amiga Summer Jam 2001«

Rüdiger Grünewald <rudi.g@t-online.de> informs:

»Hello!

The third meeting of the Amiga-"crackpots" of Göttingen [German city] is planned for the 8th and 9th of August. There is a good chance, that it will be held together with the annual meeting of a well known, supra-regional Amiga club.

Because we again can offer Internet access via the network of Göttingen University, the themes "Amiga on the net...", Samba, HTTP/FTP server will be important topics.

And playing: Of course you may play and gamble extensively in the net ... There stands nothing against Quake and HereticII matches.

About the full detail you can inform yourselves before long on our homepage (http://www.g-a-u.org) .«
