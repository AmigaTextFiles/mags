Games section

3. News

  • First Aid at playamiga.de

At http://www.playamiga.de there now is a helping page, where you can download patches and other necessities for games. The page lives on cooperation... meaning that readers swop tips with readers.

  • Survey for Schlachtfeld successor

On http://www.amiga-society.de the fans of the strategic game "Schlachtfeld" may vote, with what playing principles the successor is to be designed: Based on rounds, on events or in real time.

  • dynAMIte update

The popular Bomberman clone dynAMIte has been released in version 1.5. Download under http://www.amisource.de

  • Genetic Species online!

The genial 3D-shooter Genetic Species has been uploaded to Aminet in full version! The game, 280 MB of size, split in several archives, is obtainable there free. Have a flatrate? Download!

  • Earth2140 Missions CD

e.p.i.c. will publish in July a Missions CD for the real time strategic game Earth2140. It contains 80 new missions, 6 new units, two new buildings and one new territory and is said to have additionally the missing multi-player mode.

  • Amiga Classix 3

The third part of the game compilation of e.p.i.c. has been released and among others it contains Der Seelenturm, XTreme Racing, Obsession Pinball, Fatman, Beneath the Steel Sky, Lure of the Temptress, Vroom, Wheelspin, Trainer, Ziriax, Sixth Sense Investigation and Blade. Available at games distributors.

  • Limited Downloads at Back2roots.org

The download service at http://www.back2roots.org has been limited to a fixed period between 6:10 to 13:10. This is the result of problems with financing.
