1. "New York Times": New Amiga to come late this year for 700 US-Dollars (approx. 760.87 Euros)

According to the "New York Times" the first AmigaNG computers are expected to be available late this year. The machine named "Amiga One" will be manufactured by a third party using available hardware components and might cost approx. 700 US-Dollars (approx. 760.87 Euros).

More informations can be found in a detailed article, which can be found on http://www.nytimes.com/library/tech/00/06/circuits/articles/22amig.html. To read it, a free registration is required.

Notice: Meanwhile, the date for the new computer given in the "New York Times" was confirmed by Amiga.
