3. Discovered a Bug in serial.device of AmigaOS 3.5 (by Ralf Eberlein)

»After 14 hours of fruitless attempts with Napalm (no problem with MFC = MultiFaceCard) I discovered that the OS 3.5 serial.device is the one creating the problems (especially on a 060). It turns up as an instability during the link-game (Recovering data).

Now with the artser.device v37.6 at 19600 Baud everything is ok, really quick and STABLE!!! Changed the name of artser.device into serial.device and with help of a hex-editor the name inside of the device from "artser" to "serial", too. Now, it is FULLY COMPATIBLE without changes to the software, if you used the serial.device with it or if the device cannot be chosen by the software.

Ralf Eberlein <R_TEAM@t-online.de>«
