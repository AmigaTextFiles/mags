Preface for the readers of AMIGA aktuell International

Welcome, to the latest edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

This edition should contain less mistakes than the previous ones, due to our two new language assistants Judy and John from Seedney, Australia. The correct all the stuff they get from us, because all of us, who are translating, are Germans and so English is not our native language. Thank you, Judy and John for all the work you do!

If you, dear reader, just read this and think- "Hmmm. Well, actually I could join the team and write some nice articles..." -- Don't hesistate and get in touch with us (AaktInt@gmx.de or aakt@gmx.de). If it is just one article you have - fine. If you have two or more - better. If you want to write on a regular basis - incredible!

Well, not everyone is good in writing articles or has the time to do so. We understand that. But we don't just hope on articles, but on feedback. So, just write us an eMail or a letter how you like (or dislike) AMIGA aktuell (International) and why. We are always interested in feedback.

Now, I better stop torturing you with such a boring preface, so you can begin reading...

Have fun with this edition,

yours

Martin Henke
