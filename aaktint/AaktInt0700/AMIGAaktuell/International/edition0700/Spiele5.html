5. Previews

Silencer please

Gobsmackamundo! Demo of Silencer has been released - just a walk around you understand, not a lot you can do yet, but as the Yugoslav developer team said, this was just a presentation of the game engine.

I would say it in other words: A presentation of the game graphics - and really impressive. Rendered game graphics in full colour, that just says more more more more. And what if you find in the ReadMe file that the full version will support PowerPC, AHI and graphics boards? The developer team are looking for a distributor of the full game, shouldn't you take that next step??? Those of you who want a look at the demo can download it from http://afrodita.rcub.bg.ac.yu/~afront/SILENCER.lha. For the the URL challenged, there are a few nice screenshots in this edition of AMIGA-aktuell-International. If the team can keep their promises, I might die a happy man.

Joyride

It's been a long long long lonely vroom vroom but now there's a 3D car racer out there. The final Joyride demo version doesn't have a heap of rev-head things to set up, only has one course to play on, and you have to get used to the controls. Graphically Joyride looks fine and runs smoothly on my 68040 with AGA. The sounds also fit the game. The demo version is available in two different archives: the AGA version, and one for graphics boards. Available on the Aminet (a mere 3.5 MB download). In summary, the final Joyride demo version made a quite good impression and the game slots in nicely as good middle class.
