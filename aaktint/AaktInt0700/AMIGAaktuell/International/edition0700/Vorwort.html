Welcome, to the latest edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

First, I have to thank you for participating the survey. In the next edition I will comment the results. Attention: All of oyu, who used the online form and did not preclude a participation at AMIGA aktuell (International) are asked to contact me again by eMail (aakt@gmx.de).

The main event during the last week was the release of the Amiga SDK (Software Developer's Kit), which is required to write software for the next generation Amiga, already now. The first machine is expected late this year and to be named "Amiga One". More informations can be found in this edition of AMIGA aktuell International.
