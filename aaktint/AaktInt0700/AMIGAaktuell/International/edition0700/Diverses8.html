Various Amiga News

8. News In Brief

  • A few days ago the new version 3.5 of the chat program AmIRC has been released. According to VaporWare compared to v3.4 especially the CPU load has been decreased clearly, additionally there are further improvements and bug-fixes. Download is possible at http://www.vapor.com.

  • As well by VaporWare the new version 2.4 of the telnet client AmTelnet has been released. Especially the output speed when scrolling big texts is said to clearly have improved. Download of the update is possible http://www.vapor.com.

  • On http://www.yam.ch you can download the popular e-mail software YAM in the new version 2.1, now. But it is only is a minor update. Besides of a lot of bug-fixes new features are implemented, too.

  • Again, there is a new beta version of the well known FTP client AmiTradeCenter. The download is possible on the ATC homepage (http://amitradecenter.net).

  • On http://www.paulnolan.com/Amiga/download.html you can download Photogenics v4.5 release 83a. Attention: Currently AGA is not yet supported, but before long a relevant fix will be released.

  • The sound editor SoundFX in version 4.00 has come out. More details concerning improvements and aquisition of the software may be found on http://www.imn.htwk-leipzig.de/~kost/soundfx.html.

  • At Schatztruhe an update for the GlowIcons collection containing 200 new icons can be downloaded and may be envisaged as a preview for a planned next edition of GlowIcons Collection. Download is possible with http://www.schatztruhe.de/files/GlowIcons_Update.lha.

  • Of a new shareware CD burning software, CDWriter, a first public beta version is released. Download is possible via the URL http://www.students.cs.uu.nl/~wmestrom/files/CDWriter.lha.

  • Thomas Dellert, Manager of the hardware manufacturer DCE in Oberhausen, Germany, stated, that his company does not do any more Amiga-repairs since Juli,1. As well no telephonic support will be given any more. DCE wishes to concentrate fully upon production, as it was stated as reasons. This does not apply to products of DCE and Phase 5, those will be repaired in future.

  • The British distributor of Amiga accessories Eyetech founded a business branch Eye-Play, which is devoted to the publishing and the distribution of games for AmigaNG and Amiga Classic. The affiliated homepage (http://www.eye-play.com) is already online. Via http://www.eyetech.co.uk/eyeplay/news.html further informations concerning this offer may be looked up.

  • Martin Merz writes: »MI News: AppMI

    AppMI is a big collection of images for toolbars in GlowIcon design, which possibly could become a standard for the ClassicAmiga system.

    The collection may be aquired free of charge by programmers and every interested one, but to maintain the archive a registration is necessary and everyone publishing a product using the AppMI ought to concede me a copy of the full version of the programm.

    Because of this reason only registered users will get the archive.

    Now, the registration site for AppMI is online, the release is considered at end of Juli 2000.
