10. Emulator news

On 27th of October Mathias "AmiDog" released the version 0,08 Rev 5 of the Playstation emulator FPSE. There are several changes in this version, among other things the possibility to decode MDEC films in grey as well as in 24bit mode on CyberGraphX.

Christian Bauer, author of the Mac emulators ShapeShifter and Basilisk II, reworked the homepage of the latter. It now appears in a completely new outfit even if there is no update for the emulator on it.
