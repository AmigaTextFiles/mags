News about the AmigaNG

9. AmigaNG software overview (by Thorsten Schölzel)

First: Thorsten Schölzel has stopped looking after the AmigaNG program-list. So we're searching for someone who wants his job. If you are interested, please write to aakt@aakt.de

Since Amiga, Inc released the AmigaSDK (Software Developer's Kit), everybody has the chance to develop programs for the next generation Amiga, to port them, or adapt them. We want to track these developments with our regular AmigaNG program survey.

Below you will find a list of AmigaNG-programs released since the AmigaSDK became available.
