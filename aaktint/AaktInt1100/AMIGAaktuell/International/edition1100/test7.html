7. Quick test: CD-ROM »Scene Archives Volume 10« (by Sylvester M. Beck)

APC&TCP (http://www.apc-tcp.de) has released a new "Scene Archives", composed by Tony Hasselbach. As usual, the 10th volume contains only the best stuff from the scene. The CD-ROM is filled to the brim. Also new is a search tool for the entire series, but it doesn't really make it easier to find a particular demo ...

Carsten Schroder kindly provided me with a sample CD [... who in turn got it from APC&TCP - many thanks; sez Carsten]

So let's take a closer look at the CD:

After inserting the CD we find an icon like a diskette on our Workbench. After double-clicking it, we see the following:

As you can see, everything is described in the accompanying guides - not! You are confronted with a list of demos, demopacks, slideshows, music demos and so on.


I could not make out what of these things is really included on this CD. And I didn't have the time and leisure to dig through the whole CD. So I picked a few things out and.... only found excellent stuff. 
 

Result:

If you are looking for one or another demo etc., you got to have the "Scene archives". I can only recommend it to every demo-fan.

Title: Scene Archives #10 Composing: Tony Hasselbach, Australian Publisher: APC&TCP, Übersee Price: 29,95 DM Test computer: A1200 PPC, 34 MB, 1.6 GB HD, 24 x CD-ROM
