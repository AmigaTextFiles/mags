5. News from Airsoft Softwair

Airsoft Softwair (http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de) announces:

»On the Airsoft Softwair Homepage yesterday five new configs for Rainboot3 were released (Aladdin Boot, Baack Boot, Beatbox Boot, Lion King Boot and Windows Sucks Boot). The Rainboot3 configs may be downloaded right away from www.airsoftsoftwair.de.«

A new version of the Amiga CD32 Games Install Kit has just been released on the Airsoft Softwair Homepage. Version 3.9 is a major update and now supports many more games.
