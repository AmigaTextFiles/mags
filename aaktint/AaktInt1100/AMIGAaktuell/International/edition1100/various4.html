Amiga Arena - The Fire is back!

TextloaderNG keyfile now free for all Amiga Users!! Every visitor of the Amiga Arena may download the keyfile for TextloaderNG.

Previously the keyfile only was free of charge for customers of the Amiga Arena CD-ROM, I'd like to thank Pascal Gisin for the general release of the keyfile!

TextloaderNG is a hex-editor with high functionality! E.g.

  • Filter for Word, Wordperfect, MSWork, ANSI, FinalWriter, Wordworth and several more

  • Remove HTML-tags

  • Converter

  • Text encoder

TextloaderNG will be developed further and be usable without a keyfile.«

FLM at Special Price!

In cooperation with Dennis Unger FLM may be registered in the Amiga Arena at an unique bargain price! FLM is the foreign language program, which may be used as dictionary, for the translation of texts and for learning of vocabularies. FLM contains several English/German dictionaries with approx. 175000 entries. FLM is one of the tools I have always wanted for the Amiga! Leafing through paper dictionaries is a thing of the past! FLM may be registered exclusively via Amiga Arena at 10 DM! (Normally 25 DM).

It really would be a pity if this development was cancelled! Please support the developer!
