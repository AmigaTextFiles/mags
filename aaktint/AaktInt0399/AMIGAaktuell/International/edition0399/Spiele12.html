12. Preview: Virtual Grand Prix

Within the last second I got the hot demo of Virtual Grand Prix. This game caused stirs those days, when the first demo was published with the title Alien F1. This game was a formula 1 game of the extra class. Forget everything you saw on the Amiga, that was formula 1 and watch this game! Fully texturized tracks, stands, original courses etc. Virtual Grand Prix is on the best way to become a hit. Of course, graphics boards are supported, too. According to the author Paolo Cattani the game is ready, but some bugs have to be removed, then it will be distributed by Epic Marketing. The demo can be downloaded from http://www.grid9.net/~wobble/AF1new.lzx. Nicht verpassen!
