6. First OS3.5 Demonstration at Amiga International, Inc.'s Office

Thanks to Haage & Partner and their partner companies, a first demonstration of OS3.5 was shown on March 5, 1999 at AMIGA International, Inc.'s office in Langen, Germany. Thomas Raukamp of "AMIGA OS"magazine was attending the demonstration. The magazine will have a report on that first demonstration in its April issue.
