Games section

2. Summons

Dear Reader,

The AMIGA aktuell international Games section needs your help - please let me know any news about games. I am always interested in news, tips or anything to do with Amiga and games. Criticism is also welcome - please tell me if there's something you don't like. I am not perfect, either, unless you lend me your ideas.

Dear Programmers and Software firms,

There are only a few publications left that carry information on AMIGA games. It makes sense to use every opportunity to bring news, previews and tests to the attention of loyal AMIGA fans. I believe AMIGA aktuell is one of the better opportunities available. It is distributed in many formats and could put you in touch with some thousands of potential customers. It is up to you: support me and my work and get your games known out there. Please send me news of your games, as well as any demo versions, or samples of your games. In return, I will carry out an extended test of your game.
