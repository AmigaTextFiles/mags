9. News about the CD³²-Alliance

CD³²-Alliance (http://home.t-online.de/home/amiga-freak) informs:

»Discussion forum online

Unfortunately there is a pure CD³² discussion forum missing. This is why we decided to create one in the CD³²-Corner, so all the CD³²-scene might meet there.

CD³² software in Aminet now available from the CD³²-Corner.

To expand our services, you can now get into the Aminet via the CD³²-Corner to download CD³² software. Just test it!«

»* Die CD³²-Alliance closes down!

With a heart of sadness we have to tell you, that the CD³²-Alliance resigns to August 14, 2000. So we stop our activities after eight long months, we created something unique for the CD³². We're talking about a place in the Internet for this device and its users; our CD³²-Corner.

Unfortunately we were unable to have as many visitors as we expected. However, we are proud on our work. But don't worry, the CD³²-Corner as it is will remain online.

Your CD³²-Alliance
