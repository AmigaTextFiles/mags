Amiga PowerPC

2. News in Brief (by Oliver Tacke)

ppclibemu, which emulates the ppc.library from phase5's powerUP kernel under WarpOS was released in revision 0.8.
[http://devnull.owl.de/~frank/]

The WarpPNG.datatype, which is for WarpOS (only WarpOS, not powerUP!) is now available in revision V44.5.
[http://www.nanunanu.org/~oliver/]

For the favorite mp3 player AmigaAMP from Thomas Wenzel there are two new plugins (Water plugin and Subspace). Both require a PowerPC and WarpUp V4.
[http//:amigaamp.amiga-software.com/]

The PPC boards manufactured from DCE currently have two limitations: In the moment, only the 200 MHz type is available and there is no SCSI controller on the board.
[http://www.unet.univie.ac.at/~a9909727/]
