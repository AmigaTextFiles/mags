1. Supplement: the Elbox PCI project presentation by Steffen Haeuser in AMIGA aktuell International July edition

Some readers did not detect that the concluding comment following Steffen's article was written by the editor. Please note that supplied articles end with name and email address of the author (as was the case with the Elbox PCI article).

Steffen comments on my conclusion (which I still stand by):

»I cannot agree with the wording of the conclusion. At the Neuss fair my conversation with Elbox convinced me that their development was 100% serious and would be concluded successfully in a very short time. On the PCI+G3 against AmigaOne: The AmigaOne is in the future. In the meantime companies need to sell software, but how what chance do they have if there are no hardware advances now? A G3+PCI could - assuming downward compatibility - give a modest classic emulation of the AmigaOne and raise the "StartSoftware Capability" of the new Amiga System. Such a development makes sense.

Steffen Haeuser <magicsn@birdland.es.bawue.de>«
