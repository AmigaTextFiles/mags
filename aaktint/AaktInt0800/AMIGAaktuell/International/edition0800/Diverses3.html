3. Meeting in Neukirchen-Vluyn, Germany

Volker Mohr informs:

»"Classic Computing 2000"

On Saturday, September 2, 2000 from 10:00 a.m. through 4:00 p.m. there will be a user meeting for all those, who are friends of the Amiga, Atari, other 68k computers as well as the 8bit computers, in Neukirchen-Vluyn, Germany.

Announced guests are e.g. the team of Amiga news (www.amiga-news.de, the airbrush artist Rolf Tingler www.airbrush-design.com and the 8bit Nirvana (www.zock.com as well as numerous dealers and clubs. Special guest will be Petro Tyschtschenko from Amiga Germany, who is expected for the afternoon.

After that, users and dealers meet in the same room for a party (you have to have reserved).

Booth costs and entrance fees (5.00 DEM (approx. 2.55 Euros) per person) are just covering the costs, because the main goal is the fun. More dealers, but also private sellers are welcome! It is recommended to reserve as soon as possible.


More informations can be found on www.classic-computing.de, 
by eMail webmaster@classic-computing.de,
or per fax on +49 (0)69 - 7 91 22 44 80."
