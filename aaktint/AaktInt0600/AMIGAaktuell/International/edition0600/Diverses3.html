3. CeBIT Home cancelled

It seems, that AMIGAs decision, not to be present on the World of Alternatives in Neuss, but the CeBIT Home, was not lucky. Because the consumer show, which was planned for this summer and was to happen in Leipzig, Germany, was cancelled due to little interest of exhibitors, a few days ago.

So it is doubtful, if AMIGA will be present with a booth on any Germany computer show, this year.
