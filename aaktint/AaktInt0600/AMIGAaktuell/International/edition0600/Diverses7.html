»Amiga Arena News

Amiga Arena CD-ROM news

The concept of the Amiga Arena will be on CD-ROM for the first time! The "Amiga Arena CD ROM edition", as the CD is named, contains a unique offer of multiple use to homepages and their software! Special prices, full version, SE versions, large homepage data on CD-ROM for the first time! (Workshops, manuals, plug ins etc...) More informations about the content of the CD to come!

Amiga Arena CD Rom Edition - World premiere

The Amiga Arena CD Rom Edition will be distributed from APC&TCP and will be presented on the Amiga show in Neuss (June 10/11) for the first time!
