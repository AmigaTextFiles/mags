17. Emulator News

A new team of developers was founded to continue the development of the cancelled Amiga emulator Fellow. This includes also porting the currently DOS-only emulator to the WIN32 platform. The official homepage is on http://fellow.sourceforge.net".

This month, Mathias "AmiDog" Roslund released a hard improved revision of his port of the Atari-Lynx emulator "Handy". This revision is much more intuitive and supports CyberGraphX and Nostalgia. It can be downloaded from the popular homepage of the author, which is http://www.amidog.com. In addition there were new revisions of the Sony Playstation emulator SOPE and the Sega Mega Drive Emulator AmiGenerator released, this month.

On the website of the NES emulator A\NES there is a summons about the future of the emulator. In addition the website got a rework and is all new. The website can be found on http://www.emuclassics.com/ANES/. There you find also the new revision 1.18 of the CyberGraphX version of A\NES.

The popular multi emulation GUI Nostalgia revision 1.75 can be found in the Aminet. Now, it also supports the Atari Lynx emulator Handy.

And last, but not least this month a new revision of my SNES9xGUI was released released, which supports the new ChunkyPPC mode of the future WarpSNES. This should already be available, but the release has been delayed.
