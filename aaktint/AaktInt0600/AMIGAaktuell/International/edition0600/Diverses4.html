»APC&TCP releases another CD-ROM on the Amiga show in Neuss, Germany. More informations can be found on our homepage or directly from us. After the show the CD-ROM will be available in the shops as all our products:

Welcome in the cupboard of your new Workbench. The new "Digital Make-Up"-CD is an add-on-CD with a load of Tools and utilities for improving the design and tuning up the Amiga operating system and is arranged in themes. Here you don't just find powerful tools, that speed up the AmigaOS and simplify the daily work - there is also graphics material for the common gray Workbench. In addition there is software to make voice output possible. Of course, the desired voice and sound samples are not missing. Many datatypes, latest libraries, modern fonts, new commands for the C-directory and numerous other system enhancements put any Amiga system to the right course. New, nice screen savers and many icons sets for the NewIcon system or the brand new GlowIcons get a modern outfit for the Workbench. Partially unreleased software can be found on this CD-ROM. For the new AmigaOS revision 3.5 there is an extra directory where you find the latest tools for the latest revision.

Just a double click on "Quickstart" and you are inside the "Digital Make-Up CD" !

The "Digital Make-Up CD" is optimized for AmigaOS revisions 3.0 to 3.5. The main part of the software on this CD-ROM can be started directly.

System requirements: Amiga with 68020-Prozessor, 2 Mbyte ChipRAM, hard disk, CD-ROM drive, AmigaOS revision 2.05

Recommended: Amiga with 68040 processor and PPC processor, 2 Mbyte ChipRAM, 4 Mbyte FastRAM, hard disk, CD-ROM drive, AGA graphics chipset or graphics board, sound card, AmigaOS revision 3.5«
