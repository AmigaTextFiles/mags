6. News in Brief

  • In matters of our own I want to direct your attention to a survey, which is very important for the future of this magazine and which is included in this edition of AMIGA aktuell International. I ask ALL of you to have some minutes and to participate. Thank you.

  • On June 3, a new edition of AMIGAs online magazine Amiga World has been released, which contains many informations especially for AmigaNG developers. It can be found on http://www.amiga.com/press/zine/ in HTML format. A PDF version will be made available, soon.

  • AMIGA installed a support mailing list for AmigaOS 3.5. To subscribe you have to send an eMail containing the word "Subscribe" in the body to os35list-request@amiga.com. geschickt werden. After successful subscription you can give your comments on this address: os35list@amiga.com.

  • Currently another bugfix update for AmigaOS 3.5 is in work. An exact release date has not been specified, yet.
