1. Amiga products available in shops again

Translation of Haage & Partner's (http://www.haage-partner.com) information:

»Augsburg, Germany, June 2000. The Weltbild Verlag GmbH located in Augsburg, Germany offers from June of this year Amiga products from HAAGE&PARTNER, EPIC Marketing and Amiga Inc. With more than 4.5 million of sent catalogues a range of customers, which is extremely wide for Amiga circumstances, is to direct their attention on products like the new AmigaOS, AmigaWriter, ArtEffect and various games from EPIC Marketing. Also the 122 shops in Germany and 30 in Austria and Switzerland will offer all the software titles. If the sale of Amiga software becomes successful, the Weltbild Verlag thinks about continuing this campaign.
