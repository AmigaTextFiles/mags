2. News in brief

  • Haage & Partner published this news on their PPC pages (http://www.haage-partner.com) ist

    »07-Jan-99: Titan leave both one's options open

    All future products of Titan will offer in addition to a PowerUP a WarpUP support, so the user can choose his favored kernel. The game Claws of the Devil will also support Warp3D. In the near future there will be WarpUP versions of the titles Candy Factory Pro and Fantastic Dreams. www.titancomputer.de« (Sourcee: Haage & Partner)

  • In a newsgroup posting Sam Jordam, the developer of the announced 68k emulation informed about the speed to be expected: According to him PPC604 based systems could reach the speed of a 68030/25 MHz (with programs with many arithmetic commands) up to the speed of a 68040/40 MHz (with memory intense applications). Standard applications should run with the speed of a 68040/25 MHz.
  • After AkJFIF there are the PNG- resp. TIFF datatypes AkPNG and AkTIFF with optional WarpOS support available. They can be downloaded from http://wdo.de/ark/.
