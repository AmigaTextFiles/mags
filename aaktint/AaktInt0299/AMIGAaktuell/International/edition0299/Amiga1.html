1. OS 3.5: Frequently Asked Questions

Amiga OS 3.5 Frequently Asked Questions Q.When will 3.5 be completed? A. 3.5 is scheduled for release the first half of 1999. 3.5 is still on schedule. Q. How much will it cost? A. 3.5 has a targeted list price for USD$49.95 Q. Where are the RTG and RTA support? A. These are features that are no longer part of the core of 3.5. We are evaluating these features. Q. Will there be PPC support? A. Yes. 3.5 will have PPC co-processor support. Q. Will it run on a Macintosh? A. No. Q. Will 3.5 support MUI? A. As 3.1 does currently. Q. Will 3.5 have new icon support? A. Yes. More specifics will follow. Q. Do you plan on to bundle the 3.1 ROM with the 3.5 upgrade? A. This will be up to the reseller. We are not intending to offer a 3.1/3.5 bundle. Q. Is this the last upgrade for the Amiga Classic? A. There are no other upgrades planned for the Classic line. Q. Is QNX playing some role in 3.5? A. No. QNX is only working with Amiga on the NG Amiga. Q. Why is the progress bar gone? A. The progress bar is being updated with the latest information. As contracts are signed and executed we will update the bar and repost.
