4. Aminet 29 announced

Aminet CD 29 - 2/99
Aminet CD 29, dated February 1999, contains nearly 1 gigabyte of (uncompressed)
software in thousands of archives. Since the release of Aminet CD 28 over 500 MB
new software has appeared. The current edition includes the full version of
ArtEffect V1.5 and a demoversion of ArtEffect V2.6.
