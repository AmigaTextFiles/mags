5. Amiga Show to be held in Alabama

Feb 1999,
For immediate release: Bessemer State Technical Collage, Bessemer AL, USA is home to a one time Amiga show. Anyone is invited, and especially those in the Birmingham/Tuscaloosa areas of Alabama. The show will consist of three seminars, to educate and teach non-Amigans as well as those versed in our beloved platform's ins and outs. These presentations will be: February 5, 1999

@12:30 Multimedia: The Power Tool for Graphics & Video
by: Robert Hamilton

@ 1:45 Unique Solutions in Computer Hardware
by: Joe Torre

@ 3:00 P.C. Innovations: The Amiga Impact
by: Aaron Ruscetta

We will also feature */A giant 10 foot screen Amiga Demo */!!

Bessemer State Tech is located off of Interstate 20-59 exit 108, and then across from the Bessemer Carraway Medical Center.

For any questions, contact dave_111@bellsouth.net or targhan@geocities.com

Special thanks to each of our presenters and to Darreck Lisle of Amiga Inc. for getting us permission to use logos and names. Also, to E.S. Productions for allowing us to use some of his artwork!
