1. Results of the last competition

That the attendence of the edition of the January edition was much better than a month before was easy to gess, if you consider you could win Wildfire\7. But that we came into the three-digits-area I didn't dare to expect. Exactly 109 readers attended.

To keep the attendence on this high level there is a piece of hardware to be won (which is nearly an understatement: It's nothing less the brand new interface board Hypercom 3+. Our thanks goes to the firm VMC, which gave it to us for our competition.

And don't think, that our competition higlights are exhausted for this year...

But now to the results:

Standard question:  Which well known firm is going to introduce a new 3D-GFX add-on for the Amiga 
  

Answer: 'Village Tronic' was the correct answer.

Master question:  Is this add-on able to work in an A2000 with PicassoIV graphics board according to the manufacturer? (Please explain!) 
  
Answer: The correct answer is 'no'. According to Village Tronic you need a PicassoIV graphics board 
 in ZorroIII mode. The A2000 just offers ZorroII slots. 
  

Question of opinion: How will the Amiga develop in common in 1999?

The results:

Answer Anzahl der Nennungen 
  
1 (Better than in 1998) 62 
2 (As good/bad as in 1998) 38 
3 (Worse than in 1998) 8 
  

Another attendent could not decide between 1 and 2.

Conclusion:

The results are obvious. Most of you expect 1999 to become a better year for the Amiga than the last. Let's hope this becomes true!
We had a similar result to this question a year ago, when we asked it for the year 1998.
