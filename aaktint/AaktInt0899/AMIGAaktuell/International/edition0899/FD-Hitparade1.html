This month the participation was quite low, just 5 persons sent their charts to me. Why there are still such a few persons, who use their chance on one more vote makes we wonder. Just for clarification:

EVERYBODY, who sends his Top 5 for at least 3 (THREE) sections to me, gets one vote more from Carsten for the competition. So if you take some time for writing your Top 5 in three sections and send it me the same mail each month, you get one vote more each month - there is no easier way...

Especially low is the participation with the online mags, even if some new ones were released recently.

The online participation is unfortunately used very seldom (but there are mails, which are not filled in, quite often...), even if especially this one is for the people who are lazy about writing letters...

However, these are the latest charts!

(A) - Place 
(B) - Place of the last month 
(C) - Program name 
(D) - Revision number (not in the CD-ROM-charts)

 named disk is just one source, which means that are other 
 there may be other sources.  
 Aminet and other sources are only named, if there is no disk or 
 CD-ROM with the program on it known. 
(F) - Points, the program received. 
 (The judgement: the 1st place of an evaluation gets 5 points, 
 the following get a point less, each.) 
  

Regarding the order: Each place is ordered with the points. If there is a push, the place is made using the number of the evaluations and then the position in the Top 10 of the last month. If this is not possible it is in alphabetical order.

* Remark of the editor:  Due to the fact that the FD-charts are a part of AMIGA aktuell (international) and so most participants are readers of Amiga aktuell international the result is not representative. This is also true for magazines, which include AMIGA aktuell. 

How can someone participate?

The FD-charts come with each edition of Amiga aktuell international. Rankings have to reach me up to the 22nd. Later incoming mails are considered in the next edition.

Just send an eMail, a letter, a disk letter or anything else to my address:

In addition to your address you should name your 5 favorite games, 5 favorite apllications, 5 favorite FD-CD-ROMS (as existent). I further need your 5 favorite Internet addresses and multimedia files.

If the programs aren't part of the Top 10 I eventually need the author/ creator as well as the source of the program/ the file.

At last you should name the revision number of the program, if possible.

To take part of the competition with one ticket you have to evaluate in at least three categories. The winners and prizes are published within the AMIGA aktuell international competition.

The judges decision is final (I hope :-)
