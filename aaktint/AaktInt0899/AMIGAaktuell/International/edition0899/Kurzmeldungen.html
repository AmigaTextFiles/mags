AMIGA news

  • After long waiting the full version of the eMail software YAM 2.0 was released. It can be downloaded from http://www.yam.ch.

  • Holger Kruse, the author of the currently under development Java package Daytona announced, that the release of the software will be delayed approximately until autumn due to the work on the OS 3.5 version of his TCP/IP stack Miami.

  • Village Tronic looked for a name for their Voodo extension for their graphics board Picasso IV - and found one: The addon will be named 'ParaGlide'.
