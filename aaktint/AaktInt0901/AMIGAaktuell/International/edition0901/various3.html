3. News from Airsoft Softwair

»A major update for RB2Exe is available now! Version 1.5 allows you to use all great features of Rainboot3+ in your executable shows! Additionally the memory routines have been optimized and the generated executables' memory usage was reduced by almost 60 percent (!). Also, the GUI's functionality was improved and bugs were fixed. Online help is available now too. This version is only available for owners of the "The Best Of Airsoft Softwair Gold Edition CD-ROM". For all users who own the CD-ROM: RB2Exe v1.5 is available from the protected download area which can be accessed with your serial code!«

»The Airsoft Softwair Homepage is now available in a separate German version. This version can be accessed on http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de. The English version is now online at http://www.airsoftsoftwair.com/. Please change your bookmarks if you want to read the English version and you have http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de in your bookmarks!«
