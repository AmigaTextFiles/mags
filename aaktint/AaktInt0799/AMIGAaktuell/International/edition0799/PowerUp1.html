PowerUp-to-date

1. News in brief

  • Fusion PPC made it: After Microcode Solutions promised to develop a PowerPC version of the Macintosh emulator Fusion after 500 binding advanced bookings were being made this number was reached within a few weeks.

    For a PowerPC version of the PC emulator PCx, which was also considered it was less good on the closing date of Amiga aktuell. The current status, which can be read on the homepage of the manufacturer (http://www.microcode-solutions.com) shows just 336 interested people.


  • Warptrans is an announced full text translation program for PowerPC Amiga, which is going to offer also voice recognition(!). More informations can be found on http://www.w-9.de/HTML/warptrans.html.

  • Martin Tauchmann writes: »There will be a new ixemul.library and a new Oberon compiler, that is able to produce 68k and PowerPC code.« The download can be made from Martin's homepage (http://computer.freepage.de/tauchmann/devel.html).
