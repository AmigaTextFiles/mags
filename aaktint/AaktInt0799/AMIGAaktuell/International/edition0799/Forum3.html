3. Amiga in the PC Praxis magazine (by Heiko Krappmann)

»In the latest edition of the (German) PC-Praxis magazine there is an article about the Amiga.

On one hand they write how Commodore went into bankrupcy, but they also explain, that this wasn't the fault of the Amiga. On the other hand they explain, what Amiga, Inc. is going to develop soon.

PC-Praxis wants to continue writing about the Amiga as I was told in a telephone call, eventually with one page per month, but it can't be realized in the moment.

Heiko Krappmann <claudia.krappmann@planet-interkom.de>«
