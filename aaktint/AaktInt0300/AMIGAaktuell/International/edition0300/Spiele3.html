Hello, dear reader!

Welcome to a new edition of your AMIGA aktuell International games section. This edition was planned to be larger in size (I planned to introduce some more games), but unfortunately this was not possible due to a lack of time (February only has 29 days). So it is very likely to be in next month. Also in other means this edition is quite short. Neither Heiko Garrelts nor Andreas Koch have been able to bring anything productive into this edition. But I am happy about the fact, that Wolfgang W. Wobler introduced two games for the Macintosh. Then I wrote a review of the game 'Desert Racing of BarDos'.

This month there is a new section: Ingo Schmitz decided to introduce a section with informations/tests about old games, which have been installed to hard disk using JST and/or WHDLoad. He also asks for your help. It would be best to directly read his preface.

Unfortunately there is also bad news: Ewald Moeller, the master of cheats lost his data due to a hard disk crash. This is why the cheats service is no longer possible. This shows how important it is to back up someones data on a regulary basis. You can never trust technology...

Well, now have fun with this edition of the AMIGA aktuell International games section and all good to you!
