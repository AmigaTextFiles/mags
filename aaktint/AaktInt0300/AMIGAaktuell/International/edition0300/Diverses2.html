2. Aminet-CD 35 with special version of Digital Almanac II released

Schatztruhe (http://www.schatztruhe.de) informs:

»Aminet 35 CD-ROM is available! Suggested Retail Price DM 25.00 [Euro 12.78] Subscription price DM 19.80 US$1 = DM 1.95 given current exchange rates. [Euro 10.12] «

»Product information

Aminet CD 35, dated February 2000, contains 1 gigabyte (uncompressed) of software in thousands of archives. Since the release of Aminet CD 34 more than 500 MB new software has appeared. The current edition features the a special edition of Digital Almanac II.
