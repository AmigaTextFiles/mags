* Remark of the editor:  Due to the fact that the FD-charts are a part of AMIGA aktuell (international) and so most participants are readers of Amiga aktuell international the result is not representative. This is also true for magazines, which include AMIGA aktuell. 

How can someone participate?

The FD-charts come with each edition of Amiga aktuell international. Rankings have to reach me up to the 22nd. Later incoming mails are considered in the next edition.

Just send an eMail, a letter, a disk letter or anything else to my address:

 Markus Castro Y Castro 
 Ostpreussenstr. 26 
 25764 Wesselburen 
 Germany 
E-Mail: FD-Hitparade@Gmx.net 
Tel: +49 (0)4833/3728 
  

In addition to your address you should name your 5 favorite games, 5 favorite apllications, 5 favorite FD-CD-ROMS (as existent). I further need your 5 favorite Internet addresses and multimedia files.

If the programs aren't part of the Top 10 I eventually need the author/ creator as well as the source of the program/ the file.

At last you should name the revision number of the program, if possible.

To take part of the competition with one ticket you have to evaluate in at least three categories. The winners and prizes are published within the AMIGA aktuell international competition.

The judges decision is final (I hope :-)
