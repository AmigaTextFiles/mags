Amiga PowerPC

Amiga PowerPC

1. In a nutshell (by Oliver Tacke)

For Voyager there's a new image decoder for MorphOS requiring Voyager as version 3.3.35.
[ftp://ftp.vapor.com/pub/voyager/vimgdec_morphos_14.0.lzx]

The WarpJPEG datatype by Oliver Roberts has been released as version 44.13 and now supports MorphOS and 68k-Amigas.
[http://www.nanunanu.org/~oliver/]

Andreas Kleinert propably won't continue working on his akDatatypes for MorphOS because he doesn't have enough time.
[http://www.ar-kleinert.de/]

A S.u.S.E. PPC 6.3 evaluation CD-ROM for the Amiga has been published. An update to V6.4 is possible.
[http://home.germany.net/101-109037/index.html]

The development of another PlayStation emulator called "YaQuZa" has begun. It shall be executable on powerUP, WarpOS and MorphOS.
[http://frogger.rules.pl/yqz/]

UAE PPC 0.8.6 has been ported to MorphOS.
[http://uae.meanmachine.ch/]

Frogger has been published as version 1.62 beta. It supports WarpOS and powerUP and is going to support MorphOS soon as well.
[http://frogger.rules.pl/downloads.html]

For all Gameboy fans there now is the Gameboy emulator GBE in version V0.4.8b.
[ftp://ftp.amidog.com/pub/emu/GBE.lha]

For WarpOS under OS3.0 or higher the WarpGIF datatype has been released. It's said to be twice as fast as the OS3.5 GIF datatype.
[http://www.soft-ware.de/dalmanac/]
