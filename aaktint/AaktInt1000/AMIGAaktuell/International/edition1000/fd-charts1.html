First, the meaning of the following lines:


(A) - Placement
(B) - Placement last month
(C) - Name of the program
(D) - Versionnumber(not the CD-ROMs charts)
(E) - Where to get the program. DFUE and Aminet as sources of supply
 will only be given, if there's no disk or cd rom known, where you will
 find the program. In the FD-CDROM charts you will here find the name
 of the author or distributor
(F) - Points, the program (or serie) reached. Pointsystem:
 The first place gets 5 points, the following one one less
 than the previous.
 

All places are ordered by points. If there are equal points, the number decides the rang. If this is impossible, the alphabet decides, or I. ;-)

How to take part of the FD-charts?

The FD-charts appear with every AMIGA-aktuell. Placements have to be by me till the 24. of the month. Later incoming mails will be posted with the following edition.

Simply post a mail,a letter, a disk or something else to my adress:


Markus Castro Y Castro 
Ostpreussenstr. 26 
25764 Wesselburen 
Germany 
 
E-Mail: FD-Hitparade@Gmx.net 
  

It is also possible to take part online by using the BBS CCS-base or the AMIGA-aktuell-pages in the WWW.

Beside your adress, you should post your vote about 3 rubrics or more. Preferably complete :)

If these things are not part of the top 10, I also need the names of the authors and from where you got the program or file.

At least you should give me the version number of your favorite, if possible.

To be part of the raffle, you have to vote for 3 or more categories. The winner and the prices will be presented in the AMIGA-aktuell.

Markus Castro/ FD-Hitparade@Gmx.net
