2. Petro Tyschtschenko has founded own company

According to a report of amiga-news.de, which is currently available in German only (http://www.amiga-news.de/archiv/000912.shtml), Petro Tyschtschenko has founded an own company, which targets to get people from all over the world to companies for software development (accomodation, catering etc.) as a so-called "All-in-one service".

It is currently uncertain, if this new founded company has any influence on the work of Petro Tyschtschenko for AMIGA Germany.
