7. AmigaNG-Program-survey (by Thorsten Schölzel)

After Amiga Inc released the AmigaSDK (Software Developer's Kit), everybody has been able to develop programs for the next generation Amiga, or do ports or adaptations.

We want to contribute to this evolution with a regular AmigaNG-program-survey from Thorsten Schölzel (amiga-freak@t-online.de

Below you will find a list of AmigaNG-programs which have been realeased since the AmigaSDK became available.
