Various Amiga News

5. News from Airsoft Softwair

Airsoft Softwair (http://www.airsoftsoftwair.de) informs:

»On the Airsoft Softwair Homepage just now Rainboot3 was released. It is a Rainboot2 version which is extremely extended. Rainboot3 now makes it possible, what Rainboot users dreamed about since a long time: Unlimited animations, modules, cycles, user controls may be installed, there are new fade options, screens may be scrolled in and much more.

The update is a must for any Rainboot fan (and all those who want to become one) and is ready for download.«
