AMIGA aktuell International - games section

4. Game news from all over the world

  • Wasted Dreams 2
    Digital Dreams Entertainment announced Wasted Dreams 2 for the Amiga. Last year the first part was released and was a big success.
  • News from Alive Mediasoft
    For 2000 Alive Mediasoft announced new PC ports. The targeted conversions are Descent 2, Elite 3 and Quake World. Each of the games will also support the PPC and Warp3D.
  • Tales from Heaven
    Epic Marketing announced, that Tales from Heaven is ready and in the production facilities. The first copies might become available, soon. Tales from Heaven is the spectacular 3D Jump'n' Run in the style of Super Mario 64.
  • Nightlong
    There are just 91 pre-orders for the PPC port from clickBOOM. Those, who are interested in a development should order it at once, so the development can be made. On the clickBOOM homepage a new trailer of the game can be downloaded.
  • Crystal Software brings Amiga games in over 30,000 shops world wide
    "How can that be?", you might ask. The trick is, that on one CD-ROM there are the Amiga and the PC version. So the CD-ROMs will be sold in common PC shops.
  • Quake Ports
    In the Aminet you find some Amiga ports of Quake, which also support PPCs with WarpOS.
  • SeaSide
    The English versio nof SeaSide was released from APC&TCP this month.
  • Simon the Sorcerer 2
    Epic Marketing announced, that about 400 pre-orders have been made for Simon the Sorcerer 2. To release the game for the Amiga it have to be 500 orders. So, if you haven't placed your order, you should do this as soon as possible.
  • Virtual Ball Fighters preview released
    This month a preview of was released. In addition the developers of XTeam Software announced, that the game is already ready and will be released from Epic Marketing.
  • Subwar 2050 for legal download
    The Amiga Sector One made the AGA version of the game Subwar 2050 from Micropose available for free download. It can be downloaded from http://www.emucamp.com/amiga/
  • WHDLoad
    The WHDLoad website moved completely on http://www.whdload.org the complete archive of HD installers can be found, now. In addition revision 10.5 of WHDLoad was released.
  • News from Darkage Software
    Darkage Software announced to new games: Powerball, a simple puzzle game and a new CD-ROM called Amiga Classix 2, which will contain classic Amiga games. Amiga Classix 2 will be released in the end of February and Powerball is expected to be made available in March. The distributor will be Epic Marketing.
  • New games from Epic Marketing
    Epic Marketing released two new CD-ROM collections: The CD-ROM Adventures Lair is a CD-ROM with ten complete graphic adventures, which all have the Public Domain status. On the other CD-ROM named Mutantology there are also 10 games, which include some commercial games of Mutation Software (Tin Toy Adventure, Tommy Gun etc.).
  • Hyperion Software is looking for programmers
    Hyperion Software is looking for a C/C++ programmer for future projects. Skills in Warp3D, OpenGL and PowerPC would be very welcome.
