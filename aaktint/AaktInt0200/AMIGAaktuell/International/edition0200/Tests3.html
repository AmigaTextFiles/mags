3. Quicktest: "SimpleClock" by Sebastian Bauer (by Gerd Engelmann)

»I've been searching a long time for a clock for the workbench, but I didn't like anything I found.

But then I stumbled in Aminet over the program "SimpleClock" by Sebastian Bauer and was very pleased. It's very small and you can configure it for all your needs. All colors, hand sizes and shapes are adjustable, and what I liked best: You can make the background transparent and the window borderless. So it easily integrates into my workbench, without "spoiling" the whole screen.

You can also define a pattern for the background. And the whole thing is freeware, what more can you want? The only thing I miss is an alarm function. I will mail Sebastian about this.

Gerd Engelmann <G.Engelmann@t-online.de>«
