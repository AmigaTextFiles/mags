4. VMC leaves Amiga hardwar emarket / Sale to Individual Computers

VMC (http://www.vmc.de) informs:

»VMC will no longer produce hardware themselves

Early this year we decided no longer to produce or to develop hardware ourselves. VMC is going to concentrate on software development only and bring it on the market licenced by third parties.

HyperCOM 1 is not produced and sold any longer

The nearly legendary HyperCOM 1 interface board for the Amiga 1200 is no longer sold by VMC. All rights for further production as well as a software-licence was sold to Individual computers, which will offer this excellent product with an excellent support as SilverSurfer.

HyperCOM Plus series sold completely

From now on the products of the HyperCOM series is no longer produced or sold by VMC. The complete stock as well as a licence for further productions were sold to Individual computers. VMC will continue with software support and take care that functions without problems can be guaranteed. We would like to ask you to send your future orders to Individual Computers or to an Individual Computers dealer of your choice.
