The AMIGA aktuell International competition

1. Results of the last competition

There were 50 participants of the last competition, so there is an increase of participation compared with the month before.

Remember: Even, if you do not know the answer for the master question, you participate at the competition, if you answered the two other questions.

But now, the solution:


Standard question: Gateway sold AMIGA to Amino Development Corporation. Who is president of this company? 
  
Answer: Bill McEwen is president of the company, which renamed in the meantime to Amiga Incorporated. 
  
Master question: The Amiga patents were not sold in this deal. Why is Amino allowed to use them anyway? 
  
Antwort: Amino got licences for using the patents.
  
Question of opinion: How do you think about the take over of Amiga?

The results:


Answer Number of votes 
1 (very positive.) 30 
2 (quite positive.) 17 
3 (quite negative.) 1 
4 (very negative.) 0 
  

Two participants had no opinion in this matter.

Some participants commented the question of opinion, but the statements can be found in the section "News about AMIGA".

Conclusion:

The result is clear: An overwhelming majority is satisfied with the sales of the Amiga rights to Bill McEwen and Fleecy Moss. But the expectations from AMIGA Inc. might be quite high, too. The next month will show, if the faith of the users is paid.
