9. Mac corner

Summons

If someone run some games on a Mac emulator and sent me or Andreas Falkenhahn some lines about it then this someone would really help me because of my special situation of having to write articles without being able to fall back upon my Amiga. Alternatively, if you like then you may write other articles concerning the Mac corner . What about a brief comparison between Fusion and Shapeshifter concerning games, for example? Or what about your experiences which you have gained with programmes, dealers or other stuff? In addition I would be very grateful for ideas and criticism so that I know whether somebody reads my texts or not. But as long as nobody feels disturbed by them I will carry on working on the Mac corner...
