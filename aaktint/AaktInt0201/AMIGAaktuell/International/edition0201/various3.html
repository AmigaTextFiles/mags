Various Amiga news

3. News from the Amiga Arena

Amiga Arena (http://online-club.de/~ARENA/) informs:

»Amiga Arena News
(January 04, 2001)


Amiga Arena - Risiko Full Version

The Amiga Arena presents in cooperation with Frank Abbing (UAC Design) the complete "Risiko" in the final version 1.7! (Not available on the Aminet!) Risiko Amiga follows the usual rules of the original board game by Parker Bros. But Risiko additionally contains special rules - there are rebellions, earthquakes, blitzkriegs! Versions in English and German languages! For all friends of Risiko here is the best non-commercial Amiga version ready for download.«

»Amiga Arena News
(January 13, 2001)


Amiga Arena - Special Price Deal!

In cooperation with Amiga Future the Amiga Arena makes possible a special bonus for a subscription to Amiga Future! Besides the normal bonus everyone subscribing over the Amiga Arena you receive the Amiga Arena CD Rom Edition! This deal is only valid until the Jan. 31, 2001! More info in the Amiga Arena!

Amiga Arena - Interview Action!

"Rags" the action game of Shades Production did not without reason stir some little sensation on the Aminet! For anyone remembering "Beach Head II" on C64 the download is pure duty! But read for yourself the preview for "Rags" in the area "Aktionen" and the interview with Shades Production!

Amiga Arena - Special Price Deal!

The special price deal with SViewIV is online only until Jan. 15, 2001! The programs PhotoAlbum/Cybershow,GeoWorld and VPrefs are available at a special price!« only until Jan. 31, 2001

»Amiga Arena News
(January 20, 2001)


Amiga Arena - Full Version!

Weltendaemmerung (World Dawn), another strategic game by UAC Design is online! In the game Weltendaemmerung the player acts in the role of Robert Ungar. Here he must try to overcome the creatures of Dr. Coypu.

To carry out this task he must get control of an army of mutants ...

»Amiga Arena News
(January 23, 2001)


Amiga Arena - UAC Design Bonus!

"The Hunting Games" by Frank Abbing are ready for download!

Shoot down signposts (principle of "Moorhuhn")!

Shoot within one minute as many signs as possible, but of course not other hunters!

Turning Targets

Shoot against the target, until all target show in the same direction. The hit target always turns a quarter more than the other ones. Need for speed!

(Both games are little in-between games!)

Amiga Arena - UAC Design Updates!

Several updates are online for the games which Frank Abbing of UAC Design put at my disposal.

The quiz game Awaking AGA/ECS now is available in executable version.

(Directly playable from the HD, no longer in .adf format!) Risiko version 1.7 now is available in the freeware version.«
