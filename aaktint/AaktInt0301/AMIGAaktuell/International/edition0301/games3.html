Games section

3. News

'Land of Genesis' published

At Crystal Interactive Software the adventure 'Land of Genesis' has been published. The game is about a special agent who is sent to Mars by the government to erase the marks of a failed experiment. Gene-manipulated men have settled there.

'Tales of Tamar'-Updates

There are new beta versions of Tales of Tamar. Trade has been activated so the player can exchange goods. The price is calculated by supply and demand. There are some new sounds added, too.

Shortly after this update a second one was published which corrects a bug on the trade screen.

'Exodus'-Update

There is also an update for ´Exodus´ to version 1.1.

New games for free download

Former Amiga game developers Lankhor and Cinemaware have offered their games for free download. These software companies developed games like ´Vroom (Lankhor), ´Wings (Cinemaware) or the 4D-Sports series (Cinemaware).

'Payback' published

Payback is now available. A demoversion was published on the Aminet. This GTA-like game needs a 68020 with 16 MB RAM.
