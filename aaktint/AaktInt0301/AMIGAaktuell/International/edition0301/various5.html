Amiga Arena - AmigaKonto Full Version!

With permission of Andreas Regul the program AmigaKonto is ready for free download.

AmigaKonto was written to simplify the management of accounts.

Especially on drawing accounts with many movements you can quickly lose control when you have to check transfers which occur repeatedly.

Therefore AmigaKonto serves as a special kind of database, which manages the single transfer orders. With help of numerous selective and statistics functions out of the whole batch of account movements single items can be selected according to different criteria.

The full advantage is obtained when control data of several years are collected and are available for comparisons, which permits a quick survey of the data with help of selective functions.

Amiga Arena - Toadies survey; Addendum
