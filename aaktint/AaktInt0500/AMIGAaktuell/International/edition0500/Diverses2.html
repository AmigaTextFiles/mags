2. Amiga job board open

Nico Barbat informs:

»PRESS RELEASE

"Free Amiga Jobs" online - http://www.amiga-news.de/jobs/

Cologne/Germany, April 14, 2000 - The strong lack of qualified personal in the computer branch is also noticed in the Amiga sector. Creative minds, mostly programmers, designers and text journalists are searched from various small and medium companies and private developers.

The newsroom of the (German magazine) Amiga plus is delighted, to offer a free job board in the Internet in cooperation with the team of Amiga-News. With "Free Amiga Jobs" we want to establish contacts between employers and employees, to speed up the software and hardware development for the Amiga. The job board can be used without any costs from employers and employees.

"Free Amiga Jobs" can be found in the Internet on this URL "http://www.amiga-news.de/jobs/".«
