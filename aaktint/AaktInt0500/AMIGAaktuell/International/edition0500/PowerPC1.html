Amiga PowerPC

1. News in Brief

  • The announced PowerPC version of the astronomy program Digital Almanac (revision III) will be released early summer. More informations can be found on http://www.rzuser.uni-heidelberg.de/~astegema.

  • On ftp://ftp.meanmachine.ch/pub/amiga/ppclib/ you can find a new revision of the ppc.library as well as flush updates for the PowerPC boards of Phase5.

  • On http://www.vgr.com/v5/ you find first informations about revision 5 of the graphics board software CyberGraphX, which is to run under MorphOS.

  • Andreas Kleinert announced, that he wants to release his AK-datatypes also for MorphOS.
