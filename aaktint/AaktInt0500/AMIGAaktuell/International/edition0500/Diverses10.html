Various Amiga news

10. News in Brief

  • Cloanto put the full version of its programs ColorType, PFM and PersonalPaint 7.1b(!) into the Aminet (directory biz/cloan).

  • AmyGate is an Internet client, which allows not just up- and downloads, but also supports the program evaluation etc. Downloading is possible from http://members.xoom.com/AmosFactory/.

  • For the Workbench replacement Scalos exists a developer package, which can be downloaded from http://www.satanicdreams.com/products/archives/ScalosDev.lha.

  • On http://www.vgr.com/v4/ the PreRelease of the graphics board software CyberGraphX 4.2 has been made available.

  • Ruediger Engel made an Aminet mirror with his A1200/030(!), which is available on http://aminet.a500.de.

  • The former Aminet mainserver of the University of Paderborn (Germany) is back online after being offline for several weeks.
