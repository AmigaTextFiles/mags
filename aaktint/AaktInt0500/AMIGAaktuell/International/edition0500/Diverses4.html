Since about three weeks the Amiga Web directory "Mitchy" of amiga.org is online again. It currently has about 1.030 entries and it is now up to you to update or extend it - visit Mitchy It now features a new rating program where you can rate a site, e.g. this one here ;-)

20 Apr 2000: Fusion 3.2 Amiga now available

Finally the 3.2 update is available in Germany, Austria and Swiss exclusively at H&P for 30 DM.

21 Apr 2000: MiniGL and new 3D games

Hyperion released MiniGL and the Heretic 2 demo. Besides two new Warp3D games were announced: Drake and Black Sun / War Machine: World

25 Apr 2000: AmigaWriter 2.0 availabe in May

The long awaited version will be available soon. The new version got a lot of powerful features - highlights are: Word import, TrueType support with Antialiasing and brochure print.

25 Apr 2000: ArtEffect 4.0 comes in May

The 4th version of the popular graphics package for image processing and natural media paint comes with many new features and improvements.

Especially the functionallity of the layers has been extended and improved. The version comes on a CDROM full of fonts, cliparts and workshops.«
