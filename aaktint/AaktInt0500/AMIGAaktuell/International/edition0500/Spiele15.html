15. Emulator-News

Amiga Sector One (http://www.emucamp.com/amiga/main-dir/index.shtml) has many Amiga games, partly original software for free, legal download for Amiga emulators. Of course, it is not forbidden to use it on 'real' Amigas. So, if you miss one or the other classic for your collection, take a look.

Mathias "AmiDog" Roslung updated some of his emulators, as he does nearly any month. So, there are new revisions of TrueReality and SOPE, this month. You can download them on http://www.amidog.com/ or on ftp://ftp.amidog.com/.
