8. News from the Amiga Arena

Amiga Arena (http://online-club.de/~ARENA/) informs:

»Amiga Arena News (April 08, 2000)

MetaView for a special price

From now on, the viewer and displayer MetaView is available for a special price of 20 DEM (approx. 10.20 Euros) at the Amiga Arena! erhaeltlich!

Program informations:

A program for displaying and converting of

  • WMF files (also embedded into Windows(tm) CLP files)

  • AMF files

  • DXF files

  • DR2D files

  • XFIG files (revision 3.0 oder 3.1)

  • WPG files (revision 1.0 und 2.0)

  • GEM files

  • DSDR files

  • CGM files

  • HPGL files

  • CMX files

to 
 
  • ILBM

  • AMF

  • CGM

  • GEM

  • DR2D

  • HPGL

  • EPS

  • AI

  • JMF

  • WMF

  • FIG

The support of the CMX format replaces the very uncomplete support of the CDR format. The preview picture of CMX files, if present, is saved as 't:preview.bmp', as it was already with CDR.
