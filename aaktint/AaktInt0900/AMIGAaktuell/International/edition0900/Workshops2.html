2. All around the internet: The best websites (by Numen Stahl)

This time the hompage presentation deals with the topic PowerPC. I picked three pages and rated them depending on look, arrangement and contents.

Warpgate

Webmaster Andreas Wendorf is responsible for this site. It's dedicated to the topic "WarpOS/3D" (and his site additionally covers the topic "music" but that's not important at the moment) and there are regular and quite detailed news. PPC users might be especially interested in the software list containing lots of games, applications and demos which require or support a PPC. Furthermore, there's a help forum which adds some points to my rating. Well, it's a pity that the design with its never ending blue is a little bit boring. Anyway: a good website.


URL: http://www.w-9.de/ 
language: English 
  
look: C - D+ 
arrangements: B - C 
contents: B 
  
overall: B - C 
  

PowerPC World powered by Haage&Partner

On the Haage&Partner hompage you can find a sub page which exclusively deals with the topic PPC. Unfortunately, the news on this site are outdated and cover the most important things only. You'll be more interested in the PPC software list. In addition, you can gather detailed information on PPC hardware in general - a service that Warpgate doesn't offer. Instead of a forum you can join a mailinglist. For gamblers Haage&Partner created the &quot;3D World&quot; dealing with games.

It's a useful website with a clearly arranged and good-looking design. Unfortunately, the news part is very disappointing.


URL: http://www.haage-partner.com/ppcworld/ 
language: German and English 
  
look: B 
arrangement: B 
contents: C - D+ 
  
overall: C 
  

Warp PPC Station

The main emphasis of this site can be explained easily: news! Everybody can send PPC news directly to the webmaster making this page similar to amiga-news.de. The only difference is that PPC Station is limited to PPC news. The updates are regular enough showing the poor news part of Haage&Partner's website the back lights. A great site.

look: C 
arrangement: B 
contents: A 
  
overall: B
