4. Games news from all over the world

  • Crystal Software cancels all Classic Amiga games

It seems, that this news on the home page from Crystal Software was not noticed by many people due to the silly season. In detail this means, that more than 10 announced games have been canceled. Just have a look into the release list and you will see what I am talking about. A small back door was left open by Crystal Software: They still want to support the new Amiga.

  • "Codename: Hell Squad" released

This month, Digital Dreams Entertainment (DDE) released the final version of "Codename: Hell Squad". The real time adventure was originally begun by Vulcan Software and was completed by Digital Dreams Entertainment. It is very likely, that you can find a review in the next edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

  • "Sin" from Hyperion Software

The port of Sin, a new 3D shooter, well known from the PC, is moving forward. But the priority lies on the Linux version of Sin. The Amiga version is expected to be released in the 4th quarter of 2000. So, it is not far away.

  • "Joyride" released

This month, Titan Computer released the final version of the 3D car racing game "Joyride". Recently, the game was introduced in the games section of Amiga aktuell International. I hope, I can present a detailed review, next month.

  • "Operation Counterstrike"
For a long time there is something new to tell about "Operation Counterstrike". According to the developers of Blue Black Solution they are still working on the game and soon a not-playable demo version will be released. So the final release might be in the distant future.
  • "Nightlong" to come in October

According to clickBOOM "Nightlong" is already being pressed and will be shipped as soon as poosible, latest date is October. A detailed review can be found in the next edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

  • "Desert Racing"

The game "Desert Racing", which was introduced in the game section of AMIGA aktuell International quite a while ago, now found a publisher: The release of the game is done by APC&TCP. But it will take a while, because there is a complete refit of the game.

  • "Gilbert Goodmate"

There is also some news about the legendary adventure "Gilbert Goodmate and the Mushroom Phungoria". Soon, the PC-version will be released an an Amiga version will only follow, if Dark Millenia sells good.

  • "Pictris" released

This month, the APC&TCP computer club relased their new game Pictris. A detailed review can be found in this edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

  • CD³² Outside Nr. 2 released

The second and last edition of the recently introduced CD³² online magazin CD³² Outside was released this month. The editor, the CD³² Alliance recently resigned and released this edition just as a matter of form.
