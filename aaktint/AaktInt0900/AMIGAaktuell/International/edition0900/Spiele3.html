3. Foreword

Hello, dear readers!

In front of you is the September edition of the AMIGA aktuell International game section. After this "nice" summer here in Germany we return to the real life. This also influences the games section - this month there has been more to report than the month before. Very interesting is the news from Crystal Software to cancel all Classic-Amiga games. Just have a look into the release list - there you can see how many games have been cancelled...

This month there are reviews and to new introductions: On one hand Pictris and on the other hand the Amiga Game Guide CD-ROM (remark of the translator: the Amiga Game Guide CD-ROM is available in German only. So it is not included in AMIGA aktuell International.). Both products are from APC&TCP. In addition to that there the PD tests from Malte Mende. But my small job for the PD section is cancelled due to technical reasons. But usually you get informed about the latest Aminet releases earlier than at the end of the month in this games section.

Of course, we are still looking for someone taking over the Mac corner, since Andreas Koch cannot continue with it. Please contact me! I am also always open for the creation of new section. But please consider, that they should not appear only once, but on a regular base. Those, who just want to write an article, can do this, of course. Just send me an eMail.

Have fun with this AMIGA aktuell International games section and it would be nice, if you sent some feedback!
