9. Emulator news

This month, the author of the ports of WarpMAME, WarpNeoMAME and WarpMESS released new versions of his programs. The latest versions are based on the latest sources and support audio via Paula, support AGA and many more like e.g. speed improvements due to less context switches. The new versions can be downloaded from http://www.powerup.com.au/~cward/.

Another version of the Playstation emulator FPSE has been released. The compatibility and speed are not satisfying. But the emulator is still in an early stage.
