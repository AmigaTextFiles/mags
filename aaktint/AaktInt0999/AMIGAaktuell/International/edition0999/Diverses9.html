Various Amiga news

9. News in brief

  • The long awaited new revision of the WWW browser IBrowse v2.1 will be released according to HiSOFT within the next days: These improvements and new features have been announced:

    »Remember, there's lots of new stuff in iBrowse 2: full JavaScript 1.2 implementation, HTML 4 support, improved GUI, improved preferences, the cache system has been rewritten (it's now even faster and even easier to use), the table parser has been rewritten (it's now lots faster), spoofing has been implemented, there's external news client support, an improved URL completer, multiple browsers can be opened in one window (controlled by a nifty tab system) plus, of course, plug-in support. And those are just the highlights!«
