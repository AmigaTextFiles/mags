5. Prices for Wildfire7\PPC and Wildfire for Movieshop reduced

»For immediate release!

August 29, 1999

Wildfire7\PPC and Wildfire fuer Movieshop price reduction

From now on the recommended sales price of the rewarded special effect program Wildfire revision 7\PPC has been reduced from 279 DEM (approx. 142.35 Euros) to 249 DEM (approx. 127.04 Euros).

In addition there is the 'Wildfire for Movieshop' example movie, which will be released in September. Within this video any special effects, which are offered for the Wildfire-Movieshop combination are shown. The video runs approx. 100 minutes. Now, the price has been reduced from 40 DEM (approx. 20.41 Euros) to 35 DEM (approx. 17.86 Euros).
