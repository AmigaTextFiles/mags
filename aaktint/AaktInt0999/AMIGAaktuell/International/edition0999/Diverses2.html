2. ZorroIV-daughter board available

According to TGV-Haupt the ZorroIV daughter board, which has been announced for a long time, is now available. The ZorroIV is a technically improved ZorroII slot. These are more informations:

»The long waiting is over
The Z4 daughter board is available


Z4-daughter board for Mikronik, Elbox, Winner, Power -Tower 
Features: 
- 5 Zorro2-Slots 
- 2 Z4-LocalSlots (double bus band width (up to 19MB/sec) like Zorro3) 
- 2 Zorro2-Slots switchable to Fast-Z2 (double speed for 
 special adjusted boards, 
 in approx. 3-4 Wochen available: Buddha-FastZ2 (IDE-Contr.), 
 CV64-3D-FastZ2 
- Videoslot (for activating video adaptor required, DEM79.- (approx. 40.31 Euros)) 
- 4 clock port slots 
- compatible with any common turbo boards with memory in the upper address range 
- P8/P9 Power supply connector for standard-AT power supply 
  
  • Price DEM 299.- (approx. 152.55 Euros) (until September 10, 999 special price of 249 DEM (approx. 127.04 Euros))

24-graphics board for 24-LocalSlot Features:

  • Grafikchip Cirrus Logic CL5434GD

  • max. 1280*1024 pixeles

  • TrueColor (24Bit) modes

  • 4MB RAM

  • automatic switching between Amiga & graphics board

  • fast drawing due to high speed Z4 interface

  • Price DEM 299.- (approx. 152.55 Euros)

Z4-FastIDE-Controller

  • Data transfer up to 16.6MB/sec
