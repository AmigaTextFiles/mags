Preface 2: Another bad news

Welcome to this edition of Amiga aktuell international. As you expected, it is full with interesting informations about the Amiga. Unfortunately (at least from my point of view) Amiga aktuell international continously grows in size. Normally this would be fine, but unfortunately my time is much more limited than it was before. You might have noticed that, because Amiga aktuell international was released in the end of the month instead of the mid. Because I am unable to translate the edition of Amiga aktuell international on time and I got just one person, who wants to help me (as I asked for help in Amiga aktuell) this will be the last edition of Amiga aktuell international. Carsten Schroeder (aakt@gmx.de) is trying to find some other people, who would like to translate Amiga aktuell into the English language, but in the meantime there will be no more Amiga aktuell international. However, the AaktInt Specials will keep you informed.

Anyway, have fun with this edition,

yours

Martin Henke
