Software eupdates

Silly season or waiting for the new Amiga? The number of new releases and updates was very clearly again, this month. Andreas Kleinert publishes each month new revisions of his Datatypes and, which is fine to see, thanks to Voyager, something is done on the Amiga browser market.

It is surprising, that there is still a need for Anti virus software. Praise to the authors, but the ages of the great virus problems due to pirate copying and exchanging via mailboxes seems to be over - at least for the Amiga.

Let's hope, that most of the clever shareware authors bring a large software range to the new computers from Amiga - if they are able to compete with others - so the system would not die due to a lack of software.
