7. Movieplayer MooVID available on CD-ROM from Epic Marketing

»August 18, 1999

Now, the full version of the movie player MooVID is available on CD-ROM from Epic Marketing.

MooVID Features:

  • Coded 100% in Assembler

  • Supports AVI and MOV files

  • Supports the INTEL INDEO 3.1 & 3.2 format

  • Nice VCR like GUI

  • Extremely quick playing

  • Fast c2p algorythms for smooth ECS/AGA playback

  • ECS support (16 grayscales), AGA (256 colours, HAM8, 18 bit)

  • Support of CyberGFX and Picasso96

  • System friendly

MooVID Gold can be purchased from Epic Marketing for DEM 49.- (plus post and package) (Telephone: +49 (0)7071 400492, Fax: +49(0)7071 400493, eMail:order@epic-marketing.de). On the CD there are in addition to the MooVID program dozens of movie clips in various formats.
