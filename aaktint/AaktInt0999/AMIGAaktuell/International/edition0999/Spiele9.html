9. Preview: The Last Seal

Since mid of this month there is a playable preview of the adventure The Last Seal for download on the homepage of ANCHOR (http://www.anchor.ch). In the demo you have to try to get out of the prison, which is not very difficult. After the intro you find yourself there and can walk around in the room, that is shown in various perspectives. The background pictures have been made with the raytracer and look very good, of course. Unfortunately there are many bugs in the demo version, which I hope will be removed. However, the first impression is fine, especially the graphics. The only thing, that is a bit nerve consuming is the sound of the walking hero.
