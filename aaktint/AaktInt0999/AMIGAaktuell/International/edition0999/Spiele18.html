18. Summons

At last there is the obligatory request to all of them, who run a Mac emulation and own one or the other game: if you have the time send me a test, comment or just an information about the speed the game runs. For this purpose you find a clear form in this edition of the Mac corner, which you might use as a little help, but you don't have to. However, I am happy about any kind of ideas or criticism.

If you ask yourself, why I mostly test games, which are no longer available from the mail order dealers, this is due to the sad reason, that I just have a limited number of games and those are mostly of older nature.

To come up to the software corner I will try to solve this problem in the near future.

However, of course I would be much more delighted, if I got one or the other test report from you...
