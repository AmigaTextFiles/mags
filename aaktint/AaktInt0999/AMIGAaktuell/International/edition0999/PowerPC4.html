4. FusionPPC not yet finally save

Unfortunately it occured, what might bring the project FusionPPC to fail: Until some days ago just 380 of meanwhile nearly 700 bookings payed in advance for it. Microcode Solution, the manufacturer, stated, that the PowerPC version will only be released, if at least 500 bookings will be paid. Meanwhile the handling of the bookings has been transferred to the British distributor Blittersoft. If you are interested, find the desired form on http://secure100.local.net/cgi-bin/2/webc.cgi/~bsoft/st_prod.html?p_prodid=392&sid=4veD2LOC95913JT.

By the way, in opposition to previous plans the cheap introductionary price of 149.95 US dollars resp. 96.75 British Pounds plus post and package is still available. The payment is made by credit cards.
