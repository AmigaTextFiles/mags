Games section

3. Games news from all over the world

  • New games at AmigaLand.de

AmigaLand has managed it to get licences for free download of 17 Amiga games. For example, Xtreme Racing (+ Data Disk) or Embryo.

  • New update and new demo of Payback

There is a second update and a new demoversion for the GTA clone Payback due to reports about several bugs. Besides there is a Extra map part on the homepage of Payback.

  • X-Cellence looks for co-worker

For their AmigaDE games SCR2K and NYN the boys of X-Cellence need Amiga programmers. Anyone who is interested should visit the website: www.x-cellence.de

  • New games at Back2Roots.org

The boys of Back To The Roots have new licences, too, for example Fire&Ice, Gloom Deluxe and Uridium 2.

  • Source codes of Crossfire II and Between the Lines are lost.

Due to a disk crash the source codes of Crossfire II and Between the Lines are lost. The team were able to recover Crossfire II, but it is the state it had before last Christmas. As solace some screenshots of Between the Lines were published: www.dreamworlds.de

  • New Ermentrud Demo

A new demo version of the graphics adventure Ermentrud has been published. This third demo has a system of inventory and dialog.
