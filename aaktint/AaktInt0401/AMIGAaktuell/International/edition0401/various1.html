1. News from Haage & Partner

Haage & Partner (http://www.haage-partner.com) informs:

»06. April 2001:
PageStream 4.0.9 for Amiga


PageStream4.0.9 for Amiga and Macintosh is now available. Improvements include PDF compression, more language modules and language dictionaries, faster and more robust spell checking and hyphenation, improved font handling, printing fixes, and better window handling.

06. April 2001:
H&P at Gateway Computer Show 2001


As last year, H&P attended the Gateway Computer Show Amiga 2001 in St. Louis, USA (30. March to 1. April). We showed the latest version of the StormC 4 compiler system and held seminars on StormC and on AmigaOS 3.9.«
