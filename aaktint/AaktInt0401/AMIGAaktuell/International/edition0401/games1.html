1. Foreword

Hello dear friends of computer games!

It is the white end of winter, the children are playing in the snow, inside the computer is running war. And what are you doing with the computer? Playing, of course. Well, and writing forewords.

I hope you all used the cold season for playing a lot of games, ´cause the summer will come, it´ll be warmer, the nights get longer, the Sundays shorter. There will be no time for playing. And when your're playing in the summer you´re called a stay-at-home by your mom, girlfriend or boyfriend. Or your wife or husband.

But today there is still a lot of snow and so you can play all the new games without remorse.

Mac&Jürgen
