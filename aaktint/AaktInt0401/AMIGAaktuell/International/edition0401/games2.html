Dear Reader,

The AMIGA aktuell international Games section needs your help, this is why I ask you to inform me, if you have news about games. I am always interested in news, tips or whatever, if it has something to do with Amiga and games. Critics is also welcome, please tell me, if there's something you don't like. Because I am not perfect, either. So - just give me your ideas.
