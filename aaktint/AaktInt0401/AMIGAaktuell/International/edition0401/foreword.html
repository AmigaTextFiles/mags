+++ After the show in St. Louis uncertainties prevail about a possible change of course with AmigaDE +++ AMIGA cooperates with Sharp +++ AMIGA announces PowerPC native version of the AmigaOS +++

With this news digest, welcome to the latest edition of AMIGA aktuell International.

Most users who made public statements have hailed the decision of AMIGA to develop a PowerPC version of the AmigaOS. Against them, the chief editor of this magazine cannot participate fully in the jubilation, especially because information published recently by staff members of AMIGA does not clearly disclose if the planning for AmigaDE will or will not proceed as it was earlier understood. More about this in the final words of this edition.
